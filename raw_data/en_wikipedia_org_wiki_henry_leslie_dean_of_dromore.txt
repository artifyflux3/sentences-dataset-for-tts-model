(1651-1733) was an Anglican priest in Ireland in the late seventeenth and early 18th centuries.

[ 1 ] Mathews was born in County Down on 4 November 1651 [ 2 ] and educated at Trinity College, Dublin .

[ 3 ] He was Archdeacon of Down from 1695 until his death [ 4 ] on 3 September 1733; [ 5 ] and Dean of Dromore from February 5, 1722 to February 16, 1722, ostensibly to test a clause in the right of presentation.
