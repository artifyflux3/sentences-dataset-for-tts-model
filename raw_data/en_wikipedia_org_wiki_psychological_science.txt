Indexing CODEN ( alt · alt2 ) · JSTOR ( alt ) · LCCN ( alt ) MIAR · NLM ( alt ) · Scopus · W&L ISSN 0956-7976 (print) 1467-9280 (web) Links Journal homepage Psychological Science , the flagship journal of the Association for Psychological Science , is a monthly, peer-reviewed scientific journal published by SAGE Publications . The journal publishes research articles, short reports, and research reports covering all aspects of psychology .

[ 1 ] Its editor-in-chief is Simine Vazire ( University of Melbourne ).
