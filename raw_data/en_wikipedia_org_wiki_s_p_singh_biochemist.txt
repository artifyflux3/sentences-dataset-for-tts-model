( September 2017 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) Dr. S.P. Singh Born ( 1948-02-01 ) 1 February 1948 (age 77) Agra , United Provinces , India Nationality Indian Occupation Professor S. P. Singh (born 1 February 1948), is a professor of biochemistry .

Early life and education [ edit ] Singh received his Master's degree from Lucknow University , Uttar Pradesh, India. and his PhD in Medical Biochemistry from Meerut University , Uttar Pradesh, India. He took his early education from a primary village school at Tundla , Agra later on he took his education up to 12th standard from Northern Railways Intermediate college where is father was posted in railways as station master.

Career [ edit ] Singh has written nine biochemistry books, one of which ( Manual of Practical Biochemistry ) has been translated into Persian and one of which has been written in Hindi at the request of HRD Ministry.

In 1973 he founded the Department of Biochemistry at Baba Raghav Das Medical College , Gorakhpur .

[ 1 ] He has taught for 40 years and since 1984 he is Head of the Department of Biochemistry at Maharani Laxmi Bai Medical College , Jhansi , Uttar Pradesh . He has so far published 65 research publications in various national and international journals.

He is Vice President of the Uttar Pradesh Chapter of Association of Clinical Biochemists of India.

[ 2 ] and serves on the editorial board of the Journal of Advance Researches in Biological Sciences.
