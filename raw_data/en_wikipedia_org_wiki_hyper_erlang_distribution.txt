A hyper-Erlang distributed random variable X has a probability density function given by A ( x ) = ∑ ∑ i = 1 n p i E l i ( x ) {\displaystyle A(x)=\sum _{i=1}^{n}p_{i}E_{l_{i}}(x)} where each p i > 0 with the p i summing to 1 and each of the E l i being an Erlang distribution with l i stages each of which has parameter λ i .
