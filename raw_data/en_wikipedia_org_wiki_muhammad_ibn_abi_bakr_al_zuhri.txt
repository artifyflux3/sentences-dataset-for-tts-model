1130s–1150s) was a geographer . He was the writer of a notable work, Kitāb al-Jughrāfiyya (Book of Geography). Al-Zuhri was able to use the writings of the geographers of the reign of caliph al-Ma'mun of Baghdad (d. 456/1068).

[ 1 ] He belonged to the Arab tribe of Banu Zuhrah .

[ 2 ] Al-Zuhri died between 1154 and 1161.
