The metric system was adopted in 1876, and has been compulsory in Mauritius since 1878.

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] System before metric system [ edit ] Systems including old French and British were used.

Capacity [ edit ] In addition to old French and British units, the following were used: 1 cash = 227.11 litre 1 velt = 1 ⁄ 30 cash.
