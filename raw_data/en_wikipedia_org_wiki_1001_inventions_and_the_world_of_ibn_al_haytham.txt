It is Sharif's final film.

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] The film was produced by 1001 Inventions , a British foundation aiming to promote the achievements of the Golden Age of Islam . 
Both the film and the exhibition were created to coincide with the United Nations campaign celebrating the International Year of Light , operated by UNESCO .

[ 3 ] Within the film, Sharif's character helps his granddaughter with a challenging homework assignment about Ibn Al-Haytham , the eleventh century scholar who made significant contributions to the principles of optics and visual perception.
