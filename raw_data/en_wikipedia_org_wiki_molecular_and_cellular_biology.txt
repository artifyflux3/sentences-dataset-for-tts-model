6492263 Links Journal homepage Online access Online archives Molecular and Cellular Biology is a biweekly peer-reviewed scientific journal covering all aspects of molecular and cellular biology .

[ 1 ] It is published by the American Society for Microbiology and the editor-in-chief is Peter Tontonoz ( University of California, Los Angeles ). It was established in 1981.

[ 2 ] The h-index (1981-2021) is 338.

Abstracting and indexing [ edit ] The journal is abstracted and indexed in: AGRICOLA BIOSIS Previews [ 3 ] Current Contents /Life Sciences [ 3 ] Embase [ 4 ] Index Medicus / MEDLINE / PubMed [ 5 ] Science Citation Index [ 3 ] Scopus [ 6 ] According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2021 impact factor of 5.069.
