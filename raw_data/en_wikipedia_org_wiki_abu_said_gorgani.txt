He wrote a treatise on geometrical problems and another on the drawing of the meridian .

George Sarton considers him a pupil of Ibn al-A'rabi , but Carl Brockelmann rejects this opinion.

Works [ edit ] Two of his works are extant: Masa'il Hindisia (a manuscript is available in Cairo ) Istikhraj khat nisf al-nahar min kitab analima wa al-borhan alayh (available in Cairo, translated by Carl Schoy) See also [ edit ] List of Iranian scientists Sources [ edit ] ^ Dianat, Abu'l-Hasan.
