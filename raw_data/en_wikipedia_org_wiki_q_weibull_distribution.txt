Relationship to other distributions [ edit ] The q -Weibull is equivalent to the Weibull distribution when q = 1 and equivalent to the q -exponential when κ κ = 1 {\displaystyle \kappa =1} The q -Weibull is a generalization of the Weibull, as it extends this distribution to the cases of finite support ( q < 1) and to include heavy-tailed distributions ( q ≥ ≥ 1 + κ κ κ κ + 1 ) {\displaystyle (q\geq 1+{\frac {\kappa }{\kappa +1}})} .

The q -Weibull is a generalization of the Lomax distribution (Pareto Type II), as it extends this distribution to the cases of finite support and adds the κ κ {\displaystyle \kappa } parameter.
