1039259630 Links Journal homepage Online access Online archive PLOS Biology is a monthly peer-reviewed scientific journal covering all aspects of biology . Publication began on October 13, 2003. It is the first journal published by the Public Library of Science . The editor-in-chief is Nonia Pariente.

[ 1 ] In addition to research articles, the journal publishes magazine content aimed to be accessible to a broad audience. Article types in this section are essays, "unsolved mysteries", editorials, and synopses.

[ 2 ] [ 3 ] Abstracting and indexing [ edit ] The journal is abstracted and indexed in: Biological Abstracts [ 4 ] BIOSIS Previews [ 4 ] Current Contents /Agriculture, Biology & Environmental Sciences [ 4 ] Current Contents /Life Sciences [ 4 ] Chemical Abstracts Service [ 5 ] Embase [ 6 ] Index Medicus / MEDLINE / PubMed [ 7 ] Science Citation Index [ 4 ] Scopus [ 8 ] The Zoological Record [ 4 ] According to Journal Citation Reports , the journal had a 2023 impact factor of 7.8.

[ 9 ] The journal does not list this impact factor on its website.

[ 10 ] Instead, the journal promotes the use of article level metrics to provide a measure of the impact of their published articles.
