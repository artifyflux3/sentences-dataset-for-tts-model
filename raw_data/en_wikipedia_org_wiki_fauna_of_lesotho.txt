( May 2022 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) Drakensberg siskin The wildlife of Lesotho is composed of its flora and fauna .
Lesotho has 60 species of mammals and 339 species of birds.

Fauna [ edit ] Mammals [ edit ] Main article: List of mammals of Lesotho African leopard South African cheetah Birds [ edit ] Main article: List of birds of Lesotho Reptiles [ edit ] Main article: List of reptiles of Lesotho Flora [ edit ] Grass is the natural vegetation in Lesotho. The high plateau is covered with montane or subalpine grassland. Red oat grass forms a dry carpet in much of the Drakensberg foothill region.
