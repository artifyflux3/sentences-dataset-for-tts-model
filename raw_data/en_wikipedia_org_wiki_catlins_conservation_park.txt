[ 1 ] The park includes the McLean Falls , [ 2 ] and a range of tramping tracks.

[ 3 ] History [ edit ] The park was established in 1975.

[ 1 ] It was included in the 1080 pest control programme in 2014.

[ 4 ] The body of missing Dunedin man Stephen Lowe was found in the park in September 2017 by a librarian and her dog.

[ 5 ] [ 6 ] In January 2019, local MP Sarah Dowie proposed converting the park into a national park .
