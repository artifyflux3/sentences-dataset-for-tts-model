↓ Type Geological group Overlies Fig Tree Formation Lithology Primary Greenstone belt Other Sedimentary Rock Location Coordinates 25°48′S 31°00′E ﻿ / ﻿ 25.8°S 31.0°E ﻿ / -25.8; 31.0 Region Barberton Greenstone Belt , Mpumalanga Country South Africa , Eswatini Extent Kaapvaal craton Location of the Barberton Greenstone Belt The Moodies Group is a geological formation in South Africa and Eswatini .

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] It has the oldest well-preserved siliciclastic tidal deposits on Earth, where microbial mats flourished.
