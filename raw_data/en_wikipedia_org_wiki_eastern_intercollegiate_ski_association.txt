Find sources: "Eastern Intercollegiate Ski Association" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( February 2022 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) The Eastern Intercollegiate Ski Association ( EISA ) is an NCAA skiing -only conference.  As the NCAA does not have divisions in skiing, it is composed of NCAA Division I , Division II , and Division III schools.
