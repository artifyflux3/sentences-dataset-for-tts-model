The Northern Miner began publication in Cobalt , Ontario, Canada, in 1915, and has since moved publication to Toronto .

[ 3 ] [ 4 ] The weekly journal is considered to be the "leading authority on the mining industry in Canada", [ 2 ] and " the mining industry's bible".
