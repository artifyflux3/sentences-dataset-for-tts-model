The term hunting dog is broad and includes all breeds and skills of hunting canines, but "gun dogs" refers to canines that are trained to work alongside a loud firearm while hunting or retrieving game.

[ 2 ] Gun dogs are divided into three main categories: pointers and setters, retrievers, and spaniels.

[ 3 ] [ failed verification ] Types [ edit ] There are several breeds used as gundogs with varying instinctive skill sets for each one.

[ 4 ] Gun dogs are divided into three primary types: retrievers , pointing breeds , and water dogs .
