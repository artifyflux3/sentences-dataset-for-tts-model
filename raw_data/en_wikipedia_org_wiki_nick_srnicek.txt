He is currently a lecturer in Digital Economy in the Department of Digital Humanities, King's College London .

[ 4 ] Srnicek is associated with the political theory of accelerationism and a post-scarcity economy .

Biography [ edit ] Srnicek took a double major in Psychology and philosophy [ 5 ] before completing an MA at the University of Western Ontario in 2007.

[ 6 ] He proceeded to a PhD at the London School of Economics , completing his thesis in 2013 on "Representing complexity: the material construction of world politics".

[ 7 ] He has worked as a Visiting Lecturer at City University and the University of Westminster .
