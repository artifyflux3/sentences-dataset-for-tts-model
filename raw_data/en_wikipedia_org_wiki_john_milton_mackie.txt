Biography [ edit ] He graduated from Brown University in 1832, and studied at the University of Berlin , Germany, 1833–1834. On his return to the United States, he was tutor at Brown 1835–1838. He contributed articles on German topics to the North American Review , American Whig Review , and Christian .
