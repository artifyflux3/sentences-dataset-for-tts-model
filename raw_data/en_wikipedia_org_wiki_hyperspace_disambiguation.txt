From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

[![Image 5](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/99/Wiktionary-logo-en-v2.svg/40px-Wiktionary-logo-en-v2.svg.png)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Wiktionary-logo-en-v2.svg)

Look up _**[hyperspace](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/hyperspace "wiktionary:hyperspace")**_ in Wiktionary, the free dictionary.

**[Hyperspace](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hyperspace "Hyperspace")** is a faster-than-light method of traveling used in science fiction.

**Hyperspace** or **HyperSpace** may also refer to:

Mathematics
-----------

[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Hyperspace_(disambiguation)&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Mathematics")]

*   [Hypertopology](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hypertopology "Hypertopology"), a topological space within which some of its elements form another topological space
*   [Higher dimensions](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Higher_dimensions "Higher dimensions"), including Kaluza–Klein's 4-dimensional space and Superstring theory's 9-dimensional space and Supergravity/M-theory's 10-dimensional space
*   [_n_-dimensional space](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/N-dimensional_space "N-dimensional space"), the original meaning of the word _hyperspace_, common in late nineteenth century British books
*   [Non-Euclidean space](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Non-Euclidean_space "Non-Euclidean space")

Media
-----

[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Hyperspace_(disambiguation)&action=edit&section=2 "Edit section: Media")]

### Books

[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Hyperspace_(disambiguation)&action=edit&section=3 "Edit section: Books")]

*   [_Hyperspace_ (book)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hyperspace_(book) "Hyperspace (book)"), a 1994 book by Michio Kaku that attempts to explain the possibility of 10-dimensional space using string theory
*   [_Hyperspace_ (gamebook)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hyperspace_(gamebook) "Hyperspace (gamebook)"), a book in the _Choose Your Own Adventure_ series

### Film and television

[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Hyperspace_(disambiguation)&action=edit&section=4 "Edit section: Film and television")]

*   [_Hyperspace_ (film)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hyperspace_(film) "Hyperspace (film)"), a 1984 3D science fiction comedy film
*   _Hyperspace_, the U.S. title of _[Space](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space\_(2001\_TV\_series) "Space (2001 TV series)")_, a 2001 BBC documentary

### Music

[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Hyperspace_(disambiguation)&action=edit&section=5 "Edit section: Music")]

*   _Hyperspace,_ the opening song on the [Nada Surf](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nada_Surf "Nada Surf") album _[The Proximity Effect](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The\_Proximity\_Effect\_(Nada\_Surf\_album) "The Proximity Effect (Nada Surf album)")_
*   _[Hyperspace](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hyperspace\_(album) "Hyperspace (album)"),_ 2019 studio album by American producer and musician [Beck](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Beck "Beck")
*   _Hyperspace,_ a song by Buckner & Garcia from the album _[Pac-Man Fever](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pac-Man\_Fever\_(album) "Pac-Man Fever (album)")_
