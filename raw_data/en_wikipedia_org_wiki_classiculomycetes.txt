(2006) [ 2 ] Order: Classiculales R.Bauer, Begerow, Oberw. & Marvanová (2003) [ 1 ] Family: Classiculaceae R.Bauer, Begerow, Oberw. & Marvanová (2003) [ 1 ] Genera Classicula Jaculispora The Classiculomycetes are a class of fungi in the Pucciniomycotina subdivision of the Basidiomycota . The class contains a single order , the Classiculales , which in turn contains the single family Classiculaceae . The family contains two monotypic genera.

[ 3 ] They belong to a morpholigical group called " aquatic hyphomycetes " which are fungi that grow on submerged decaying plant matter, but they are also found as mycoparasites and endophytes .

[ 4 ] This is only an informal descriptive group which is divided phylogenetically between asco types (the great majority) and basidio types (a small percentage of hyphomycetes).

[ 4 ] The Classiculomycetes belong to the basidiomycete minority.
