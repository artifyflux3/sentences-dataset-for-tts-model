The family produced three popes of the Catholic Church : Callixtus III (born Alfons de Borja; 1378–1458) –  served as pope from 8 April 1455 until his death on 6 August 1458 [ 1 ] Alexander VI (born Rodrigo Lanzol Borgia; 1431–1503) –  served as pope from 11 August 1492 until his death on 18 August 1503; his maternal uncle was Callixtus III [ 2 ] Innocent X [ 3 ] (born Giovanni Battista Pamphilj (or Pamphili); 1574–1655) –  served as pope from 15 September 1644 until his death on 7 January 1655; he was the great-great-great-grandson of Alexander VI, but his surname was not Borgia See also [ edit ] Biography portal Catholicism portal Lists portal Spain portal Vatican City portal List of popes Route of the Borgias References [ edit ] ^ Strathern, Paul (2019).
