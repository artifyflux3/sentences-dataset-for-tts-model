He is known for his expertise on Plato and Aristotle .

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] [ 3 ] Politis is a Fellow of the Wissenschaftskolleg zu Berlin (2009–10) and Trinity College Dublin (2005) and director of the Dublin Centre for the Study of the Platonic Tradition.
