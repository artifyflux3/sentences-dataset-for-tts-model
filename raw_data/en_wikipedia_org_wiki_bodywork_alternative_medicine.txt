hypersensitivity Heavy legs Leaky gut syndrome Multiple chemical sensitivity Vertebral subluxation Wilson's temperature syndrome v t e In alternative medicine , bodywork is any therapeutic or personal development technique that involves working with the human body in a form involving manipulative therapy , breath work , or energy medicine . Bodywork techniques also aim to assess or improve posture , promote awareness of the " bodymind connection " which is an approach that sees the human body and mind as a single integrated unit, or to manipulate the electromagnetic field alleged to surround the human body and affect health.
