The table is considered to be the largest of its kind ever produced by one person during the Middle Ages . Although the main purpose of the work was astronomical timekeeping, it can also be used to solve all problems of spherical trigonometry by changing the arguments of the table.

[ 1 ] Najm al‐Din also wrote an important illustrated treatise that describes more than 100 different astronomical instruments, such as astrolabes , quadrants and sundials , including ones he invented himself. This work is of high importance for modern scholars and one of the main sources on the subject.
