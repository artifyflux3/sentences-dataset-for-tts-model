Indexing CODEN ( alt ) · JSTOR ( alt ) · LCCN ( alt ) MIAR · NLM ( alt ) · Scopus · W&L ISSN 0018-8646 (print) 0018-8646 (web) Links Journal homepage Online access IBM Journal of Research and Development is a former, peer-reviewed bimonthly scientific journal covering research on information systems . 
This Journal has ceased production in 2020.

[ 1 ] According to the Journal Citation Reports in 2019, the journal had an impact factor of 1.27.

[ 2 ] IBM also published the IBM Systems Journal ( ISSN 0018-8670 ) starting in 1962; it ceased publication in 2008 and was absorbed in part by the IBM Journal of Research and Development .
