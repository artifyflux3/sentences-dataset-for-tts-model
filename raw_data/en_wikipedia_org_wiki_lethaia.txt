02277593 Links Journal homepage Lethaia is a quarterly peer-reviewed scientific journal of Earth science , covering research on palaeontology and stratigraphy . Now published by Scandinavian University Press , it was originally published by the International Commission on Stratigraphy . It is an official publication of the International Palaeontological Association and the International Commission on Stratigraphy. The journal had a 2012 impact factor of 2.488, ranking it 7th out of 48 journals in the category "Paleontology", though its IF has since declined.
