The Royal Museums Greenwich Foundation is a Private Limited Company by guarantee without share capital use of 'Limited' exemption, company number 08002287, incorporated on 22 March 2012.

[ 1 ] It is registered as charity number 1147279.

[ 2 ] National Maritime Museum Queen's House Royal Observatory, Greenwich Cutty Sark For a year between 2016 and 2017 the museum reported 2.41 million visitors to the National Maritime Museum.
