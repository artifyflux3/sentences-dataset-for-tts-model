Zoophobia , or animal phobia , is the irrational fear or aversion towards non-human animals. Zoophobia is the general negative reaction to animals, but it is usually divided into many subgroups, each being of a specific type of zoophobia. Although zoophobia as a whole is quite rare, types of the fear are common.

[ 1 ] As mentioned before by Sigmund Freud , an animal phobia is one of the most frequent psychoneurotic diseases among children.
