He served as a Preacher General Dominican Order and a lecturer in ecclesiastical history at the University of Florence .

[ 1 ] He is most famous for his involvement in the Galileo trails, the Galileo affair . He was a member of the Pigeon League named for one of Galileo's rivals, Lodovico delle Colombe . Lorini instigated the events of 1616 by sending the Roman Inquisition a copy of Galileo's letter to Benedetto Castelli .
