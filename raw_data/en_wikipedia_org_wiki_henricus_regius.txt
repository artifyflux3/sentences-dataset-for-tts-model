[ 1 ] Biography [ edit ] Regius was born in Utrecht , and was also known by his birth name, Hendrik de Roy , or by its French rendering, Henri Le Roy .  He studied liberal arts at the University of Franeker and medicine at Groningen University , Leiden University , and subsequently at the Universities of Montpellier and Padua .

He was a vocal proponent of Cartesianism , and corresponded frequently with René Descartes .

[ 2 ] He was the author of a textbook of natural philosophy, Fundamenta Physices .

He died in Utrecht.
