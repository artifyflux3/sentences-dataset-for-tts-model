National liberation has been promoted by Marxists out of an international-socialist perspective rather than a bourgeois nationalist perspective.

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] [ 3 ] [ 4 ] [ 5 ] After the Russian Revolution , revolutionaries declared that all peoples had the right to self-determination. While communism is critical of nationalism , communists claimed that the cause of national liberation was not a matter of chauvinism , but a matter of radical democracy .
