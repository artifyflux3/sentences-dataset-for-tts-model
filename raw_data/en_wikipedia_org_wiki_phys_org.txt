Find sources: "Phys.org" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( May 2024 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) Phys.org Available in English Founded 2004 ; 21 years ago ( 2004 ) Headquarters United Kingdom URL phys .org Phys.org is an online science, research and technology news aggregator which re-publishes press releases and stories from news agencies (a business model known as churnalism ).

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] [ 3 ] In 2012, PhysOrg.com was changed to Phys.org.

[ 4 ] As of 2014 [update] , Phys.org was posting an average of 98 items per day.

[ 5 ] It is part of the Science X network of websites, headquartered on the Isle of Man .
