**Phys.org** is an online science, research and technology [news aggregator](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/News_aggregator "News aggregator") which re-publishes [press releases](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Press_release "Press release") and stories from [news agencies](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/News_agencies "News agencies") (a business model known as [churnalism](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Churnalism "Churnalism")).[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phys.org#cite_note-1)[[2]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phys.org#cite_note-2)[[3]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phys.org#cite_note-3) In 2012, **PhysOrg.com** was changed to Phys.org.[[4]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phys.org#cite_note-4) As of 2014[[update]](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Phys.org&action=edit), Phys.org was posting an average of 98 items per day.[[5]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phys.org#cite_note-5) It is part of the Science X network of websites, headquartered on the [Isle of Man](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Isle_of_Man "Isle of Man").
