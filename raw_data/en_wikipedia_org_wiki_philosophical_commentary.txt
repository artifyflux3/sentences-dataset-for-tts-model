[ 1 ] [ 2 ] A large portion of the schools of thought was originated through the analysis that different commentators carried out on renowned philosophical texts, especially texts from Plato and Aristotle . A significant portion of Thomas Aquinas 's philosophical ideas were the result of commentaries to some of Aristotle's ideas.
