39018023 Links Journal homepage Protist archives Archiv für Protistenkunde archives Protist is a peer-reviewed scientific journal focusing on protists . It was founded as Archiv für Protistenkunde by editor Fritz Schaudinn in 1902, and originally published by Gustav Fischer and later Jena . The journal is now published by Elsevier , and is currently edited by Michael Melkonian (Botanical Institute, University of Cologne ). The journal changed its name to Protist in 1998.
