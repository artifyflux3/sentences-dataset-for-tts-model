A. Guerreiro, H.D.T. Nguyen, Begerow (2023) Orders Peribolosporales The Peribolosporomycetes are a class of fungi in the subdivision Ustilaginomycotina of the Basidiomycota . Species have been isolated from forest soils and are notably heat resistant and osmotolerant . They produce hyphae bearing conidia and chlamydospores in culture.
