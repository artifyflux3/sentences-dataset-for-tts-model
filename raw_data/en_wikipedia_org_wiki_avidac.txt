It was built by the Laboratory's Physics Division for $250,000 and began operations on January 28, 1953.

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] [ 3 ] As with almost all computers of its era, it was a one-of-a-kind machine that could not exchange programs with other computers (even other IAS machines ).
