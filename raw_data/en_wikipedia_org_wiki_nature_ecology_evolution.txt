969358709 Links Journal homepage Nature Ecology and Evolution is an online-only monthly peer-reviewed scientific journal published by Nature Portfolio covering all aspects of research on ecology and evolutionary biology . It was established in 2017. Its first editor-in-chief was Patrick Goymer.

[ 1 ] The current editor-in-chief is Simon Harold.

[ 2 ] According to the Journal Citation Reports , Nature Ecology and Evolution has a 2024 impact factor of 14.2.
