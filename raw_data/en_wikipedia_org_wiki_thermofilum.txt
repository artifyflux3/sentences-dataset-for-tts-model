[ 2 ] Thermofilum pendens is a hyperthermic member of the archael kingdom Crenarchaeota , [ 3 ] and represents a deep branch in the order Thermoproteales .

T. pendens lacks the genes for purine nucleotide biosynthesis and thus relies on environmental sources to meet its purine requirements.

[ 4 ] [ 3 ] Taxonomy [ edit ] The currently accepted taxonomy is based on the List of Prokaryotic names with Standing in Nomenclature (LPSN) [ 1 ] and National Center for Biotechnology Information (NCBI).
