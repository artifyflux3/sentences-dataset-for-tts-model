Spirotaenia Spirotaenia condensata Scientific classification (unranked): Archaeplastida Kingdom: Viridiplantae Genus: Spirotaenia Brébisson 1848 Type species Spirotaenia condensata Brébisson 1848 Synonyms Entospira Kuntze 1898 Endospira Brébisson 1850 Spirotaenia is a genus of basal unicellular green algae that may be sister to the Chlorokybophyceae .

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] It was previously considered to be part of the Zygnemataceae . It is sexually conjugating , a mode of reproduction that was previously only known in the Zygnemataceae / Mesotaeniaceae , the sister groups to the land plants . This is surprising, as Spirotaenia is much more basal. The conjugating process is substantially aberrant.

[ further explanation needed ] [ 3 ] [ 4 ] Spirotaenia may actually be more than one distinct lineage which may not be closely related.
