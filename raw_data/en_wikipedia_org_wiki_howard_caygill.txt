He has held the position of Professor of Modern European Philosophy at the Centre for Research in Modern European Philosophy (CRMEP) Kingston University since 2011.

[ 1 ] Previously he had taught at University of East Anglia and Goldsmiths College, University of London .

[ 2 ] He is known for his work on Walter Benjamin , Immanuel Kant , Emmanuel Levinas , and Franz Kafka ; and concepts such as resistance have influenced fields including political philosophy , aesthetics , literary theory and continental philosophy .

Jay Bernstein has described Caygill as "one of the two or three leading practitioners and exponents of European philosophy in the UK".

[ 3 ] Caygill is the Literary Executor of the estate of Gillian Rose , [ 4 ] who was his supervisor at the University of Sussex .
