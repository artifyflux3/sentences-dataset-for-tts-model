[ 1 ] The Best Defensive Forward was first awarded in 1993 (the same year that the Best Defensive Defenseman award was revived) and every year thereafter. Three players ( Ian Sharp , Stephen Baby and Nico Sturm ) have received the award two separate times, all three doing so in consecutive years.
