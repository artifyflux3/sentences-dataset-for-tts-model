Indexing CODEN ( alt · alt2 ) · JSTOR ( alt ) · LCCN ( alt ) MIAR · NLM ( alt ) · Scopus · W&L CODEN BJLSBG ISSN 0024-4066 Links Journal homepage Online access The Biological Journal of the Linnean Society is a direct descendant of the oldest biological journal in the world, the Transactions of the Linnean Society . It succeeded the earlier title in 1969. The journal specializes in evolution , and encompasses work across all taxonomic groups in all five kingdoms of living organisms. It includes all methods, whole-organism or molecular , practical or theoretical.

[ 1 ] The journal is published by the Linnean Society of London .
