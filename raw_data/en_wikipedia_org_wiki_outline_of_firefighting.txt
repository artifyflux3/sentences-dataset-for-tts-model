( September 2017 ) The following outline is provided as an overview of and topical guide to firefighting: Firefighting – act of extinguishing fires . A firefighter fights these fires to prevent destruction of life, property and the environment. Firefighting is a highly technical skill that requires professionals who have spent years training in both general firefighting techniques and specialized areas of expertise.
