From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

(Redirected from [Blank space (disambiguation)](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Blank_space_(disambiguation)&redirect=no "Blank space (disambiguation)"))

**Blank space** may refer to:

*   [Space (punctuation)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_(punctuation) "Space (punctuation)"), a blank area devoid of content used to separate characters in writing 
    *   [Whitespace character](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Whitespace_character "Whitespace character"), any character that represents a space in typography

*   "[Blank Space](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blank_Space "Blank Space")", a song by Taylor Swift
*   "Blank Space", a song by Tanya Chua nominated for the [Best Composer award at the 19th Golden Melody Awards](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/19th_Golden_Melody_Awards#Best_Composer "19th Golden Melody Awards")
