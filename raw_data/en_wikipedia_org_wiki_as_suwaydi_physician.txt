Active in Cairo and Damascus , he compiled three works:   a treatise on plant names, a treatise on the medical use of stones, and a book of medical recipes and procedures ( Tadhkirah ).
As-Suwaydi's Tadhkirah was epitomized by Shaʿrānī in the 16th century.
