The Baltic plate collided against Siberia , to form the Ural Mountains about 280 million years ago. The Baltic plate, however, fused onto the Eurasian plate when the Baltic plate collided against Siberia when the Ural Mountains were completely formed. The Baltic plate contained Baltica and the Baltic Shield which is now located in Norway , Sweden and Finland .
