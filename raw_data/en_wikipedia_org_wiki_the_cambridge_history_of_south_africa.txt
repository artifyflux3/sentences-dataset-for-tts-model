The Cambridge History of South Africa is a two volume history of South Africa published by Cambridge University Press in 2009 (Vol. 1) and 2011 (Vol. 2).

Volumes [ edit ] Volume 1: From Early Times to 1885 . 2009. Edited by Carolyn Hamilton , Bernard K. Mbenga , and Robert Ross.

ISBN 9780521517942 [ 1 ] Volume 2: 1885–1994 . 2011. Edited by Robert Ross, Anne Kelk Mager , and Bill Nasson .
