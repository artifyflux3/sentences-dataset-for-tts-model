[ 1 ] [ 2 ] [ 3 ] The Moa plate was obliquely subducted beneath the Gondwana margin, but some material from it was accreted and continues to exist. Today, this material forms part of Eastern New Zealand . The plate was named in 2001 by Rupert Sutherland and Chris Hollis.
