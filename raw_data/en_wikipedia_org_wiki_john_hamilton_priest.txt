[ 1 ] As such they are responsible for the disciplinary supervision of the clergy [ 2 ] within the Raphoe part of the diocese , [ 3 ] which is by far the largest.

[ 4 ] The archdeaconry can trace its history back to Thomas O'Nahan , who held the office from 1299 to 1306, [ 5 ] to the current incumbent David Huss who assumed office in 2013.
