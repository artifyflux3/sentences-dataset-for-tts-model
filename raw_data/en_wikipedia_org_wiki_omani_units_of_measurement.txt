System before metric system [ edit ] Several units were used.

Muscat [ edit ] Length [ edit ] One muscat was equal to 39.13 in.

[ 1 ] Mass [ edit ] Units included: 1 maund = 24 kotschas = 8 + 3 ⁄ 4 lb 1 candy = 60 maunds.

[ 1 ] Capacity [ edit ] One ferren was equal to 7.9254 gallons and also equal to 34 sidios.
