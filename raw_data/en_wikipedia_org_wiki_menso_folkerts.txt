Early life [ edit ] Menso Folkerts was born on June 22, 1943, in Eschwege , Germany . From 1962 to 1967 he studied classical philology, mathematics and historical auxiliary sciences at the University of Göttingen .

[ 1 ] Career [ edit ] From 1980 to 2008 he was a professor at Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich .

He contibruted to the Encyclopedia Britannica .

[ 2 ] Folkerts completed a database of over 7000 letters written by or sent to Gauss, which can be accessed on the internet.

[ 3 ] Awards and Honours [ edit ] He received the Kenneth O. May Prize in 2013.

[ 4 ] His scholarship has been recognized by various academies.

[ 1 ] For example, he is a Member of the International Academy of the History of Science , [ 5 ] and the German National Academy of Sciences Leopoldina .
