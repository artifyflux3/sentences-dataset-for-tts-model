Find sources: "Abu al-Qasim Muqane'i" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( August 2017 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) Tahir ibn Mohammad ibn Ibrahim (Bokhari) or Abu al-Qasim Muqane'i was a Persian physician in the 10th century. He was the disciple of Rhazes .

Abu Bakr Rabee Ibn Ahmad Al-Akhawyni Bokhari cited him in his book Hidayat al-Muta`allemin Fi al-Tibb as "Master": What I'm saying here is from my master Abu al-Qasim Muqane'i whose name is Tahir and he was disciple of Al-razi [ 1 ] References [ edit ] ^ Najm Abadi, Mahmoud.

writings of Great Iranian Physician and scientist Muhammad ibn Zakariya al-Razi . Tehran university press.
