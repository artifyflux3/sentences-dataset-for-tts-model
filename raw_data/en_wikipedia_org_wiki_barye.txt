CGS base units 1 cm −1 ⋅g⋅s −2 SI units 0.1 Pa English Engineering units 1.450377 × 10 −5 psi The barye (symbol: Ba), or sometimes barad , barrie , bary , baryd , baryed , or barie , is the centimetre–gram–second (CGS) unit of pressure . It is equal to 1 dyne per square centimetre .
