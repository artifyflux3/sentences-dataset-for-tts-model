1640733 Links Journal homepage Mycologia at Taylor & Francis Online Mycologia is a peer-reviewed scientific journal that publishes papers on all aspects of the fungi , including lichens . It first appeared as a bimonthly journal in January 1909, published by the New York Botanical Garden under the editorship of William Murrill . It became the official journal of the Mycological Society of America , which still publishes it today. It was formed as a merger of the Journal of Mycology (14 volumes; 1885–1908) and the Mycological Bulletin (7 volumes; 1903–1908). The Mycological Bulletin was known as the Ohio Mycological Bulletin in its first volume.
