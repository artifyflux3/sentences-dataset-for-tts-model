[ 1 ] Cope was educated at Trinity College, Dublin .

[ 2 ] Previously Dean of Dromore , he was nominated to be Bishop of Clonfert and Kilmacduagh on 27 January 1772 and consecrated on 15 March that year.

Translated [ 3 ] to Ferns and Leighlin [ 4 ] on  9 August 1782, he died in post on 31 July 1787.
