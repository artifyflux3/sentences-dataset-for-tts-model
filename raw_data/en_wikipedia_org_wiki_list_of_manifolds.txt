Generic families of manifolds [ edit ] Euclidean space , R n n -sphere , S n n -torus , T n Real projective space , RP n Complex projective space , CP n Quaternionic projective space , HP n Flag manifold Grassmann manifold Stiefel manifold Lie groups provide several interesting families. See Table of Lie groups for examples. See also: List of simple Lie groups and List of Lie group topics .
