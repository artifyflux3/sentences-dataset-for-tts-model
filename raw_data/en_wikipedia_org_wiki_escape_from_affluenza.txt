While the original concentrates on affluenza — consumerism and materialism in modern society—the sequel focuses on how to avoid this.

[ 1 ] It looks at stories of how to reduce debt , stress , time-pressure, and possession-overload.

[ 2 ] The cast includes Wanda Urbanska as the host and Cecile Andrews, author of "Circle of Simplicity".
