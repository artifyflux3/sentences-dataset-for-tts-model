299662112 Links Journal homepage Marine Biology is a peer-reviewed scientific journal covering research on all aspects of marine biology . The journal was established in 1967 and is published monthly by Springer Science+Business Media . The editor-in-chief is Ulrich Sommer ( Helmholtz Centre for Ocean Research ). According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2014 impact factor of 2.391.
