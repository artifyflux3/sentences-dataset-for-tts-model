84003117 [ 1 ] Added to NRHP September 24, 1984 Caldwell Hall , on the Cornell University campus, was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1984.

[ 1 ] It was named after George Chapman Caldwell (1834–1907), the first head of the chemistry department at Cornell.
