The Global Southwest is the southwestern region of the world. It mainly includes Latin America , [ 1 ] although it can broadly include the Caribbean as well.

[ 2 ] The term can also be used to refer to the interaction between the American South (or the southern border region in general) and Latin America (primarily with Mexico ).
