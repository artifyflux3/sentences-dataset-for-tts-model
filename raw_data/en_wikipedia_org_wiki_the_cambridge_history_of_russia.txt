Country United Kingdom Language English Genre Russian history Publisher Cambridge University Press Published 2006 No. of books 3 The Cambridge History of Russia is a multi-volume survey of Russian history published by Cambridge University Press (CUP).

Volumes [ edit ] Vol. 1.

From Early Rus' to 1689 edited by Maureen Perrie , covering Russian history before Peter the Great ( ISBN 978-0-521-81227-6 ) Vol. 2.

Imperial Russia, 1689–1917 edited by Dominic Lieven , covering the Russian Empire ( ISBN 978-0-521-81529-1 ) Vol. 3.

The Twentieth Century edited by Ronald Grigor Suny , covering Russian history after the October Revolution , including the Soviet Union ( ISBN 978-0-521-81144-6 ) See also [ edit ] History of Russia The Cambridge History of Russian Literature The Cambridge History of Inner Asia , which includes many chapters on nomadic and forest people in present-day Russia.
