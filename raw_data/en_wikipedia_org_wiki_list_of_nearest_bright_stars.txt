Right ascension and declination coordinates are for the epoch J2000 . The distance measurements are based on the Hipparcos Catalogue and other astrometric data. In the event of a spectroscopic binary , the combined spectral type and absolute magnitude are listed in italics .

The list is ordered by increasing distance.
