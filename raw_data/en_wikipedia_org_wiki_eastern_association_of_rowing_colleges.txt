It is an affiliate of the Eastern College Athletic Conference (ECAC).

[ 1 ] Members [ edit ] Fifteen colleges and universities are members of the EARC, mostly in the North-East and Mid-Atlantic United States, but also in the Mid-West with the University of Wisconsin–Madison .

[ 2 ] All eight Ivy League universities are members of the EARC.
