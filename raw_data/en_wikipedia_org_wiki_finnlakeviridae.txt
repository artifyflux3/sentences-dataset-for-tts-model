The family contains a single genus, Finnlakevirus , which contains a single species, Finnlakevirus FLiP .

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] [ 3 ] This virus was isolated in 2010, with its gram-negative host Flavobacterium , from Lake Jyväsjärvi , a boreal freshwater habitat in Central Finland , and is the first described single-stranded DNA virus with an internal membrane.

[ 2 ] Genome organization of Flavobacterium phage FLiP .

The genome is circular single-stranded DNA of about 9200 nucleotides in length.
