The founding editor was Morris H. DeGroot , who explained the mission of the journal in his 1986 editorial: [ 1 ] "A central purpose of Statistical Science is to convey the richness, breadth and unity of the field by presenting
the full range of contemporary statistical thought at a modest technical level accessible to the wide community
of practitioners, teachers, researchers and students of statistics and probability."
