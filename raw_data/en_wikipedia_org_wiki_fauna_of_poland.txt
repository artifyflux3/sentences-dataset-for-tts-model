[ 1 ] The most common type of fauna in Poland are arthropods (Arthropoda), and within the phyla, insects (Insecta) are most numerous.

[ 1 ] Polish fauna is represented by vertebrates including but not limited to: [ 1 ] ray-finned fishes (Actinopterygii) – 129 species (35 species are believed to be non-indigenous) [ 2 ] amphibians (Amphibia) – 18 species reptiles (Reptilia) – 9 species birds (Aves) – 435 species mammals (Mammalia) – 105 species See also [ edit ] List of birds of Poland List of Lepidoptera of Poland List of mammals of Poland List of non-marine molluscs of Poland List of reptiles of Poland References [ edit ] ^ a b c Piotr Węgrzynowicz (2007-12-16).
