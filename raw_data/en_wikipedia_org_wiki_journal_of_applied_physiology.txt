11603017 Links Journal homepage The Journal of Applied Physiology is a monthly peer-reviewed medical journal of physiology published by the American Physiological Society . The journal was established in 1948, and is currently edited by Sue Bodine .

[ 1 ] According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2020 impact factor of 3.531.
