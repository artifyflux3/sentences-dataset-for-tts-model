Summary of women's fashion silhouet changes, 1794–1887 The following is a chronological list of articles covering the history of Western fashion —the story of the changing fashions in clothing in countries under influence of the Western world ⁠—from the 5th century to the present. The series focuses primarily on the history of fashion in Western European countries and countries in the core Anglosphere .
