( August 2015 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) Aleutian Current is also called the "Subarctic Current". An eastward flowing ocean current which lies north of the North Pacific Current ; it is the northern branch of the Kuroshio Current which moves northeast then east between 40° N and 50° N. As it approaches the coast of North America it divides to form the northward-flowing Alaska Current and the southward-flowing California Current .
