It was discovered in the images transmitted by the Cassini spacecraft. It received the name of Dilmun , the garden paradise in Sumerian mythology .

Dilmun is located near the equator, centered on 15°N and 175 ° W.

[ 1 ] The large dark area of Shangri-la is located in the south.
