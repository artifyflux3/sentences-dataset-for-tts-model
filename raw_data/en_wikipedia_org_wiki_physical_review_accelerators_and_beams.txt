932197551 Links Journal homepage Online archive Physical Review Accelerators and Beams is a monthly peer-reviewed open-access scientific journal , published by the American Physical Society . The journal focuses on accelerator physics and engineering.

[ 1 ] Its lead editor is Frank Zimmermann ( CERN ). The journal was established in 1998 as Physical Review Special Topics – Accelerators and Beams , obtaining its current title in 2016.

[ 2 ] The journal does not require article processing charges , being sponsored by academic and industrial institutions.

[ 1 ] Abstracting and indexing [ edit ] The journal is abstracted and indexed in: Current Contents /Physical, Chemical & Earth Sciences [ 3 ] Inspec [ 4 ] Science Citation Index Expanded [ 3 ] Scopus [ 5 ] According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2021 impact factor of 1.879.
