[ 1 ] The internecine quarrel took place in the years before World War I, between the members of the Verein für Socialpolitik ; the primary antagonists were Max Weber , Werner Sombart , and Gustav Schmoller .

The Zweite Werturteilsstreit —better known as Positivismusstreit — was the debate between the supporters of Kritische Theorie and those of Kritischer Rationalismus during the 1960s .
