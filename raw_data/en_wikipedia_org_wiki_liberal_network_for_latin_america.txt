Libertarianism Left Right Pirate Party Progressivism Sexually liberal feminism Utilitarianism Liberalism portal Politics portal v t e The Liberal Network for Latin America ( Spanish : Red Liberal de América Latina ), ( Portuguese : Rede Liberal da América Latina ), abbreviated to RELIAL , is a network of 46 liberal institutions from 16 Latin American countries. Members of RELIAL include political parties as well as think tanks, foundations and research institutes. RELIAL is the regional organization of the Liberal International .

It was founded in 2003 with the official launch taking place in Costa Rica November, 2004.
