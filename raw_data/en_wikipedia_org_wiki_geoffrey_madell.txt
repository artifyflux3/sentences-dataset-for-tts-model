Madell was Senior Lecturer in Philosophy at the University of Edinburgh . He authored articles for Mind and the Philosophy journal.

[ 1 ] He defended Cartesian dualism in his book Mind and Materialism , published in 1988.

[ 2 ] [ 3 ] He commented that "interactionist dualism looks to be by far the only plausible framework in which the facts of our experience can be fitted".
