Zhonghe Zhou ( Chinese : 周忠和 ; born 19 January 1965 in Jiangdu , Jiangsu province) is a Chinese palaeontologist . He described the ancient bird Confuciusornis .

[ 1 ] Zhonghe graduated from Nanjing University and earned a Ph.D. in Biology in 1999 from the University of Kansas . He is the director of the Institute of Vertebrate Paleontology and Paleoanthropology of the Chinese Academy of Sciences in Beijing , and in 2010 was elected to the U.S. National Academy of Sciences .
