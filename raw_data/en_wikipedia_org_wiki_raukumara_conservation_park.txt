[ 1 ] The park is managed by the New Zealand Department of Conservation .

[ 1 ] Geography [ edit ] The park covers 111,021 hectares (274,340 acres) of the Raukumara Range and surrounding area, [ 2 ] including Mount Hikurangi and the Mōtū River .

[ 1 ] History [ edit ] The park was established in 1979.
