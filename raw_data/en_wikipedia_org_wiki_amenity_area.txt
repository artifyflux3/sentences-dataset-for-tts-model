[ 1 ] Under the Conservation Act 1987 , designated Amenity Areas must legally be managed so: its indigenous natural resources and its historic resources are protected it contributes to and allow for people to appreciate its indigenous natural resources and its historic resources [ 1 ] it fosters the recreational features of the area.
