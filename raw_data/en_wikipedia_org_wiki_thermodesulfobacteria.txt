[ 3 ] Phylogeny [ edit ] See also: Bacterial taxonomy The currently accepted taxonomy is based on the List of Prokaryotic names with Standing in Nomenclature (LPSN) [ 1 ] and National Center for Biotechnology Information (NCBI).
