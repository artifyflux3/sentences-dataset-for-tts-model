Look up _**[spacing](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/spacing "wiktionary:spacing")**_ in Wiktionary, the free dictionary.

**Spacing** may refer to:

*   [_Spacing_ (magazine)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spacing_(magazine) "Spacing (magazine)"), a Canadian magazine
*   [Spacing effect](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spacing_effect "Spacing effect") in psychology; the opposite of cramming
*   The usage of [spaces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_(punctuation) "Space (punctuation)") in typography 
    *   [Letter spacing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Letter_spacing "Letter spacing"), the amount of space between a group of letters
    *   Line spacing, interline spacing, or [leading](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leading "Leading"), the amount of added vertical spacing between lines of type
    *   [Sentence spacing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sentence_spacing "Sentence spacing"), the horizontal space between sentences in typeset text
    *   [French spacing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/French_spacing "French spacing"), one convention for the use of spaces in printed text around punctuation, words, and sentences
    *   [Word spacing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Word_spacing "Word spacing"), the amount of space between words

*   Spacing, a science fiction term for a theoretical method of execution by [space exposure](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_exposure "Space exposure")
*   Spacing, the distance between microphones in an AB microphone system (see [time-of-arrival stereophony](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stereophonic_sound#A-B_technique:_time-of-arrival_stereophonytime-of-arrival "Stereophonic sound"))
