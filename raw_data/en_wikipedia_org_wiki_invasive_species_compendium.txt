[ 1 ] It comprises peer-reviewed datasheets, images, and maps, a bibliographic database, and full text articles. New datasheets, data sets, and scientific literature are added on a weekly basis. The ISC has been resourced by a diverse international consortium of government departments, non-governmental organizations , and private companies.

[ 2 ] Coverage [ edit ] The Invasive Species Compendium currently covers over 1,500 species with over 7,000 basic summary datasheets and 1,500 detailed datasheets.

[ 3 ] In addition, it provides access to over 1,100 full text articles (in PDF format) and 75,000 article abstracts.
