From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Functional group

In [organic chemistry](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Organic_chemistry "Organic chemistry"), the term **cyanomethyl** ([cyanide](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cyanide "Cyanide") (N≡C) + [methyl](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Methyl_group "Methyl group") (CH 3)) designates:

*   A **cyanomethyl [group](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Functional_group "Functional group")** (**N≡CCH 2–**), a type of [nitrile group](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nitrile_group "Nitrile group")
*   The **cyanomethyl [radical](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Radical_(chemistry) "Radical (chemistry)")** (**N≡CCH 2·**)
*   The **cyanomethyl [carbanion](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carbanion "Carbanion")** (**N≡CCH 2−**)
