[ 1 ] He was the son of George Wandesford and Catharine Hanby, and the brother of Christopher Wandesford , [ 2 ] [ 3 ] who was to be Lord Deputy of Ireland in 1640, [ 4 ] Michael Wandsford was appointed Dean of Limerick in May 1635; [ 5 ] and Dean of Derry in November 1635.

[ 6 ] He died in 1637.
