Find sources: "Poly-Weibull distribution" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( April 2024 ) In probability theory and statistics , the poly-Weibull distribution is a continuous probability distribution.  The distribution is defined to be that of a random variable defined to be the smallest of a number of statistically independent random variables having non-identical Weibull distributions .
