It is a gas giant with an average radius about nine times that of Earth .

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] Although only one-eighth the average density of Earth, with its larger volume Saturn is just over 95 times more massive.

[ 3 ] [ 4 ] Saturn is named after the Roman god of agriculture ; its astronomical symbol (♄) represents the god's sickle .
