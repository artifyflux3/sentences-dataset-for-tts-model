Nucleus with a dark nucleolus. The cell usually measures between 20 and 30 micrometers.

The nuclear envelope does not break down during mitosis , which is thus termed closed mitosis , or "dinomitosis".

[ 1 ] The mitotic spindle is extranuclear.

[ 2 ] Histones are absent.

[ 3 ] However, recent EST sequencing has revealed the presence of histones in one of the closest relative to dinoflagellates, Perkinsus marinus and an early-branching dinoflagellate, Hematodinium sp.

[ 4 ] However, histone-like proteins of bacterial origins are found in the coding regions at periphery of the dinokaryon's chromosomes.
