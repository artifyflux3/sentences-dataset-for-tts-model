Its headquarters are located in Daejeon , in the Daedeok Science Town . Research at KASI covers main areas of modern astronomy, including Optical Astronomy , Radio Astronomy , Space Science , and Theoretical Astronomy . As of May 2024, KASI is an affiliated research institute of the Korea AeroSpace Administration .
