Science
-------

[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Lineage&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Science")]

*   [Lineage (anthropology)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lineage_(anthropology) "Lineage (anthropology)"), a group that can demonstrate its common descent from an apical ancestor or a direct line of descent from an ancestor
*   [Lineage (evolution)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lineage_(evolution) "Lineage (evolution)"), a temporal sequence of individuals, populations or species which represents a continuous line of descent
*   [Lineage (genetic)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lineage_(genetic) "Lineage (genetic)")
*   [Lineage markers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lineage_markers "Lineage markers")
*   [Data lineage](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Data_lineage "Data lineage")

Gaming
------

[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Lineage&action=edit&section=2 "Edit section: Gaming")]

*   [Lineage (series)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lineage_(series) "Lineage (series)"), a medieval fantasy massively multiplayer online role-playing game franchise 
    *   [_Lineage_ (video game)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lineage_(video_game) "Lineage (video game)"), the original 1998 game
    *   _[Lineage II](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lineage\_II "Lineage II")_, a 2003 prequel to _Lineage_
    *   _[Lineage III](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lineage\_III "Lineage III")_, an upcoming sequel to _Lineage II_
    *   _[Lineage W](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lineage\_W "Lineage W")_, 3D mobile version of Lineage aiming for global service

*   _[Assassin's Creed: Lineage](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Assassin%27s\_Creed:\_Lineage "Assassin's Creed: Lineage")_, a series of short films based on the _Assassin's Creed II_ video game
