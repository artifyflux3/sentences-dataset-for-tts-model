[ 1 ] He was the author of a treatise on the arrangement of Aristotle 's writings and his system of philosophy which was quoted by Simplicius , [ 2 ] and by Achilles Tatius .

[ citation needed ] Some commentaries of his on the Timaeus of Plato are also quoted by Porphyry , [ 3 ] which was also used by Theon of Smyrna in the surviving sections of his On Mathematics Useful for the Understanding of Plato , [ 1 ] [ 4 ] and a treatise on the Categories of Aristotle by Galen .

[ 5 ] [ page needed ] None of these works have survived.

[ 5 ] In some medieval manuscripts, a work with the title Harmonica was attributed to Adrastus, however, this was a misattribution of a work by Manuel Bryennios .
