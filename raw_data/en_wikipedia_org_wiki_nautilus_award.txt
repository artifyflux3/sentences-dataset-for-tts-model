Szmidt of the Science Fiction magazine .

[ 1 ] Each year, five novels and short stories are recognized. The awards have been presented for years 2003-2007, 2009-2010 and 2012. The awards were not given out due in 2008 to magazine publisher change and in 2011 due to the magazine folding. The last edition of the award, in 2014, was cancelled due to controversies over vote counting, and the award has not been awarded sice.
