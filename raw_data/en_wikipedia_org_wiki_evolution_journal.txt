Evolution is published by Oxford Academic (formerly by Wiley ) for the Society for the Study of Evolution . Its current editor-in-chief is Jason Wolf of the University of Bath , United Kingdom.

[ 1 ] Former editors-in-chief [ edit ] The journal was founded soon after the Second World War. Its first editor was the systematic ornithologist Ernst Mayr .
