Born in Osnabrück , Lower Saxony , he studied mathematics , mathematical logic and physics in Münster . He established and developed the theory of complex-analytic spaces in joint work with Hans Grauert . Until his retirement in 1995, he was a professor for complex analysis in Münster .

Remmert wrote two books on number theory and complex analysis , which contain a huge amount of historical information together with references on important papers in the subject.
