Logo The Internet Philosophy Ontology Project ( InPhO / ˈ ɪ n f oʊ / ) is a project of Indiana University 's Cognitive Science Program funded by a grant from the National Endowment for the Humanities . It is an attempt to create a model of the discipline of philosophy as an online resource. The website makes it possible to search and navigate via relations among philosophical ideas, scholars and works.
