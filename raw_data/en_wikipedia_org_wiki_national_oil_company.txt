[ 1 ] : 3 NOCs produce about half the world’s oil and gas.

[ 2 ] [ 3 ] Due to their increasing dominance over global reserves, the importance of NOCs has risen dramatically in recent decades relative to International Oil Companies (IOCs), such as BP , ExxonMobil or Shell plc . NOCs are also increasingly investing outside their national borders.
