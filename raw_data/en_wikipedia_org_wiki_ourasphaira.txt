Proterozoic Archean Had.

↓ Scientific classification Kingdom: Fungi (?) Genus: † Ourasphaira Species: † O. giraldae Binomial name † Ourasphaira giraldae Loron et al.

, 2019 [ 1 ] Ourasphaira giraldae is an extinct process -bearing multicellular eukaryotic microorganism . Corentin Loron argues that it was an early fungus . It existed approximately a billion years ago during the time of the transition from the Mesoproterozoic to Neoproterozoic periods, and was unearthed in the Amundsen Basin in the Canadian Arctic , specifically from layers within the Grassy Bay Formation .

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] [ 3 ] [ 4 ] This fungus may have existed on land well before plants .
