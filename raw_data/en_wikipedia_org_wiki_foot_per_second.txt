m/s 0.3048 mph 0.6818 kn 0.5925 km/h 1.0973 The foot per second (plural feet per second ) is a unit of both speed (scalar) and velocity (vector quantity, which includes direction).

[ 1 ] It expresses the distance in feet ( ft ) traveled or displaced, divided by the time in seconds ( s ).

[ 2 ] The corresponding unit in the International System of Units (SI) is the meter per second .

Abbreviations include ft/s , fps , and the scientific notation ft s −1 .
