He conducted experiments in mechanics and invented the Morin dynamometer . He introduced the term coefficient of friction and demonstrated its utility.

[ 1 ] In 1850, he was elected a foreign member of the Royal Swedish Academy of Sciences .  His name is one of the 72 names inscribed on the Eiffel Tower . He was conferred with Honorary Membership of the Institution of Engineers and Shipbuilders in Scotland in 1859.

[1] He was named as one of the 23 "Men of Tribology" by Duncan Dowson .
