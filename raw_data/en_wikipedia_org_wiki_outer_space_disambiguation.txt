From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

[![Image 5](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/99/Wiktionary-logo-en-v2.svg/40px-Wiktionary-logo-en-v2.svg.png)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Wiktionary-logo-en-v2.svg)

Look up _**[outer space](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/outer\_space "wiktionary:outer space")**_ in Wiktionary, the free dictionary.

**[Outer space](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Outer_space "Outer space")** comprises the relatively empty regions of the universe outside the atmospheres of celestial bodies.

**Outer space** may also refer to:

Music
-----

[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Outer_space_(disambiguation)&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Music")]

*   [OuterSpace](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/OuterSpace "OuterSpace"), a hip hop duo from Philadelphia
*   [_Outerspace_ (album)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Outerspace_(album) "Outerspace (album)"), a 2004 compilation by OuterSpace
*   [_Outer Space_ (EP)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Outer_Space_(EP) "Outer Space (EP)"), a 2009 EP by S-Endz
*   [The Outerspace](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Outerspace "The Outerspace"), a 1995 EP by Transwave
*   "Outer Space", a 2015 song by 5 Seconds of Summer from _[Sounds Good Feels Good](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sounds\_Good\_Feels\_Good "Sounds Good Feels Good")_
*   "Outer Space", a 1997 song by the Muffs from _[Happy Birthday to Me](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Happy\_Birthday\_to\_Me\_(album) "Happy Birthday to Me (album)")_
*   ["Outer Space" (Yelawolf song)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Outer_Space_(Yelawolf_song) "Outer Space (Yelawolf song)"), from the 2015 album _Love Story_
*   "Cosmos (Outer Space)", a 2005 song by t.A.T.u. from _[Dangerous and Moving](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dangerous\_and\_Moving "Dangerous and Moving")_
