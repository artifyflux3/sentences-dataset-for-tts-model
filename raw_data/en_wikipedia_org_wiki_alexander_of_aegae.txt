[ 1 ] He was tutor to the emperor Nero .

[ 2 ] [ 3 ] He wrote commentaries on the Categories [ 4 ] and the De Caelo [ 5 ] of Aristotle .

[ 6 ] He had a son named Caelinus or Caecilius.

[ 2 ] Attempts in the 19th century to ascribe some of the works of Alexander of Aphrodisias to Alexander of Aegae have been shown to be mistaken.
