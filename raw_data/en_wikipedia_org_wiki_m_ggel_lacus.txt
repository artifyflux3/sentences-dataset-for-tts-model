Feature type Lacus Coordinates 84°26′N 203°30′W ﻿ / ﻿ 84.44°N 203.5°W ﻿ / 84.44; -203.5 Diameter 170 km [ note 1 ] Eponym Müggelsee Müggel Lacus is one of a number of hydrocarbon seas and lakes found on Saturn's largest moon, Titan .

[ 1 ] The lake is located at latitude 84.44° N and longitude 203.5° W on Titan 's globe, [ 2 ] and is composed of liquid methane and ethane , [ 3 ] With a diameter of 170 km, it is the fifth largest of Titan's named lakes. It is named after Müggelsee in Berlin , Germany, the term being adopted by the IAU on Dec 3, 2013.

[ 2 ] Notes [ edit ] ^ The USGS web site gives the size as a "diameter", but it is actually the length in the longest dimension.
