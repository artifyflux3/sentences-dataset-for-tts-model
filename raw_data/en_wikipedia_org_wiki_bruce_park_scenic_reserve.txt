Memorial to Robert Cunningham Bruce within the reserve Bruce Park Scenic Reserve is located near Hunterville in the Rangitikei District of the North Island of New Zealand .

[ 1 ] The park is named after Robert Cunningham Bruce , who gifted the forest remnant to the public.

The remnant contains a variety of native flora including rimu , tōtara and kahikatea trees.
