Pake Prize" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( March 2017 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) The George E. Pake Prize is a prize that has been awarded annually by the American Physical Society since 1984. The recipients are chosen for " outstanding work by physicists combining original research accomplishments with leadership in the management of research or development in industry ". The prize is named after George E. Pake (1924–2004), founding director of Xerox PARC , and as of 2007 it is valued at $5,000.
