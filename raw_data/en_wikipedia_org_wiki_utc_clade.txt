Scientific classification Kingdom: Plantae Division: Chlorophyta Clade : UTC clade Fučiková et al. 2014 [ 1 ] The UTC clade is a grouping of Chlorophyta .

[ 2 ] It includes Ulvophyceae , Trebouxiophyceae and Chlorophyceae .
