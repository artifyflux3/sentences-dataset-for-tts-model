Basiluzzo ( Italian pronunciation: [baziˈluttso] ) is an islet (1 km 2 (0.39 sq mi)), and the smallest of eight islands in the Aeolian Islands , a volcanic island chain north of Sicily . In antiquity, the island was named "Hicesia".

[ 1 ] Media [ edit ] It is featured in the Italian film L'avventura , where the characters are on a motor boat looking for somewhere to swim.

[ 2 ] They sail up to Basiluzzo but decide to travel to Lisca Bianca , another of the Aeolian Islands nearest to Basiluzzo.
