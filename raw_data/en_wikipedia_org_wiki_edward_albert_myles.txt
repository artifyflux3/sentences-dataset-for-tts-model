He was ordained deacon in 1889 and priest in 1890. He began his career with curacies in Donaghcloney , Belfast and Seapatrick . He was the incumbent at Tullylish [ 3 ] from 1896 until his death.

[ 4 ] He was appointed Dean of Dromore in 1933, serving until 1950.
