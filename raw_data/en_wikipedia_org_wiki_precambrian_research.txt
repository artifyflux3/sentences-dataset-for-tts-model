Indexing CODEN ( alt · alt2 ) · JSTOR ( alt ) · LCCN ( alt ) MIAR · NLM ( alt ) · Scopus · W&L ISSN 0301-9268 Links Journal homepage Online archive Precambrian Research is a peer-reviewed scientific journal covering the geology of the Earth and its planetary neighbors. It is published by Elsevier and, as of 2014 [update] , the editor-in-chief is V. Pease ( Stockholm University ). It was established in 1974. According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2022 impact factor of 3.8.
