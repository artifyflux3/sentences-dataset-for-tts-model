[ 1 ] The library opened in 1973 as part of the Harvard Science Center and was named after Godfrey Lowell Cabot , a Harvard graduate and chemist.

[ 1 ] The library was redesigned in 2016 and reopened in 2017, with more flexible spaces and updated media resources.
