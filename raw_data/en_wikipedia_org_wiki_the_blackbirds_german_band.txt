**The Blackbirds** were a German [Krautrock](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Krautrock "Krautrock") band active 1968-1983. The band had a similar sound to [New Trolls](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Trolls "New Trolls").[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Blackbirds_(German_band)#cite_note-1) The band came together for a 50 year reunion in 2015.[[2]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Blackbirds_(German_band)#cite_note-2)
