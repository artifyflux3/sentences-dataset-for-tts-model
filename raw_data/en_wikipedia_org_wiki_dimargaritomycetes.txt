Benj.

1979 [ 1 ] Families Dimargaritaceae Dimargaritales is a monotypic order of fungi in the monotypic Dimargaritomycetes class within the subdivision of Kickxellomycotina .

[ 2 ] Dimargaritales was published in 1979, while Dimargaritomycetes was published by Tedersoo et al. in Fungal Diversity vol.90, Issue 1 on page 151 in 2018.

[ 3 ] It is parasitic .

[ 4 ] But can grow solitary on agar media , thus cold facultative parasite .

Only one family , Dimargaritaceae , exists, containing three genera : Dimargaritaceae Dimargaris - 7 spp.

Dispira - 4 spp.

Tieghemiomyces - 2 spp.
