Originally established in 1950 as Richards Rosen Press, the company is headquartered in New York City . In 1982, it changed its name to Rosen Publishing Group.

[ 1 ] The company publishes titles for the school market under the Britannica Educational Publishing imprint as of 2017.

[ 2 ] Imprints [ edit ] Rosen Publishing has acquired several publishers, incorporating them as imprints : Gareth Stevens in 2009; [ 3 ] Cavendish Square in 2013; [ 4 ] Enslow Publishing in 2014; [ 5 ] Jackdaw Publications in 2015; [ 6 ] and Greenhaven Press, Lucent Books, and KidHaven Press in 2016.

[ 7 ] Rosen Teen Health & Wellness [ edit ] Rosen Teen Health & Wellness is an database providing material aimed at teenagers, covering topics such as gender identity , female genital mutilation , stalking , human trafficking , and various other issues. It was launched in 2007.
