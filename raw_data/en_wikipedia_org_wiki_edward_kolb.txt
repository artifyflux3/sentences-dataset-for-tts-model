Kolb , known as Rocky Kolb , (born October 2, 1951) is a cosmologist and a professor at the University of Chicago as well as the dean of Physical Sciences . He has worked on many aspects of the Big Bang cosmology, including baryogenesis , nucleosynthesis and dark matter . He is author, with Michael Turner , of the popular textbook The Early Universe (Addison-Wesley, 1990). Additionally, alongside his co-author Michael Turner, Kolb was awarded the 2010 Dannie Heineman Prize for Astrophysics .

[ 3 ] Kolb's collaborators also include Stephen Wolfram [ 4 ] and Richard Slansky .

[ 5 ] Doctor Kolb is married to Adrienne Kolb , a historian of science , [ 6 ] and has three children.
