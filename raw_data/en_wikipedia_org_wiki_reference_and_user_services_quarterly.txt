37395409 Links Journal homepage Reference and User Services Quarterly is a quarterly peer-reviewed academic journal covering library science . It is the official journal of the Reference and User Services Association and is published by the American Library Association . The journal was established as the Reference Quarterly in 1961 under the editorship of William Katz .

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] A silver anniversary series appeared in 1986.

[ 3 ] [ 4 ] The journal was retitled and obtained its current name in 1997.

In 1985 the journal appeared twice in the set design for the movie, Back to the Future , next to the bed of Marty McFly .

[ 5 ] The journal is available electronically via EBSCO , Academic OneFile , and WilsonWeb , as well as its own website.
