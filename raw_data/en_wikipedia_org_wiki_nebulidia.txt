2022 Class: Nebulidea Tikhonenkov et al.

2022 Order: Nebulidida Tikhonenkov et al.

2022 Family: Nebulidae Tikhonenkov et al.

2022 Genera Nebulomonas Ancoracysta Nebulidia is a phylum of predatory microbial eukaryotes in the supergroup Provora . Known as nebulids , the group contains the class Nebulidea , order Nebulidida , family Nebulidae , and genera Nebulomonas and Ancoracysta .
