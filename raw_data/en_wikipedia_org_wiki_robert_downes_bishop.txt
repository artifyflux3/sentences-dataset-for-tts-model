[ 1 ] Downes was the son of an Anglican bishop, Henry Downes . He was educated at Merton College, Oxford .

[ 2 ] He held incumbencies at Balteagh , Desertmartin and Kilcronaghan and was appointed Prebendary of Comber in 1734.

[ 3 ] He was Dean of Derry from 1740 until 1744; Bishop of Ferns and Leighlin from 1744 until 1752; [ 4 ] Down and Connor from 1752 until 1753 [ 5 ] and Raphoe from 1753 until his death on 20 June 1763.
