Formerly known as the Redfern Land Council, the organisation was established to facilitate developments in land rights cases following the passing of the Aboriginal Lands Right Act (1983) .

[ 1 ] A public artwork, entitled Gadigal Mural, reaching over 25 metres long and 17 meters high across the back of William House , was produced in consultation with the council.
