Bhattacharya Genomics [ edit ] Plastid genomes of Proteorhodophytina are large, highly diversified, and intron -rich [ 1 ] which contrasts with the generally compact and slow-evolving plastid genomes of other rhodophytes .

[ 2 ] Mitochondrial genomes of Proteorhodophytina are also larger than in other groups of red algae.
