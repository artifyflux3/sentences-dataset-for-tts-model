The creation of Species 2000 was initiated by Frank Bisby and colleagues at the University of Reading in the UK in 1997 [ 1 ] [ 2 ] and the Catalogue of Life was first published in 2001.

[ 3 ] While administrators and member organizations of Species 2000 are located around the world, the secretariat is located at the Naturalis Biodiversity Center in Leiden , Netherlands .
