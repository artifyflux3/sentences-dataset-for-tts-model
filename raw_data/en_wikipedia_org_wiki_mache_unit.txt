[ 1 ] It was defined as the quantity of radon (ignoring its daughter isotopes ; in practice, mostly radon-222) per litre of air which ionises a sustained current of 0.001 esu (0.001 StatAmpere ).

1 ME = 3.64 Eman = 3.64×10 −10 Ci /L = 13.4545 Bq /L.
