[ 1 ] The family contains a single genus, Alphaovalivirus , which contains a single species, Sulfolobus ellipsoid virus 1 ( Alphaovalivirus fumarolicaense ).

[ 2 ] The linear genome of dsDNA is 23,219 bp with 172 bp inverted terminal repeats. Sulfolobus ellipsoid virus 1 was isolated from an acidic hot spring (86−106oC, pH 2.2−2.5) in Laguna Fumarólica , [ 3 ] Costa Rica ; the only known host is Sulfolobus sp. A20.
