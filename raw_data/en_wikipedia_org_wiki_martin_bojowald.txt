[ 2 ] Prior to joining Penn State he spent several years at the Max Planck Institute for Gravitational Physics [ 3 ] in Potsdam , Germany . He works on loop quantum gravity and physical cosmology and is credited with establishing the sub-field of loop quantum cosmology .
