Chemical compound

**Octatetraynyl radical** (C 8 H) is an [organic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Organic_molecule "Organic molecule")[free radical](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Free_radical "Free radical") with eight [carbon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carbon "Carbon") atoms linked in a [linear chain](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Linear_molecular_geometry "Linear molecular geometry") with alternating [single bonds](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Single_bond "Single bond") and [triple bonds](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Triple_bond "Triple bond") (H−C≡C−C≡C−C≡C−C≡C•).

In 2007 negatively charged octatetraynyl was detected in Galactic molecular source [TMC-1](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/TMC-1 "TMC-1"), making it the second type of [anion](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anion "Anion") to be found in the [interstellar medium](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Interstellar_medium "Interstellar medium") (after [hexatriynyl radical](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hexatriynyl_radical "Hexatriynyl radical")) and the largest such molecule detected to date.[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Octatetraynyl_radical#cite_note-1)[[2]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Octatetraynyl_radical#cite_note-2)
