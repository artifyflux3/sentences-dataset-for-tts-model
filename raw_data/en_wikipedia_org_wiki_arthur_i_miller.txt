Miller is Emeritus Professor of History and Philosophy of Science at University College London .

[ 1 ] He took a PhD in physics at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology . From 1991 to 2005 he was Professor of History and Philosophy of Science at University College London (UCL). At UCL, Professor Miller helped restructure an academic unit combining history and philosophy of science , sociology of science , and science communication to create UCL Department of Science and Technology Studies , renamed in 1994. He was instrumental in developing the UK's first undergraduate single honours BSc degree in History and Philosophy of Science, at UCL, launched in 1993.
