[ 2 ] [ 3 ] It is hosted by the Center for Open Science .

[ 4 ] The preprint service was inspired by the arXiv repository. The service allows researchers to upload manuscripts regarding psychology and related fields prior to peer review.

[ 5 ] As of April 2017, it is indexed by Google Scholar .
