01481728 Links Journal homepage Online access Online archive Applied Optics is a peer-reviewed scientific journal published by Optica three times a month. It was established in 1962 with John N. Howard as founding editor-in-chief and Patricia Wakeling as managing editor. Wakeling worked on the journal for over three decades. The editor-in-chief is Gisele Bennett .

[ 1 ] The journal covers all aspects of optics , photonics , imaging , and sensing . According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2023 impact factor of 1.7.
