Erikss. & Winka 1997 [ 1 ] [ 2 ] Orders Schizosaccharomycetales Schizosaccharomycetes is a class in the kingdom of fungi . It contains the order Schizosaccharomycetales , the fission yeasts . The genus Schizosaccharomyces is a broad and ancient clade within Ascomycota including five known fission yeast : Schizosaccharomyces pombe , Schizosaccharomyces japonicius , Schizosaccharomyces octosporus , and Schizosaccharomyces cryophilus , and Schizosaccharomyces osmophilus .
