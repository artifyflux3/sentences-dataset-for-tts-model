173734101 Links Journal homepage Online archive Evolutionary Ecology is a bimonthly peer-reviewed scientific journal covering the study of ecology from an evolutionary perspective. It was established in 1987 and is published by Springer Science+Business Media . The editor-in-chief is Matthew Symonds ( Deakin University ). According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2017 impact factor of 2.133.
