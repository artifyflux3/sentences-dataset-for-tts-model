Chemical compound

**Methoxyethane**, also known as **ethyl methyl ether**, is a colorless gaseous [ether](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ether "Ether") with the formula CH 3 OCH 2 CH 3. Unlike the related [dimethyl ether](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dimethyl_ether "Dimethyl ether") and [diethyl ether](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diethyl_ether "Diethyl ether"), which are widely used and studied, this mixed alkyl ether has no current applications. It is a [structural isomer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Structural_isomer "Structural isomer") of [isopropyl alcohol](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Isopropyl_alcohol "Isopropyl alcohol"). Its utility as an anesthetic[[3]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Methoxyethane#cite_note-3) and solvent[[4]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Methoxyethane#cite_note-4) have been investigated.
