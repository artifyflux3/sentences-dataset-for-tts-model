N verify ( what is Y N ?) Infobox references Chemical compound Methoxyethane , also known as ethyl methyl ether , is a colorless gaseous ether with the formula CH 3 OCH 2 CH 3 . Unlike the related dimethyl ether and diethyl ether , which are widely used and studied, this mixed alkyl ether has no current applications. It is a structural isomer of isopropyl alcohol . Its utility as an anesthetic [ 3 ] and solvent [ 4 ] have been investigated.
