[ 1 ] It belongs to the subphylum Pezizomycotina in the phylum Ascomycota .

[ 2 ] The asci ( spore -bearing cells) of the Lecanoromycetes most often release spores by rostrate dehiscence .

[ 1 ] The group is monophyletic (composed only of all the descendants of a common ancestor).

[ 3 ] Genera of uncertain placement [ edit ] There are several genera in the Lecanoromycetes that have not been placed into any order or family. These are: [ 4 ] Argopsis Th.Fr. (1857) – 1 sp.
