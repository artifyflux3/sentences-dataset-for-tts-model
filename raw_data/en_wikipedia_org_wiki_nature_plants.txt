964863802 Links Journal homepage Online access Online archive Nature Plants is a monthly peer-reviewed online-only scientific journal covering all aspects of plants and plant biology . It was established in 2015 and is published by Nature Publishing Group .  The editor-in-chief is Chris Surridge. According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2024 impact factor of 13.2.
