Scientific classification Domain: Eukaryota Clade : Sar Clade : Rhizaria Phylum: Endomyxa Class: Phytomyxea Order: Phagomyxida Cavalier-Smith, 1993 Family: Phagomyxidae Cavalier-Smith, 1993 Genera Phagomyxa Maullinia Phagomyxids are a group of obligate endoparasitic protists belonging to the subphylum Endomyxa in Cercozoa .

[ 1 ] Taxonomically, they are united under a single family Phagomyxidae , order Phagomyxida , sister to the plasmodiophores .

[ 2 ] Ecology [ edit ] Phagomyxids are mainly parasites of brown algae , [ 3 ] but some can parasite oomycetes .

[ 4 ] Taxonomy [ edit ] The group was created in 1993 by Cavalier-Smith , when it contained only the genus Phagomyxa .

[ 2 ] Since then, Maullinia , a genus previously in Plasmodiophoridae , has joined the phagomyxids.
