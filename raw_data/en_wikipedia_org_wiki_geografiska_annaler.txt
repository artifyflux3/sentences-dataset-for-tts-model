Indexing CODEN ( alt · alt2 ) · JSTOR ( alt ) · LCCN ( alt ) MIAR · NLM ( alt ) · Scopus · W&L ISSN 1651-3215 JSTOR 16513215 Links Journal homepage Geografiska Annaler is a scientific journal published by the Swedish Society for Anthropology and Geography in Stockholm, Sweden . The journal is founded in 1919. Since 1965 the journal is published in two series A and B. Series A deals with arctic research, physical geography , glaciology and quaternary science in general. Series B covers the topics of human geography and economic geography , with a special, but not exclusive, focus on the Nordic and Baltic countries .
