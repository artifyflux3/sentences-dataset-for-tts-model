Find sources: "Tethersonde" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( February 2018 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) A tethersonde is a type of radiosonde used in atmospheric science for atmospheric sounding . Tethersondes are attached to fixed or tethered balloons via a separate tether and can be moved up and down the tether to obtain multiple atmospheric readings.
