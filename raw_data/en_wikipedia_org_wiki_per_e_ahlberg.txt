He took his Ph.D. in zoology at the University of Cambridge in 1989 under English palaeontologist Jenny Clack .

[ 1 ] He is currently professor at the Department of 
Organismal Biology, University of Uppsala .

[ 2 ] He has collaborated with Clack on a number of projects.

[ 3 ] He was elected to the Royal Swedish Academy of Sciences early in 2012.
