Range of goldfields [ edit ] There are goldfields across the state of Western Australia that area included in the range of Mineral fields of Western Australia , from the Kimberley region to the south coast .

Designated goldfields in Western Australia have included: Mid West region [ edit ] Ashburton Goldfield [ 1 ] East Murchison Goldfield [ 2 ] Gascoyne Goldfield [ 3 ] Murchison Goldfield [ 4 ] Goldfields region [ edit ] Broad Arrow Goldfield Coolgardie Goldfield East Coolgardie Goldfield North East Coolgardie Goldfield North Coolgardie Goldfield Yilgarn Goldfield Goldfields to Eastern Goldfields [ edit ] In the 1890s the goldfields term was used for country between Southern Cross and Coolgardie , however as the gold fields extended to Kalgoorlie and beyond, the term Eastern Goldfields was used for the locations in vicinity of Kalgoorlie at that stage.

[ 5 ] [ 6 ] Sometimes West Australian Goldfields , even the goldfields or Eastern Goldfields is a term that has been used to either identify the region surrounding Kalgoorlie-Boulder within the current broader designated region of Goldfields-Esperance in Western Australia .

The term has been used in books specifically referring to the Kalgoorlie region.

[ 7 ] The term has been used in government reports as a reference for all of the goldfields found in Western Australia.

[ 8 ] The term has been used in company names.

[ 9 ] Kimberley goldfield [ edit ] The Kimberley goldfield was proclaimed in 1886.

[ 10 ] [ 11 ] The Kimberley goldfield was relatively short lived, mainly the rush period of the 1880s and 1890s being the notable era on the field.
