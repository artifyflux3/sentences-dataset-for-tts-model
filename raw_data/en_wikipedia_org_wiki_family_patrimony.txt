( April 2023 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) Family patrimony is a type of civil law patrimony that is created by marriage or civil union (where recognized) which creates a bundle of entitlements and obligations that must be shared by the spouses or partners upon divorce , annulment , dissolution of marriage or dissolution of civil union , when there must be a division of property . It is similar to the common law concept of community property .
