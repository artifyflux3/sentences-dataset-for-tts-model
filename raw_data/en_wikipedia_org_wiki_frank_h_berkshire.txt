Berkshire is a British mathematician, an expert on fluid dynamics , biomechanics , and the mathematics of gambling .

[ 1 ] He is a coauthor of the textbook Classical Mechanics .

[ 2 ] [ 3 ] Education and career [ edit ] Berkshire is an alumnus of St John's College, Cambridge .

[ 4 ] He joined the faculty in the department of mathematics at Imperial College London in 1967. There he became a senior lecturer, director of undergraduate studies since 1987, and teaching fellow in 1996.

[ 4 ] In 2000, he won Imperial College's Rector's Medal for Outstanding Contribution to Teaching Excellence.

[ 5 ] He retired in 2011 and remains principal teaching fellow in dynamics at Imperial.

[ 6 ] Textbook [ edit ] In 1997, Berkshire became a co-author on the 4th edition of Tom Kibble 's textbook Classical Mechanics .

[ 2 ] The fifth edition was published by the Imperial College Press in 2004.
