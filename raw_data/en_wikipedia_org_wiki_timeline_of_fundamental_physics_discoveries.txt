From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This timeline lists significant discoveries in [physics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Physics "Physics") and the laws of nature, including experimental discoveries, theoretical proposals that were confirmed experimentally, and theories that have significantly influenced current thinking in modern physics. Such discoveries are often a multi-step, multi-person process. [Multiple discovery](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Multiple_discovery "Multiple discovery") sometimes occurs when multiple research groups discover the same phenomenon at about the same time, and [scientific priority](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scientific_priority "Scientific priority") is often disputed. The listings below include some of the most significant people and ideas by date of publication or experiment.

*   624–546 BCE – [Thales of Miletus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thales_of_Miletus "Thales of Miletus"): Introduced [natural philosophy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Natural_philosophy "Natural philosophy")
*   610–546 BCE – [Anaximander](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anaximander "Anaximander"): Concept of Earth floating in [space](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Outer_space "Outer space")[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_fundamental_physics_discoveries#cite_note-1)
*   460–370 BCE – [Democritus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Democritus "Democritus"): [Atomism](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Atomism "Atomism") via [thought experiment](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thought_experiment "Thought experiment")
*   384–322 BCE – [Aristotle](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aristotle "Aristotle"): [Aristotelian physics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aristotelian_physics "Aristotelian physics"), earliest [effective theory](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Effective_theory "Effective theory") of physics[[2]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_fundamental_physics_discoveries#cite_note-Rovelli2015-2)
*   c. 300 BCE – [Euclid](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Euclid "Euclid"): [Euclidean geometry](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Euclidean_geometry "Euclidean geometry")
*   c. 250 BCE – [Archimedes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Archimedes "Archimedes"): [Archimedes' principle](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Archimedes%27_principle "Archimedes' principle")
*   310–230 BCE – [Aristarchos](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aristarchos_of_Samos "Aristarchos of Samos"): Proposed [heliocentricism](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Heliocentrism "Heliocentrism")[[3]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_fundamental_physics_discoveries#cite_note-3)
*   276–194 BCE – [Eratosthenes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eratosthenes "Eratosthenes"): [Circumference of the Earth](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Earth%27s_circumference "Earth's circumference") measured
*   190–150 BCE – [Seleucus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seleucus_of_Seleucia "Seleucus of Seleucia"): Support of [heliocentrism](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Heliocentrism "Heliocentrism") based on reasoning[[4]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_fundamental_physics_discoveries#cite_note-4)
*   220–150 BCE – [Apollonius](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apollonius_of_Perga "Apollonius of Perga"): and [Hipparchus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hipparchus "Hipparchus"): Invention of [Astrolabe](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Astrolabe "Astrolabe")
*   205–86 BCE – Hipparchus or unknown: [Antikythera mechanism](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antikythera_mechanism "Antikythera mechanism") an [analog computer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Analog_computer "Analog computer") of planetary motions
*   129 BCE – Hipparchus: [Hipparchus star catalog](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hipparchus_star_catalog "Hipparchus star catalog") of the entire sky[[5]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_fundamental_physics_discoveries#cite_note-5) and [precession of the equinoxes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Precession_of_the_equinoxes "Precession of the equinoxes")
*   60 CE – [Hero of Alexandria](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hero_of_Alexandria "Hero of Alexandria"): [Catoptrics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Catoptrics "Catoptrics"): Hero's [principle of the shortest path of light](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fermat%27s_principle#History "Fermat's principle")[[6]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_fundamental_physics_discoveries#cite_note-6)
*   c.150 CE – [Ptolemy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ptolemy "Ptolemy"): Ptolomaic model standardized [geocentricism](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Geocentrism#Ptolemaic_model "Geocentrism")

*   500 CE – [John Philoponus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Philoponus "John Philoponus"): [Theory of impetus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Theory_of_impetus "Theory of impetus")
*   984 CE – [Ibn Sahl](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ibn_Sahl_(mathematician) "Ibn Sahl (mathematician)"): Law of [refraction](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Refraction "Refraction")
*   1010 – [Ibn al-Haytham](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ibn_al-Haytham "Ibn al-Haytham") (Alhazen): [Optics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Optics "Optics"), finite [speed of light](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Speed_of_light "Speed of light")
*   c. 1030 – Ibn Sina ([Avicenna](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Avicenna "Avicenna")): Concept of [force](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Force "Force")
*   c. 1050 – [al-Biruni](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Al-Biruni "Al-Biruni"): Speed of light is much larger than [speed of sound](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Speed_of_sound "Speed of sound")
*   c. 1100 – [Al-Baghdadi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Abu%27l-Barak%C4%81t_al-Baghd%C4%81d%C4%AB "Abu'l-Barakāt al-Baghdādī"): Theory of motion with distinction between [velocity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Velocity "Velocity") and [acceleration](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Acceleration "Acceleration")[[7]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_fundamental_physics_discoveries#cite_note-7)

*   1514 – [Nicolaus Copernicus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nicolaus_Copernicus "Nicolaus Copernicus"): [Heliocentrism](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Heliocentrism "Heliocentrism")
*   1586 – [Simon Stevin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Simon_Stevin "Simon Stevin"): [Delft tower experiment](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Delft_tower_experiment "Delft tower experiment")

*   1608 – Earliest known [telescopes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Telescope "Telescope")
*   1609, 1619 – [Kepler](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kepler "Kepler"): [Kepler's laws of planetary motion](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kepler%27s_laws_of_planetary_motion "Kepler's laws of planetary motion")
*   1610 – [Galileo Galilei](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Galileo_Galilei "Galileo Galilei"): discovered the [Galilean moons](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Galilean_moons "Galilean moons") of Jupiter
*   1613 – [Galileo Galilei](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Galileo_Galilei "Galileo Galilei"): [Inertia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inertia "Inertia")
*   1621 – [Willebrord Snellius](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Willebrord_Snellius "Willebrord Snellius"): [Snell's law](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Snell%27s_law "Snell's law")
*   1632 – [Galileo Galilei](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Galileo_Galilei "Galileo Galilei"): [The Galilean principle](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Galilean_invariance "Galilean invariance") (the laws of motion are the same in all [inertial frames](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inertial_frames "Inertial frames"))
*   1660 – [Blaise Pascal](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blaise_Pascal "Blaise Pascal"): [Pascal's law](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pascal%27s_law "Pascal's law")
*   1660 – [Robert Hooke](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_Hooke "Robert Hooke"): [Hooke's law](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hooke%27s_law "Hooke's law")
*   1662 – [Robert Boyle](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_Boyle "Robert Boyle"): [Boyle's law](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boyle%27s_law "Boyle's law")
*   1663 – [Otto von Guericke](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Otto_von_Guericke "Otto von Guericke"): first [electrostatic generator](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Electrostatic_generator "Electrostatic generator")
*   1676 – [Ole Rømer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ole_R%C3%B8mer "Ole Rømer"): [Rømer's determination of the speed of light](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/R%C3%B8mer%27s_determination_of_the_speed_of_light "Rømer's determination of the speed of light") traveling from the moons of Jupiter.
*   1678 – [Christiaan Huygens](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Christiaan_Huygens "Christiaan Huygens") mathematical [wave theory of light](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wave_theory_of_light "Wave theory of light"), published in his _[Treatise on Light](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Treatise\_on\_Light "Treatise on Light")_
*   1687 – [Isaac Newton](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Isaac_Newton "Isaac Newton"): [Newton's laws of motion](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Newton%27s_laws_of_motion "Newton's laws of motion"), and [Newton's law of universal gravitation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Newton%27s_law_of_universal_gravitation "Newton's law of universal gravitation")[[8]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_fundamental_physics_discoveries#cite_note-8)

*   1738 – [Daniel Bernoulli](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Daniel_Bernoulli "Daniel Bernoulli"): First model of the [kinetic theory of gases](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kinetic_theory_of_gases "Kinetic theory of gases")
*   1745–46 – [Ewald Georg von Kleist](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ewald_Georg_von_Kleist "Ewald Georg von Kleist") and [Pieter van Musschenbroek](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pieter_van_Musschenbroek "Pieter van Musschenbroek"): discovery of the [Leyden jar](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leyden_jar "Leyden jar")
*   1752 – [Benjamin Franklin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Benjamin_Franklin "Benjamin Franklin"): [kite experiment](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kite_experiment "Kite experiment")
*   1760 – [Joseph-Louis Lagrange](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Joseph-Louis_Lagrange "Joseph-Louis Lagrange"): [Lagrangian mechanics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lagrangian_mechanics "Lagrangian mechanics")
*   1782 – [Antoine Lavoisier](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antoine_Lavoisier "Antoine Lavoisier"): [conservation of mass](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Conservation_of_mass "Conservation of mass")
*   1785 – [Charles-Augustin de Coulomb](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Charles-Augustin_de_Coulomb "Charles-Augustin de Coulomb"): [Coulomb's inverse-square law](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coulomb%27s_inverse-square_law "Coulomb's inverse-square law") for electric charges confirmed[[9]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_fundamental_physics_discoveries#cite_note-9)
*   1800 – [Alessandro Volta](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alessandro_Volta "Alessandro Volta"): discovery of [voltaic pile](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Voltaic_pile "Voltaic pile")

*   1800 - [William Herschel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/William_Herschel "William Herschel"): [Infrared light](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Infrared_light "Infrared light")
*   1801 – [Thomas Young](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thomas_Young_(scientist) "Thomas Young (scientist)"): [Wave theory of light](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wave_theory_of_light "Wave theory of light")
*   1801 - [Johann Wilhelm Ritter](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Johann_Wilhelm_Ritter "Johann Wilhelm Ritter"): [Ultraviolet light](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ultraviolet_light "Ultraviolet light")
*   1803 – [John Dalton](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Dalton "John Dalton"): [Atomic theory](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Atomic_theory "Atomic theory") of matter[[10]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_fundamental_physics_discoveries#cite_note-10)
*   1806 – [Thomas Young](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thomas_Young_(scientist) "Thomas Young (scientist)"): [Kinetic energy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kinetic_energy "Kinetic energy")
*   1814 – [Augustin-Jean Fresnel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Augustin-Jean_Fresnel "Augustin-Jean Fresnel"): [Wave theory of light](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wave_theory_of_light "Wave theory of light"), [optical interference](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Optical_interference "Optical interference")
*   1820 – [André-Marie Ampère](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andr%C3%A9-Marie_Amp%C3%A8re "André-Marie Ampère"), [Jean-Baptiste Biot](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jean-Baptiste_Biot "Jean-Baptiste Biot"), and [Félix Savart](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/F%C3%A9lix_Savart "Félix Savart"): Evidence for [electromagnetic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Electromagnetic "Electromagnetic") interactions ([Biot–Savart law](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Biot%E2%80%93Savart_law "Biot–Savart law"))
*   1822 – [Joseph Fourier](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Joseph_Fourier "Joseph Fourier"): [Heat equation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Heat_equation "Heat equation")
*   1824 – [Nicolas Léonard Sadi Carnot](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nicolas_L%C3%A9onard_Sadi_Carnot "Nicolas Léonard Sadi Carnot"): [Ideal gas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ideal_gas "Ideal gas") cycle analysis ([Carnot cycle](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carnot_cycle "Carnot cycle")), [internal combustion engine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Internal_combustion_engine "Internal combustion engine")
*   1826 – [Ampère's circuital law](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amp%C3%A8re%27s_circuital_law "Ampère's circuital law")
*   1827 – [Georg Ohm](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Georg_Ohm "Georg Ohm"): [Electrical resistance](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Electrical_resistance "Electrical resistance")
*   1831 – [Michael Faraday](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Michael_Faraday "Michael Faraday"): [Faraday's law of induction](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Faraday%27s_law_of_induction "Faraday's law of induction")
*   1833 – [William Rowan Hamilton](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/William_Rowan_Hamilton "William Rowan Hamilton"): [Hamiltonian mechanics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hamiltonian_mechanics "Hamiltonian mechanics")
*   1838 – [Michael Faraday](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Michael_Faraday "Michael Faraday"): [Lines of force](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lines_of_force "Lines of force")
*   1838 – [Wilhelm Eduard Weber](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wilhelm_Eduard_Weber "Wilhelm Eduard Weber") and [Carl Friedrich Gauss](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carl_Friedrich_Gauss "Carl Friedrich Gauss"): [Earth's magnetic field](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Earth%27s_magnetic_field "Earth's magnetic field")[_[clarification needed](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Please\_clarify "Wikipedia:Please clarify")_]
*   1842–43 – [William Thomson, 1st Baron Kelvin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/William_Thomson,_1st_Baron_Kelvin "William Thomson, 1st Baron Kelvin") and [Julius von Mayer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Julius_von_Mayer "Julius von Mayer"): [Conservation of energy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Conservation_of_energy "Conservation of energy")
*   1842 – [Christian Doppler](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Christian_Doppler "Christian Doppler"): [Doppler effect](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Doppler_effect "Doppler effect")
*   1845 – [Michael Faraday](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Michael_Faraday "Michael Faraday"): [Faraday rotation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Faraday_rotation "Faraday rotation") (interaction of light and magnetic field)
*   1847 – [Hermann von Helmholtz](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hermann_von_Helmholtz "Hermann von Helmholtz")&[James Prescott Joule](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/James_Prescott_Joule "James Prescott Joule"): Conservation of Energy 2[_[clarification needed](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Please\_clarify "Wikipedia:Please clarify")_]
*   1850–51 – [William Thomson, 1st Baron Kelvin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/William_Thomson,_1st_Baron_Kelvin "William Thomson, 1st Baron Kelvin")&[Rudolf Clausius](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rudolf_Clausius "Rudolf Clausius"): [Second law of thermodynamics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Second_law_of_thermodynamics "Second law of thermodynamics")
*   1857 – [Rudolf Clausius](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rudolf_Clausius "Rudolf Clausius"): Introduced translational, rotational, and vibrational molecular motions
*   1857 – [Rudolf Clausius](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rudolf_Clausius "Rudolf Clausius"): Introduced the concept of [mean free path](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mean_free_path "Mean free path")
*   1860 – [James Clerk Maxwell](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/James_Clerk_Maxwell "James Clerk Maxwell"): Introduced [statistical mechanics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Statistical_mechanics "Statistical mechanics") with the [Maxwell distribution](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maxwell%E2%80%93Boltzmann_distribution "Maxwell–Boltzmann distribution")
*   1861 – [Gustav Kirchhoff](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gustav_Kirchhoff "Gustav Kirchhoff"): [Black body](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Black_body "Black body")
*   1861–62 – [Maxwell's equations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maxwell%27s_equations "Maxwell's equations")
*   1863 – [Rudolf Clausius](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rudolf_Clausius "Rudolf Clausius"): [Entropy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Entropy "Entropy")
*   1864 – [James Clerk Maxwell](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/James_Clerk_Maxwell "James Clerk Maxwell"): _[A Dynamical Theory of the Electromagnetic Field](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/A\_Dynamical\_Theory\_of\_the\_Electromagnetic\_Field "A Dynamical Theory of the Electromagnetic Field")_ ([electromagnetic radiation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Electromagnetic_radiation "Electromagnetic radiation"))
*   1867 – [James Clerk Maxwell](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/James_Clerk_Maxwell "James Clerk Maxwell"): _On the Dynamical Theory of Gases_ ([kinetic theory of gases](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kinetic_theory_of_gases "Kinetic theory of gases"))
*   1871–89 – [Ludwig Boltzmann](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ludwig_Boltzmann "Ludwig Boltzmann")&[Josiah Willard Gibbs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Josiah_Willard_Gibbs "Josiah Willard Gibbs"): [Statistical mechanics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Statistical_mechanics "Statistical mechanics") ([Boltzmann equation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boltzmann_equation "Boltzmann equation"), 1872)
*   1873 – Maxwell: _[A Treatise on Electricity and Magnetism](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/A\_Treatise\_on\_Electricity\_and\_Magnetism "A Treatise on Electricity and Magnetism")_
*   1884 – Boltzmann derives [Stefan radiation law](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stefan%E2%80%93Boltzmann_law "Stefan–Boltzmann law")[[11]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_fundamental_physics_discoveries#cite_note-11)
*   1887 – [Michelson–Morley experiment](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Michelson%E2%80%93Morley_experiment "Michelson–Morley experiment")
*   1887 – [Heinrich Rudolf Hertz](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Heinrich_Rudolf_Hertz "Heinrich Rudolf Hertz"): [Electromagnetic waves](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Electromagnetic_radiation "Electromagnetic radiation")
*   1888 – [Johannes Rydberg](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Johannes_Rydberg "Johannes Rydberg"): [Rydberg formula](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rydberg_formula "Rydberg formula")
*   1889, 1892 – [Lorentz-FitzGerald contraction](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lorentz_ether_theory#Length_contraction "Lorentz ether theory")
*   1893 – [Wilhelm Wien](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wilhelm_Wien "Wilhelm Wien"): [Wien's displacement law](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wien%27s_displacement_law "Wien's displacement law") for black-body radiation
*   1895 – [Wilhelm Röntgen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wilhelm_R%C3%B6ntgen "Wilhelm Röntgen"): [X-rays](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/X-ray "X-ray")
*   1896 – [Henri Becquerel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Henri_Becquerel "Henri Becquerel"): [Radioactivity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Radioactivity "Radioactivity")
*   1896 – [Pieter Zeeman](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pieter_Zeeman "Pieter Zeeman"): [Zeeman effect](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zeeman_effect "Zeeman effect")
*   1897 – [J. J. Thomson](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/J._J._Thomson "J. J. Thomson"): [Electron](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Electron "Electron") discovered
*   1900 – [Max Planck](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Max_Planck "Max Planck"): Formula for [black-body radiation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Black-body_radiation "Black-body radiation") – the [quanta](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quantum "Quantum") solution to radiation [ultraviolet catastrophe](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ultraviolet_catastrophe "Ultraviolet catastrophe")
*   1900 - [Paul Villard](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paul_Villard "Paul Villard"): [Gamma rays](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gamma_rays "Gamma rays")

*   1904 – [J. J. Thomson](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/J._J._Thomson "J. J. Thomson")'s [plum pudding model](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Plum_pudding_model "Plum pudding model") of the atom 1904
*   1905 – [Albert Einstein](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Albert_Einstein "Albert Einstein"): [Special relativity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Special_relativity "Special relativity"), proposes light quantum (later named [photon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Photon "Photon")) to explain the [photoelectric effect](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Photoelectric_effect "Photoelectric effect"), [Brownian motion](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brownian_motion "Brownian motion"), [Mass–energy equivalence](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mass%E2%80%93energy_equivalence "Mass–energy equivalence")
*   1908 – [Hermann Minkowski](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hermann_Minkowski "Hermann Minkowski"): [Minkowski space](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minkowski_space "Minkowski space")
*   1911 – [Ernest Rutherford](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ernest_Rutherford "Ernest Rutherford"): Discovery of the [atomic nucleus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Atomic_nucleus "Atomic nucleus") ([Rutherford model](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rutherford_model "Rutherford model"))
*   1911 – [Kamerlingh Onnes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Heike_Kamerlingh_Onnes "Heike Kamerlingh Onnes"): [Superconductivity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Superconductivity "Superconductivity")
*   1912 - [Victor Francis Hess](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Victor_Francis_Hess "Victor Francis Hess"): [Cosmic rays](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cosmic_rays "Cosmic rays")
*   1913 – [Niels Bohr](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Niels_Bohr "Niels Bohr"): [Bohr model](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bohr_model "Bohr model") of the atom
*   1915 – [Albert Einstein](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Albert_Einstein "Albert Einstein"): [General relativity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/General_relativity "General relativity")
*   1915 – [Emmy Noether](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Emmy_Noether "Emmy Noether"): [Noether's theorem](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Noether%27s_theorem "Noether's theorem") relates [symmetries](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Symmetry_in_physics "Symmetry in physics") to [conservation laws](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Conservation_law "Conservation law").
*   1916 – [Schwarzschild metric](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Schwarzschild_metric "Schwarzschild metric") modeling gravity outside a large sphere
*   1917 - [Ernest Rutherford](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ernest_Rutherford "Ernest Rutherford"): [Proton](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Proton "Proton") proved
*   1919 – [Arthur Eddington](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arthur_Eddington "Arthur Eddington"):[Light bending confirmed](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tests_of_general_relativity#Deflection_of_light_by_the_Sun "Tests of general relativity") – evidence for general relativity
*   1919–1926 – [Kaluza–Klein theory](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kaluza%E2%80%93Klein_theory "Kaluza–Klein theory") proposing unification of gravity and electromagnetism
*   1922 – [Alexander Friedmann](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alexander_Friedmann "Alexander Friedmann") proposes expanding universe
*   1922–37 – [Friedmann–Lemaître–Robertson–Walker metric](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Friedmann%E2%80%93Lema%C3%AEtre%E2%80%93Robertson%E2%80%93Walker_metric "Friedmann–Lemaître–Robertson–Walker metric") cosmological model
*   1923 – [Stern–Gerlach experiment](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stern%E2%80%93Gerlach_experiment "Stern–Gerlach experiment")
*   1923 – [Edwin Hubble](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edwin_Hubble "Edwin Hubble"): [Galaxies](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Galaxy "Galaxy") discovered
*   1923 – [Arthur Compton](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arthur_Compton "Arthur Compton"): Particle nature of photons confirmed by observation of [photon momentum](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Photon_momentum "Photon momentum")
*   1924 – [Bose–Einstein statistics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bose%E2%80%93Einstein_statistics "Bose–Einstein statistics")
*   1924 – [Louis de Broglie](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Louis_de_Broglie "Louis de Broglie"): [De Broglie wave](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Matter_wave "Matter wave")
*   1925 – [Werner Heisenberg](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Werner_Heisenberg "Werner Heisenberg"): [Matrix mechanics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Matrix_mechanics "Matrix mechanics")
*   1925–27 – [Niels Bohr](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Niels_Bohr "Niels Bohr")&[Max Planck](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Max_Planck "Max Planck"): [Quantum mechanics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quantum_mechanics "Quantum mechanics")
*   1925 – [Stellar structure](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stellar_structure "Stellar structure") understood[[12]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_fundamental_physics_discoveries#cite_note-12)[[13]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_fundamental_physics_discoveries#cite_note-13)
*   1926 – [Fermi-Dirac Statistics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fermi%E2%80%93Dirac_statistics "Fermi–Dirac statistics")
*   1926 – [Erwin Schrödinger](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Erwin_Schr%C3%B6dinger "Erwin Schrödinger"): [Schrödinger Equation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Schr%C3%B6dinger_Equation "Schrödinger Equation")
*   1927 – [Werner Heisenberg](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Werner_Heisenberg "Werner Heisenberg"): [Uncertainty principle](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Uncertainty_principle "Uncertainty principle")
*   1927 – [Georges Lemaître](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Georges_Lema%C3%AEtre "Georges Lemaître"): [Big Bang](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Big_Bang "Big Bang")
*   1927 – [Paul Dirac](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paul_Dirac "Paul Dirac"): [Dirac equation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dirac_equation "Dirac equation")
*   1927 – [Max Born](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Max_Born "Max Born"): [Born rule](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Born_rule "Born rule")
*   1928 – [Paul Dirac](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paul_Dirac "Paul Dirac") proposes the [antiparticle](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antiparticle "Antiparticle")
*   1929 – [Edwin Hubble](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edwin_Hubble "Edwin Hubble"): [Expansion of the universe](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Metric_expansion_of_space "Metric expansion of space") confirmed
*   1932 – [Carl David Anderson](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carl_David_Anderson "Carl David Anderson"): [Antimatter](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antimatter "Antimatter") ([positrons](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Positrons "Positrons")) discovered
*   1932 – [James Chadwick](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/James_Chadwick "James Chadwick"): [Neutron](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Neutron "Neutron") discovered
*   1933 – [Ernst Ruska](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ernst_Ruska "Ernst Ruska"): Invention of the [electron microscope](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Electron_microscope "Electron microscope")
*   1935 – [Subrahmanyan Chandrasekhar](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Subrahmanyan_Chandrasekhar "Subrahmanyan Chandrasekhar"): [Chandrasekhar limit](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chandrasekhar_limit "Chandrasekhar limit") for black hole collapse
*   1937 - [Majorana particle](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Majorana_particle "Majorana particle"), hypothesized as a fermion that is its own antiparticle.
*   1937 – [Muon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Muon "Muon") discovered by [Carl David Anderson](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carl_David_Anderson "Carl David Anderson") and [Seth Neddermeyer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seth_Neddermeyer "Seth Neddermeyer")
*   1938 – [Pyotr Kapitsa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pyotr_Kapitsa "Pyotr Kapitsa"): [Superfluidity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Superfluid "Superfluid") discovered
*   1938 – [Otto Hahn](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Otto_Hahn "Otto Hahn"), [Lise Meitner](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lise_Meitner "Lise Meitner") and [Fritz Strassmann](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fritz_Strassmann "Fritz Strassmann")[Nuclear fission](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nuclear_fission "Nuclear fission") discovered
*   1938–39 – [Stellar fusion](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stellar_fusion "Stellar fusion") explains energy production in stars[[14]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_fundamental_physics_discoveries#cite_note-14)
*   1939 – [Uranium](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Uranium "Uranium") fission discovered
*   1941 – [Feynman path integral](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Feynman_path_integral "Feynman path integral")
*   1944 – Theory of magnetism in 2D: [Ising model](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ising_model "Ising model")
*   1947 – [C.F. Powell](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/C.F._Powell "C.F. Powell"), [Giuseppe Occhialini](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Giuseppe_Occhialini "Giuseppe Occhialini"), [César Lattes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/C%C3%A9sar_Lattes "César Lattes"): [Pion](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pion "Pion") discovered
*   1948 – [Richard Feynman](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Richard_Feynman "Richard Feynman"), [Shinichiro Tomonaga](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shinichiro_Tomonaga "Shinichiro Tomonaga"), [Julian Schwinger](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Julian_Schwinger "Julian Schwinger"), [Freeman Dyson](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Freeman_Dyson "Freeman Dyson"): [Quantum electrodynamics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quantum_electrodynamics "Quantum electrodynamics")
*   1948 – Invention of the [maser](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maser "Maser") and [laser](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Laser "Laser") by [Charles Townes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Charles_Townes "Charles Townes")
*   1948 – [Feynman diagrams](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Feynman_diagrams "Feynman diagrams")
*   1955 - [Emilio Segrè](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Emilio_Segr%C3%A8 "Emilio Segrè") and [Owen Chamberlain](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Owen_Chamberlain "Owen Chamberlain"): [Antiproton](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antiproton "Antiproton") discovered
*   1956 – [Bruce Cork](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bruce_Cork "Bruce Cork"): [Antineutron](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antineutron "Antineutron") discovered
*   1956 – [Electron neutrino](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Electron_neutrino "Electron neutrino") discovered
*   1956–57 – [Parity violation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Parity_(physics) "Parity (physics)") proved by [Chien-Shiung Wu](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chien_Shiung_Wu "Chien Shiung Wu")
*   1957 - [Many-worlds](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Many-worlds "Many-worlds"), also called the[relative state formulation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Relative_state_formulation "Relative state formulation")or the[Everett interpretation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Everett_interpretation "Everett interpretation").
*   1957 – [BCS theory](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/BCS_theory "BCS theory") explaining [superconductivity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Superconductivity "Superconductivity")
*   1959–60 – Role of topology in quantum physics predicted and confirmed[_[citation needed](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation\_needed "Wikipedia:Citation needed")_]
*   1962 – [Murray Gell-Mann](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Murray_Gell-Mann "Murray Gell-Mann") and [Yuval Ne'eman](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yuval_Ne%27eman "Yuval Ne'eman"): [SU(3)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/SU(3) "SU(3)") theory of [strong interactions](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Strong_interaction "Strong interaction")[[15]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_fundamental_physics_discoveries#cite_note-15)[[16]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_fundamental_physics_discoveries#cite_note-16)
*   1962 – [Muon neutrino](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Muon_neutrino "Muon neutrino") discovered
*   1963 – Chien-Shiung Wu confirms the conserved vector current theory [for weak interactions](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Weak_interaction "Weak interaction")
*   1963 – [Murray Gell-Mann](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Murray_Gell-Mann "Murray Gell-Mann") and [George Zweig](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/George_Zweig "George Zweig"): Quarks predicted
*   1964 – [Bell's Theorem](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bell%27s_Theorem "Bell's Theorem") initiates quantitative study of [quantum entanglement](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quantum_entanglement "Quantum entanglement")
*   1964 - First [black hole](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Black_hole "Black hole"), [Cygnus X-1](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cygnus_X-1 "Cygnus X-1"), discovered
*   1964 – [CP violation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/CP_violation "CP violation") discovered by [James Cronin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/James_Cronin "James Cronin") and [Val Fitch](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Val_Fitch "Val Fitch").
*   1965 – [Arno Penzias](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arno_Penzias "Arno Penzias") and [Robert Wilson](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_Woodrow_Wilson "Robert Woodrow Wilson"): [Cosmic Microwave Background](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cosmic_Microwave_Background "Cosmic Microwave Background") (CMB) discovered
*   1967 – Unification of [weak interaction](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Weak_interaction "Weak interaction") and electromagnetism ([electroweak theory](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Electroweak_theory "Electroweak theory"))
*   1967 – [Solar neutrino problem](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Solar_neutrino_problem "Solar neutrino problem") found
*   1967 – [Pulsars](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pulsars "Pulsars") (rotating neutron stars) discovered
*   1968 – Experimental evidence for [quarks](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quark "Quark") found
*   1968 – [Vera Rubin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vera_Rubin "Vera Rubin"): [Dark matter](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dark_matter "Dark matter") theories
*   1970–73 – [Standard Model](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Standard_Model "Standard Model") of [elementary particles](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elementary_particle "Elementary particle") invented
*   1971 – [Helium 3](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Helium-3 "Helium-3")[superfluidity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Superfluidity "Superfluidity")
*   1971–75 – [Michael Fisher](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Michael_Fisher "Michael Fisher"), [Kenneth G. Wilson](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kenneth_G._Wilson "Kenneth G. Wilson"), and [Leo Kadanoff](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leo_Kadanoff "Leo Kadanoff"): [Renormalization group](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Renormalization_group "Renormalization group")
*   1972 – [Jacob Bekenstein](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jacob_Bekenstein "Jacob Bekenstein"): [Black Hole Entropy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Black_hole_thermodynamics "Black hole thermodynamics") suggested[[17]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_fundamental_physics_discoveries#cite_note-17)
*   1974 – [Stephen Hawking](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stephen_Hawking "Stephen Hawking"): Black hole radiation ([Hawking radiation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hawking_radiation "Hawking radiation")) predicted[[18]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_fundamental_physics_discoveries#cite_note-18)
*   1974 – [Charmed quark](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Charmed_quark "Charmed quark") discovered
*   1975 – [Tau lepton](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tau_(particle) "Tau (particle)") found
*   1975 – [Abraham Pais](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Abraham_Pais "Abraham Pais") and [Sam Treiman](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sam_Treiman "Sam Treiman"): Introduction of the [Standard Model](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Standard_Model "Standard Model") of particle physics term
*   1977 – [Bottom quark](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bottom_quark "Bottom quark") found
*   1977 – [Anderson localization](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anderson_localization "Anderson localization") recognised (Nobel prize in 1977, [Philip W. Anderson](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philip_W._Anderson "Philip W. Anderson"), Mott, Van Fleck)
*   1980 – [Strangeness](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Strangeness_and_quark%E2%80%93gluon_plasma "Strangeness and quark–gluon plasma") as a signature of [quark-gluon plasma](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quark%E2%80%93gluon_plasma "Quark–gluon plasma") predicted[[19]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_fundamental_physics_discoveries#cite_note-19)
*   1980 – [Richard Feynman](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Richard_Feynman "Richard Feynman") proposes [quantum computing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quantum_computing "Quantum computing")
*   1980 – [Quantum Hall effect](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quantum_Hall_effect "Quantum Hall effect")
*   1981 – [Alan Guth](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alan_Guth "Alan Guth") Theory of [cosmic inflation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cosmic_inflation "Cosmic inflation") proposed [_[dubious](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Accuracy\_dispute#Disputed\_statement "Wikipedia:Accuracy dispute") – [discuss](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Talk:Timeline\_of\_fundamental\_physics\_discoveries#Dubious "Talk:Timeline of fundamental physics discoveries")_]
*   1982 – [Aspect experiment](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aspect_experiment "Aspect experiment") confirms violations of [Bell's inequalities](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bell_theorem "Bell theorem")
*   1981 – [Fractional quantum Hall effect](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fractional_quantum_Hall_effect "Fractional quantum Hall effect") discovered
*   1984 – [W and Z bosons](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/W_and_Z_bosons "W and Z bosons") directly observed
*   1984 – First laboratory implementation of [quantum cryptography](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quantum_cryptography "Quantum cryptography")
*   1987 – [High-temperature superconductivity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/High-temperature_superconductivity "High-temperature superconductivity") discovered in 1986, awarded Nobel prize in 1987 (J. Georg Bednorz and K. Alexander Müller)

*   1995 – [Top quark](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Top_quark "Top quark") discovered
*   1995–2000 – [Econophysics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Econophysics "Econophysics") and [Kinetic exchange models of markets](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kinetic_exchange_models_of_markets "Kinetic exchange models of markets")
*   1997 – [Juan Maldacena](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Juan_Maldacena "Juan Maldacena") proposed the [AdS/CFT correspondence](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AdS/CFT_correspondence "AdS/CFT correspondence")
*   1998 – [Accelerating expansion of the universe](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Accelerating_expansion_of_the_universe "Accelerating expansion of the universe") discovered by the [Supernova Cosmology Project](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Supernova_Cosmology_Project "Supernova Cosmology Project") and the [High-Z Supernova Search Team](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/High-Z_Supernova_Search_Team "High-Z Supernova Search Team")
*   1998 – [Atmospheric neutrino oscillation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Atmospheric_neutrino_oscillation "Atmospheric neutrino oscillation") established
*   1999 – [Lene Vestergaard Hau](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lene_Hau "Lene Hau"): [Slow light](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Slow_light "Slow light") experimentally demonstrated
*   2000 – [Quark-gluon plasma](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quark%E2%80%93gluon_plasma "Quark–gluon plasma") found[[20]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_fundamental_physics_discoveries#cite_note-20)
*   2000 – [Tau neutrino](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tau_neutrino "Tau neutrino") found

*   2001 – [Solar neutrino oscillation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Neutrino_oscillation#Solar_neutrino_oscillation "Neutrino oscillation") observed, resolving the [solar neutrino problem](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Solar_neutrino_problem "Solar neutrino problem")
*   2003 – [WMAP](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/WMAP "WMAP") observations of [cosmic microwave background](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cosmic_microwave_background "Cosmic microwave background")
*   2004 – Exceptional properties of [graphene](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Graphene "Graphene") discovered
*   2007 – [Giant magnetoresistance](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Giant_magnetoresistance "Giant magnetoresistance") recognized (Nobel prize, Albert Fert and Peter Grünberg)
*   2008 – First artificial production of [antimatter](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antimatter "Antimatter") ([positrons](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Positrons "Positrons")), by the [LLNL](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lawrence_Livermore_National_Laboratory "Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory")
*   2008 – 16-year study of stellar orbits around [Sagittarius A*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sagittarius_A*#History "Sagittarius A*") provides strong evidence for a [supermassive black hole](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Supermassive_black_hole "Supermassive black hole") at the centre of the [Milky Way](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Milky_Way "Milky Way") galaxy
*   2009 – _[Planck](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Planck\_(spacecraft) "Planck (spacecraft)")_ begins observations of [cosmic microwave background](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cosmic_microwave_background "Cosmic microwave background")
*   2012 – [Higgs boson](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Higgs_boson "Higgs boson") found by the [Compact Muon Solenoid](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Compact_Muon_Solenoid "Compact Muon Solenoid")[[21]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_fundamental_physics_discoveries#cite_note-cms0731-21) and [ATLAS](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ATLAS_experiment "ATLAS experiment")[[22]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_fundamental_physics_discoveries#cite_note-22) experiments at the [Large Hadron Collider](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Large_Hadron_Collider "Large Hadron Collider")
*   2015 – [Gravitational waves](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gravitational_waves "Gravitational waves") are [observed](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_gravitational_wave_observations "List of gravitational wave observations")
*   2016 – [Topological order](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Topological_order "Topological order") – topological phase transitions and order – recognized (Nobel prize, David J. Thouless, F. Duncan M. Haldane and J. Michael Kosterlitz)
*   2019 – First image of a [black hole](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Black_hole "Black hole")
*   2023 – Experimental evidence of stochastic [gravitational wave background](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gravitational_wave_background "Gravitational wave background")[[23]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_fundamental_physics_discoveries#cite_note-23)
*   2023 – First "image" of the Milky Way [in neutrinos](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Neutrino_astronomy "Neutrino astronomy") instead of light[[24]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_fundamental_physics_discoveries#cite_note-24)
