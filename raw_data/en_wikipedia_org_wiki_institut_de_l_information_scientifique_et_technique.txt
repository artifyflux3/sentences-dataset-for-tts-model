It has as mission to collect, treat and diffuse results of scientific and technical research. The INIST produces three bibliographic multilingual and multidisciplinary databases : PASCAL , FRANCIS , and DOGE . It is based at Vandœuvre-lès-Nancy , in a building designed by Jean Nouvel . In addition, INIST publishes a number of electronic journals .
