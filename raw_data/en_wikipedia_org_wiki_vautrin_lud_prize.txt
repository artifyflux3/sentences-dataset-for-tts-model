[ 1 ] Established in 1991, the award is named after the 16th Century French scholar Vautrin Lud [ fr ] , who first named, in 1507, the new continent explored by Amerigo Vespucci "America". The award is given in the autumn of each year at the International Geography Festival in Saint-Dié-des-Vosges , France (the home town of Vautrin Lud) and decided upon by a five-person international jury.
