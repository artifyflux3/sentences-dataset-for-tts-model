934998403 Links Journal homepage Online archive Nature Microbiology is a monthly online-only peer reviewed scientific journal published by Nature Portfolio . It was established in 2016.

[ 1 ] The editor-in-chief is Susan Jones who is part of an in-house team of editors.

[ 2 ] Abstracting and indexing [ edit ] The journal is abstracted and indexed in: PubMed [ 3 ] Science Citation Index Expanded [ 4 ] Scopus [ 5 ] According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2023 impact factor of 20.5.
