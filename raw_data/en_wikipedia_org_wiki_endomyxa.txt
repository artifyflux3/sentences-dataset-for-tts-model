Bass & Berney in Adl et al. 2019 [ 1 ] Subgroups [ 1 ] Vampyrellida Phytomyxea Filoreta Gromia Viscidocauda [ 2 ] [ 3 ] Ascetosporea Endomyxa is a group of eukaryotic organisms in the supergroup Rhizaria .

[ 4 ] [ 5 ] They were initially a subphylum of Cercozoa and later a subphylum of Retaria , but several analyses have proven they are a phylogenetically separate lineage, [ 1 ] and Endomyxa is currently regarded as its own phylum .
