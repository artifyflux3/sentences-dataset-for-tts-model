Infobox references Chemical compound Iodous acid is the chemical compound with the formula HIO 2 . Its salts are named iodites ; these are exceedingly unstable and have been observed but never isolated.

[ 1 ] They will rapidly disproportionate to molecular iodine and iodates .

Other oxyacids [ edit ] Iodous acid is part of a series of oxyacids in which iodine can assume oxidation states of −1, +1, +3, +5, or +7. A number of neutral iodine oxides are also known.
