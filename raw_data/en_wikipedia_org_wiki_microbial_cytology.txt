[ 1 ] Origin of "Microbial" 1880-85; < Greek mīkro- micro- small + bíos life).  "Cytology" 1857; < Cyto-is derived from the Greek "kytos" meaning "hollow, as a cell or container." + -logy meaning "the study of").

[ 2 ] Microbial cytology is analyzed under a microscope for cells which were collected from a part of the body. The main purpose of microbial cytology is to see the structure of the cells , and how they form and operate.
