The known numbered Saturn-crossers (as of 2005) are listed below. There is only one inner-grazer ( 944 Hidalgo ) and no outer-grazers or co-orbitals known; most if not all of the crossers are centaurs .

(15504) 1999 RG 33 is a damocloid .
