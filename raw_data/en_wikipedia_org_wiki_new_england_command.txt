They have played in the WPLL since the 2018 season.  In the 2018 season, the five teams in the WPLL played in a barnstorming format, with all five teams playing at a single venue, and the Command were the inaugural champions.

After cancelling the 2020 season in April due to the COVID-19 pandemic , the league announced on August 6, 2020, that it would be closing down.
