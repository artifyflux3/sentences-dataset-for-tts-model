The first edition of University Physics was published by Mark Zemansky and Francis Sears in 1949.

[ 2 ] [ 3 ] Hugh Young became a coauthor with Sears and Zemansky in 1973. Now in its 15th edition, University Physics is among the most widely used introductory textbooks in the world.
