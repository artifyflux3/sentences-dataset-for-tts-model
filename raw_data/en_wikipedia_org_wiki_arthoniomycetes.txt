It includes two orders: Arthoniales and Lichenostigmatales .

[ 2 ] Most of the taxa in these orders are tropical and subtropical lichens .

Systematics [ edit ] Phylogenetic analysis supports the monophyly of this class.

[ 3 ] Dothideomycetes is a sister group.

[ 4 ] Characteristics [ edit ] Taxa have apothecia, cup- or saucer- shaped ascoma in which the hymenium is exposed at maturity. These apothecia are bitunicate - with clearly differentiated inner and outer walls.
