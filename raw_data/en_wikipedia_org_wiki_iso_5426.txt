( December 2018 ) ISO 5426 Alias(es) ISO-IR -53 Standard ISO 5426 Other related encoding(s) ETS 300 706 ISO 6937 / ITU T.51 ITU T.61 NeXT Multinational PostScript Standard Encoding ITU T.101 ISO 5426 [ 1 ] ("Extension of the Latin alphabet coded character set for bibliographic information interchange") is a character set developed by ISO , [ 2 ] similar to ISO/IEC 6937 . It was first published in 1980.

[ 5 ] It specifies a set of 70 characters, some of which do not exist in Unicode.

[ as of?

] [ clarification needed ] Michael Everson proposed the missing characters in Unicode 3.0, but some were postponed for further study. Later, new evidence was found, and more were encoded. P with belt is an error for P with flourish. P with middle tilde is an error for P with squirrel tail.

[ 6 ] The character at 0x42 will be encoded at U+1ACF in Unicode 17.0.
