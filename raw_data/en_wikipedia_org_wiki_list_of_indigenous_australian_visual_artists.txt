Find sources: "List of Indigenous Australian visual artists" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( January 2020 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) Numerous Indigenous Australians are noted for their participation in, and contributions to, the Visual arts of Australia and abroad.

Contemporary Indigenous Australian art is a national movement of international significance with work by Indigenous artists, including paintings by those from the Western Desert , achieving widespread critical acclaim.

Because naming conventions for Indigenous Australians vary widely, this list is ordered by first name rather than surname.
