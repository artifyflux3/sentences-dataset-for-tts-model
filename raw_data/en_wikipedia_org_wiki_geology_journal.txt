GSA stated (in 2006) [ 1 ] that it is the most widely read scientific journal in the field of earth science .

[ needs update?

] It is published monthly, with each issue containing 20 or more articles .

One of the goals of the journal is to provide a forum for shorter articles (four pages each) and less focus on purely academic research–type articles. According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal had a 2020 impact factor of 5.399.

[ 2 ] The journal is indexed in Scopus and SCImago .
