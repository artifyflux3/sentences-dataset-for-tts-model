Fritzsch, 2019 Clades Porifera ParaHoxozoa Synonyms Myriazoa The Benthozoa or Myriazoa [ 1 ] are a proposed basal animal clade consisting of the Porifera and ParaHoxozoa as a sister group of Ctenophora .

[ 2 ] An alternative phylogeny is given by the Porifera-sister hypothesis in which Porifera are the first diverging animal group.
