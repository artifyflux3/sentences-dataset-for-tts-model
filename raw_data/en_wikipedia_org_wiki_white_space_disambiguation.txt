Look up _**[white space](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/white\_space "wiktionary:white space")**_ or _**[whitespace](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/whitespace "wiktionary:whitespace")**_ in Wiktionary, the free dictionary.

**White space** or **whitespace** may refer to:

Technology
----------

[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=White_space&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Technology")]

*   [Whitespace characters](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Whitespace_character "Whitespace character"), characters in computing that represent horizontal or vertical space
*   [White spaces (radio)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/White_spaces_(radio) "White spaces (radio)"), allocated but locally unused radio frequencies
*   [TV White Space Database](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/TV_White_Space_Database "TV White Space Database"), a mechanism to enable utilization of the allocated but locally unused radio frequencies
*   [Whitespace (programming language)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Whitespace_(programming_language) "Whitespace (programming language)"), an esoteric programming language

Other uses
----------

[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=White_space&action=edit&section=2 "Edit section: Other uses")]

*   [White space (visual arts)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/White_space_(visual_arts) "White space (visual arts)"), portions of a page layout or image left unmarked 
    *   [Negative space](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Negative_space "Negative space"), portions of a page layout or image deliberately left unmarked and used as a component

*   [Space (punctuation)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_(punctuation) "Space (punctuation)"), the space between two words of text
*   _[The White Space](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The\_White\_Space "The White Space")_, a 2009 drama film
*   White Space, a three-book science fiction series by [Elizabeth Bear](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elizabeth_Bear "Elizabeth Bear")
*   White Space Zone, the main hub in the video game _[Sonic Generations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sonic\_Generations "Sonic Generations")_
*   A location in the video game _[OMORI](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/OMORI\_(video\_game) "OMORI (video game)")_
