An alternative to the traditional protostome- deuterostome dichotomy, it has found weak support in several studies. Under this hypothesis, Centroneuralia would be sister to Xenambulacraria ( Xenacoelomorpha + Ambulacraria ) at the base of Bilateria .

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] [ 3 ] [ 4 ] Centroneuralia, as a proposed clade, originates in phylogenomics . More precisely, recent studies correlate support for Deuterostomia with simpler, site-homogeneous models, while more sophisticated and site-heterogeneous models recover Centroneuralia more often.
