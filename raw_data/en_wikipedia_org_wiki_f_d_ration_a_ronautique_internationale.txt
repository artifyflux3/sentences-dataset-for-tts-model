The **World Air Sports Federation** ([French](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/French_language "French language"): _**Fédération aéronautique internationale**_; **FAI**) is the world governing body for [air sports](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Air_sports "Air sports"), and also stewards definitions regarding [human spaceflight](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Human_spaceflight "Human spaceflight"). It was founded on 14 October 1905, and is headquartered in [Lausanne](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lausanne "Lausanne"), Switzerland.[[3]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_A%C3%A9ronautique_Internationale#cite_note-3) It maintains world records for [aeronautical](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aeronautics "Aeronautics") activities, including [ballooning](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Balloon_(aircraft) "Balloon (aircraft)"), [aeromodeling](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aeromodeling "Aeromodeling"), and [unmanned aerial vehicles](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Unmanned_aerial_vehicle "Unmanned aerial vehicle") (drones), as well as flights into space.

History
-------

The FAI was founded at a conference held in Paris 12–14 October 1905, which was organized following a resolution passed by the [Olympic Congress](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Olympic_Congress "Olympic Congress") held in Brussels on 10 June 1905 calling for the creation of an Association "to regulate the sport of flying, ... the various aviation meetings and advance the science and sport of Aeronautics."[[4]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_A%C3%A9ronautique_Internationale#cite_note-4) The conference was attended by representatives from 8 countries: Belgium (Aéro Club Royal de Belgique, founded 1901), France ([Aéro-Club de France](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/A%C3%A9ro-Club_de_France "Aéro-Club de France"), 1898), Germany (Deutscher Luftschiffer Verband aka "German Airship League", founded 1902), Great Britain ([Royal Aero Club](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Royal_Aero_Club "Royal Aero Club"), 1901), Italy ([Aero Club d'Italia](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Aero_Club_d%27Italia&action=edit&redlink=1 "Aero Club d'Italia (page does not exist)")[[it](https://it.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aero_Club_d%27Italia "it:Aero Club d'Italia")], 1904), Spain ([Real Aero Club de España](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Real_Aero_Club_de_Espa%C3%B1a&action=edit&redlink=1 "Real Aero Club de España (page does not exist)")[[es](https://es.wikipedia.org/wiki/Real_Aero_Club_de_Espa%C3%B1a "es:Real Aero Club de España")], 1905), Switzerland ([Aero-Club der Schweiz](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aero-Club_der_Schweiz "Aero-Club der Schweiz"), 1900) and the United States ([Aero Club of America](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aero_Club_of_America "Aero Club of America"), 1905).

On 2 February 2017 the FAI announced its new strategic partnership with international asset management firm Noosphere Ventures. FAI Secretary General [Susanne Schödel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Susanne_Schoedel "Susanne Schoedel"), FAI President Frits Brink and Noosphere Ventures Managing Partner [Max Polyakov](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Max_Polyakov "Max Polyakov") signed the agreement, making Noosphere Ventures FAI's Global Technical Partner.[[5]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_A%C3%A9ronautique_Internationale#cite_note-5)[[6]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_A%C3%A9ronautique_Internationale#cite_note-6)

The FAI suspended Russia and Belarus due to the [2022 Russian invasion of Ukraine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2022_Russian_invasion_of_Ukraine "2022 Russian invasion of Ukraine"), as a result of which pilots from Russia and Belarus will not be able to compete in any FAI-sanctioned event in the 13 FAI air sports disciplines, including paragliding, hang gliding, and paramotoring.[[7]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_A%C3%A9ronautique_Internationale#cite_note-xcmag.com-7)

FAI General Conference
----------------------

The 117th FAI General Conference took place in Dayton, Ohio, US (the 'Birthplace of Aviation') on 26 and 27 October 2023. [[8]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_A%C3%A9ronautique_Internationale#cite_note-8)

The 118th FAI General Conference was held from 20 to 21 November 2024 in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia.

The 119th FAI General Conference will take place in Vantaa, Finland from 22 to 24 October 2025.

Activities
----------

The FAI is the international governing body for the following activities:

*   [Aerobatics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aerobatics "Aerobatics") through the FAI Aerobatics Commission (Commission Internationale de Voltige Aérienne – CIVA)[[9]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_A%C3%A9ronautique_Internationale#cite_note-CIVA_website-9)
*   [Aeromodeling](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aeromodeling "Aeromodeling") and [drones](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Unmanned_aerial_vehicle "Unmanned aerial vehicle") through the FAI Aeromodelling Commission (Commission Internationale d'Aéro-Modélisme – CIAM)[[10]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_A%C3%A9ronautique_Internationale#cite_note-CIAM_website-10)
*   [Ballooning](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Balloon_(aircraft) "Balloon (aircraft)") through the FAI Ballooning Commission (Commission Internationale de l'Aérostation – CIA)[[11]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_A%C3%A9ronautique_Internationale#cite_note-CIA_website-11)
*   [General aviation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/General_aviation "General aviation") through the FAI General Aviation Commission (General Aviation Commission – GAC)[[12]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_A%C3%A9ronautique_Internationale#cite_note-GAC_website-12)
*   [Gliding](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gliding "Gliding") through the [FAI Gliding Commission](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/FAI_Gliding_Commission "FAI Gliding Commission") (International Gliding Commission – IGC)[[13]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_A%C3%A9ronautique_Internationale#cite_note-IGC_website-13)
*   [Hang gliding](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hang_gliding "Hang gliding")&[Paragliding](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paragliding "Paragliding") through the FAI Hang Gliding & Paragliding Commission (Commission Internationale de Vol Libre – CIVL)[[14]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_A%C3%A9ronautique_Internationale#cite_note-CIVL_website-14)
*   [Human-powered aircraft](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Human-powered_aircraft "Human-powered aircraft") through the FAI Amateur-Built and Experimental Aircraft Commission (Commission Internationale des Aéronefs de Construction Amateur – CIACA)[[15]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_A%C3%A9ronautique_Internationale#cite_note-CIACA_website-15)
*   [Microlighting](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ultralight_aviation "Ultralight aviation") and [Paramotoring](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paramotor "Paramotor") through the FAI Microlight & Paramotor Commission (Commission Internationale de Microaviation – CIMA)[[16]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_A%C3%A9ronautique_Internationale#cite_note-CIMA_website-16)
*   [Skydiving](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skydiving "Skydiving") through the FAI [International Skydiving Commission](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/International_Skydiving_Commission "International Skydiving Commission")[[17]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_A%C3%A9ronautique_Internationale#cite_note-ISC_website-17)
*   [Rotorcraft](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rotorcraft "Rotorcraft") through the FAI Rotorcraft Commission (Commission Internationale de giraviation – CIG)[[18]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_A%C3%A9ronautique_Internationale#cite_note-CIG_website-18)

The FAI establishes the standards for records in the activities. Where these are [air sports](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Air_sports "Air sports"), the FAI also oversees international competitions at world and continental levels, and also organizes the [World Air Games](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/World_Air_Games "World Air Games") and [FAI World Grand Prix](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/FAI_World_Grand_Prix "FAI World Grand Prix").

The FAI organises the FAI International Drones Conference and Expo. This event offers a platform for organisations, businesses and individuals to discuss how drones are used today and to create a framework for how they will be used and impact on life in the future.

The FAI also keeps records set in [human spaceflight](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Human_spaceflight "Human spaceflight"), through the FAI Astronautic Records Commission (International Astronautic Records Commission – ICARE)[[19]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_A%C3%A9ronautique_Internationale#cite_note-ICARE_website-19)

### Kármán Line definition

The FAI defines the limit between [Earth's atmosphere](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Earth%27s_atmosphere "Earth's atmosphere") and [outer space](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Outer_space "Outer space"), the so-called Karman Line, as the [altitude](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Altitude "Altitude") of 100 kilometres (62 miles; 330,000 feet) above [Earth](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Earth "Earth")'s [sea level](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sea_level "Sea level").[[20]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_A%C3%A9ronautique_Internationale#cite_note-20)

Records
-------

[![Image 4](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/67/The_Soviet_Union_1971_CPA_3992_stamp_%28Yuri_Gagarin_Medal%2C_Spaceships_and_Planets%29.jpg/250px-The_Soviet_Union_1971_CPA_3992_stamp_%28Yuri_Gagarin_Medal%2C_Spaceships_and_Planets%29.jpg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:The_Soviet_Union_1971_CPA_3992_stamp_(Yuri_Gagarin_Medal,_Spaceships_and_Planets).jpg)

1971 [USSR](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/USSR "USSR") commemorative stamp depicting the Yuri A. Gagarin Gold Medal established by FAI

Among the FAI's responsibilities are the verification of record-breaking flights. For a flight to be registered as a "World Record," it has to comply with the FAI's strict rules, which include a proviso that the record must exceed the previous record by a certain percentage. Since the late 1930s, military aircraft have dominated some classes of record for powered aircraft such as speed, distance, payload, and height, though other classes are regularly claimed by civilians.

Some records are claimed by countries as their own, even though their achievements fail to meet FAI standards. These claims are not typically granted the status of official records. For example, [Yuri Gagarin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yuri_Gagarin "Yuri Gagarin") earned recognition for the first manned spaceflight, despite failing to meet FAI requirements. The FAI initially did not recognize the achievement because he did not land in his [Vostok spacecraft](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vostok_spacecraft "Vostok spacecraft") (he ejected from it), but later it recognized that Gagarin was the first human to fly into space. The FAI then established the Yuri A. Gagarin Gold Medal, which has been awarded since 1968.[[21]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_A%C3%A9ronautique_Internationale#cite_note-auto-21)

FAI Young Artists Contest
-------------------------

The FAI Young Artists Contest[[44]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_A%C3%A9ronautique_Internationale#cite_note-44) is an international painting competition for youngsters between the ages of 6 and 17. Each FAI Member Country organises the contest in their country, and the national winners are submitted to the International Jury each year.
