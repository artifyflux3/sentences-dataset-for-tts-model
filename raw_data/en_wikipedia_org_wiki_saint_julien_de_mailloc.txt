Saint-Julien-de-Mailloc ( French pronunciation: [sɛ̃ jyljɛ̃ də majo] ⓘ ) is a former commune in the Calvados department in the Normandy region in northwestern France . On 1 January 2016, it was merged into the new commune of Valorbiquet .
