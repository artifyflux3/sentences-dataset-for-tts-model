From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

[Geometry](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Geometry "Geometry") is a branch of [mathematics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mathematics "Mathematics") concerned with questions of shape, size, relative position of figures, and the properties of space. Geometry is one of the oldest mathematical sciences. Modern geometry also extends into non-Euclidean spaces, topology, and fractal dimensions, bridging pure mathematics with applications in physics, computer science, and data visualization.
