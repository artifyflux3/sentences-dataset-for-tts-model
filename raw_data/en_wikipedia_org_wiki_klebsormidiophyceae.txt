Klebsormidiales is an order of charophyte algae . Until Bierenbroodspot et al.'s phylogeny, [ 2 ] it was the only order in the class Klebsormidiophyceae , [ 3 ] sister of the Phragmoplastophyta . As of February 2022 [update] , AlgaeBase accepted two families in the order: [ 1 ] Elakatotrichaceae Hindák Klebsormidiaceae K.D.Stewart & K.R.Mattox In 2024, Bierenbroodspot et al. emended Klebsormidiophyceae and Klebsormidiales, and described 2 new orders, namely Entransiales and Hormidiellales .
