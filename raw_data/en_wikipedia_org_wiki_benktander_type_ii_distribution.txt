_{n}(x)} is the generalized Exponential integral [ note 1 ] The Benktander type II distribution , also called the Benktander distribution of the second kind, is one of two distributions introduced by Gunnar Benktander ( 1970 ) to model heavy-tailed losses commonly found in non-life/casualty actuarial science , using various forms of mean excess functions ( Benktander & Segerdahl 1960 ). This distribution is "close" to the Weibull distribution ( Kleiber & Kotz 2003 ).
