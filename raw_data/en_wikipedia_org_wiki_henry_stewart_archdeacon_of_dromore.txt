was an Anglican priest in Ireland in the nineteenth century.

[ 1 ] Stewart was born in Dublin and educated at Trinity College, Dublin .

[ 2 ] He was ordained in 1861. After two curacies he became Rector of Kilmore, County Down in 1866.

[ 3 ] From 1870 he was the incumbent at Seapatrick ; and from 1879 Archdeacon of Dromore .
