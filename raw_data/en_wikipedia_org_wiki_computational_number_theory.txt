[ 1 ] Computational number theory has applications to cryptography , including RSA , elliptic curve cryptography and post-quantum cryptography , and is used to investigate conjectures and open problems in number theory, including the Riemann hypothesis , the Birch and Swinnerton-Dyer conjecture , the ABC conjecture , the modularity conjecture , the Sato-Tate conjecture , and explicit aspects of the Langlands program .
