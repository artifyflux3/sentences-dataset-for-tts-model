Indexing CODEN ( alt · alt2 ) · JSTOR ( alt ) · LCCN ( alt ) MIAR · NLM ( alt ) · Scopus · W&L CODEN JCLLAX ISSN 0021-9541 (print) 1097-4652 (web) Links Journal homepage Online archive The Journal of Cellular Physiology is a peer-reviewed scientific journal focusing on all aspects of cellular physiology . The journal was originally established as Journal of Cellular and Comparative Physiology in 1932 and was renamed to its current title in 1966. The editor-in-chief is Alexander Hutchison (Wiley & Sons, Inc).
