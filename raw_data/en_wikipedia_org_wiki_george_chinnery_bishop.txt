[ 1 ] Chinnery was educated at Trinity College, Dublin .

[ 2 ] He was Dean of Cork from 1763 until 1779.

[ 3 ] On  21 December 1778 he was nominated to be Bishop of Killaloe and Kilfenora ; and was consecrated on 7 March 1779. He was translated to Cloyne on  15 February 1780.

[ 4 ] He died in August later that year.
