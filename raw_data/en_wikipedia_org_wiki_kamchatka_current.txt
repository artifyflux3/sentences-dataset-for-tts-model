Find sources: "Kamchatka Current" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( January 2024 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) The Kamchatka Current is a cold-water current flowing southwestward in the Bering Sea , along the Siberian Kamchatka Peninsula . It receives water from the Bering Slope Current.

[ 1 ] A portion of this current then becomes the Oyashio Current while the remainder joins the warmer North Pacific Current .
