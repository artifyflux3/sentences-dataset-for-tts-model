60623498 Links Journal homepage Online access The American Philosophical Quarterly ( APQ [ 1 ] ) is a peer-reviewed academic journal covering philosophy . It was established in 1964 by Nicholas Rescher and is published quarterly by University of Illinois Press under license with North American Philosophical Publications.
