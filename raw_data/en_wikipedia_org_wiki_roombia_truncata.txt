Roombia Light (above) and transmission electron micrographs (below) of R. truncata Scientific classification Domain: Eukaryota Clade : Pancryptista Phylum: Cryptista Subphylum: Rollomonadia Superclass: Leucocrypta Class: Leucocryptea Order: Kathablepharidida Family: Kathablepharidae Genus: Roombia Species: R. truncata Binomial name Roombia truncata Okamoto et al.

, 2009 Roombia truncata is a species of katablepharids , which are heterotrophic single-celled organisms.

[ 1 ] It was the first katablepharid to be generally available in cell culture , starting around 2009.  The culture consists of Roombia , the diatom Navicula , and unidentified bacteria.

Navicula provides the main food source for Roombia , although Roombia also feeds on the bacteria.

[ 1 ] The Roombia Truncata was identified through SSU rRNA gene analysis as a typical component of eukaryotes however its complete set of RNA transcripts show mgR2-like CCr (Light sensitive ion channel) unlike other cryptists within this branch of the eukaryotic tree of life.

Roombia was named after the Roomba robotic vacuum cleaner.
