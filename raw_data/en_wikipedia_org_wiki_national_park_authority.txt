Find sources: "National park authority" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( April 2025 ) A national park authority is a special term used in Great Britain for legal bodies charged with maintaining a national park of which, as of October 2021, there are ten in England, three in Wales and two in Scotland.

[ 1 ] The powers and duties of all such authorities are similar, but their work varies depending on where they are situated.

National park authorities were set up by the Environment Act 1995 .
