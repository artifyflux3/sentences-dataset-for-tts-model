Find sources: "Makaryevsky Uyezd" Kostroma Governorate – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( September 2020 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) Makaryevsky Uyezd ( Russian : Макарьевский уезд ) was one of the subdivisions of the Kostroma Governorate of the Russian Empire . It was situated in the southern part of the governorate. Its administrative centre was Makaryev .

Demographics [ edit ] At the time of the Russian Empire Census of 1897, Makaryevsky Uyezd had a population of 144,571. Of these, 99.9% spoke Russian as their native language.
