[ 3 ] [ 4 ] He was educated at Corpus Christi College, Oxford and ordained deacon in 1826 and priest in 1827.

[ 5 ] He began his ecclesiastical career with a curacy at Longbridge Deverill . After this he was the Rector of Clonmore [ 6 ] then a Chaplain to Thomas de Grey, 2nd Earl de Grey , Lord Lieutenant of Ireland .

[ 7 ] Stained glass window in memory of Hugh Usher Tighe in St Columb's Cathedral Tighe became Dean of Leighlin , then Dean of Ardagh and Dean of the Chapel Royal , and finally Dean of Derry in 1860. He died in 1874 and was buried in Drumcar . A stained glass window by Charles Alexander Gibbs was donated by his wife in St Columb's Cathedral .
