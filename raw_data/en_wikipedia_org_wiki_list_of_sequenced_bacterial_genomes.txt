Most of these sequences have been placed in the International Nucleotide Sequence Database Collaboration , a public database which can be searched [ 1 ] on the web . A few of the listed genomes may not be in the INSDC database, but in other public databases [ verification needed ] .

Genomes listed as "Unpublished" are in a database, but not in the peer-reviewed scientific literature.

For the genomes of archaea see list of sequenced archaeal genomes .
