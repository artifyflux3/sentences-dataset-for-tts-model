In 1008, Domiyat traveled to the Buddhist pilgrimage site in Shandong , China, to seek out the Chinese Emperor Zhenzong with gifts from his ruler Al-Hakim bi-Amr Allah .

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] His arrival successfully reopened diplomatic relations between Egypt and China that had been lost since the collapse of the Tang dynasty . Egypt became one of only a few countries in the Middle East to establish relations with China in the pre-modern period.
