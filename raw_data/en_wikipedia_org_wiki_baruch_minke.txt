He discovered TRP ion channels , involved in sensory sensations and pain .

[ 1 ] Since 1987, he has been Professor of Physiology at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem .

[ 2 ] In 2010, he was awarded the Prince of Asturias Awards for Technical and Scientific Research along David Julius and Linda R. Watkins .
