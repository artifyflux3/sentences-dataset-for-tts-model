Indexing CODEN ( alt · alt2 ) · JSTOR ( alt ) · LCCN ( alt ) MIAR · NLM ( alt ) · Scopus · W&L ISSN 1477-8238 (print) 1477-8246 (web) Links Journal homepage Online access Knowledge Management Research & Practice is a quarterly peer-reviewed academic journal covering all aspects of knowledge management , organisational learning, intellectual capital, and knowledge economics. It is an official journal of the Operational Research Society .

Abstracting and indexing [ edit ] The journal is currently abstracted and indexed in Ei Compendex , Embase , GEOBASE , Social Sciences Citation Index , and Scopus . According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2023 impact factor of 3.2.
