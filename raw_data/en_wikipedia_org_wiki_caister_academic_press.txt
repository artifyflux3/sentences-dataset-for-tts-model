The address for the editorial offices is in Poole , UK with worldwide sales and distribution through the Ingram Content Group . Published books include review volumes, practical manuals and reference texts aimed at the post-graduate, research and professional market.

Aims and scope [ edit ] Caister Academic Press aims to provide the scientific community with authoritative texts on topical areas of microbiology and molecular biology . There is a focus on current research and emerging trends. Volumes on related technology and applications are also published.

History [ edit ] The academic publishing company was founded in 1993 as Horizon Scientific Press. Incorporated as a limited company in 2001, the company changed its name to Caister Academic Press in 2008.
