Scientific classification Kingdom: Plantae Division: Chlorophyta Class: Pyramimonadophyceae Orders Pyramimonadales Pyramimonadophyceae is a class of green algae in the division Chlorophyta .

[ 1 ] In 2024, one of its former members, Pseudoscourfieldiales , was classified into the new class Pseudoscourfieldiophyceae .
