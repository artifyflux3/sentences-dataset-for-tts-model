                     Heinz Maier-Leibnitz Award (2004) Scientific career Fields Marine ecology , fisheries science Institutions Dalhousie University Boris Worm is a marine ecologist, and the Killam Research Professor at Dalhousie University in Halifax, Nova Scotia, Canada.

[ 1 ] Worm is known for his scientific contributions and commitment to spreading public awareness regarding marine conservation.

[ 2 ] In 2004 he received the German "Heinz Maier Leibnitz"-Award, an award for young researchers from the German Research Foundation (DFG).
