McCarthy is a former director of time at the United States Naval Observatory . McCarthy also works for the International Earth Rotation and Reference Systems Service .

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] In recognition of his work on Global Positioning System (GPS) satellite navigation, McCarthy was inducted into the Naval Oceanography Hall of Fame on 6 April 2023 at a ceremony hosted by the U.S. Naval Observatory.
