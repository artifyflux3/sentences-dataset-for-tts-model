[ 1 ] He was Chaplain to Oliver St John, 1st Viscount Grandison , Lord Deputy of Ireland [ 2 ] and Rector of Ardrahan .

[ 3 ] He was appointed Dean of Waterford on 17 November 1620; [ 4 ] Dean of Derry on 3 May 1621 [ 5 ] and Dean of Limerick on 9 November 1635, serving until 1640.
