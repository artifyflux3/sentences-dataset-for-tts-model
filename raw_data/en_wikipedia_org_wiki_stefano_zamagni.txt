1968) ​ Academic background Alma mater Università Cattolica del Sacro Cuore Influences Pierpaolo Donati Academic work Discipline Economics Sub-discipline International economics welfare economics Institutions University of Parma University of Bologna Stefano Zamagni KSG (born 4 January 1943) is an Italian economist .

[ 1 ] Born in Rimini , [ 2 ] Zamagni is Professor of Economics at the University of Bologna . Zamagni is also a fellow of the Human Development and Capability Association [ 3 ] and President of the Pontifical Academy of Social Sciences .
