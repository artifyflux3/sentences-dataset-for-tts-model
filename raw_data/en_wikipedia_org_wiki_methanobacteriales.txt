[ 1 ] Species within this order differ from other methanogens in that they can use fewer catabolic substrates and have distinct morphological characteristics, lipid compositions, and RNA sequences.

[ 2 ] Their cell walls are composed of pseudomurein . Most species are Gram-positive with rod-shaped bodies and some can form long filaments. Most of them use formate to reduce carbon dioxide , but those of the genus Methanosphaera use hydrogen to reduce methanol to methane .

[ 2 ] Phylogeny [ edit ] The currently accepted taxonomy is based on the List of Prokaryotic names with Standing in Nomenclature (LPSN) [ 3 ] and National Center for Biotechnology Information (NCBI).
