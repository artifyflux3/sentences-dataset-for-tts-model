[ 1 ] Post-consumerism can also be viewed as moving beyond the current model of addictive consumerism .

[ 2 ] [ 3 ] This personal and societal strategy utilizes each individual's core values to identify the "satisfaction of enough for today," [ 4 ] also called "self-defined enoughness." [ 5 ] The intent and outcome of this basic strategy to date has "reached people where they are rather than simply where we are." [ 6 ] Therefore the "Do I have enough stuff for now?" campaign "is promoting this intriguing question" regardless of the answer.
