Feature type Lacus Coordinates 76°45′N 17°29′W ﻿ / ﻿ 76.75°N 17.49°W ﻿ / 76.75; -17.49 Diameter 63 km [ note 1 ] Eponym Lake Sotonera Sotonera Lacus is one of a number of hydrocarbon lakes found on Saturn's largest moon, Titan .

[ 1 ] Sotonera Lacus is located near the north pole of Titan, centered on latitude 76.75° N and longitude 17.49° W , and measures 63 km in length.

[ 1 ] [ note 1 ] It is situated in a north polar region where the majority of Titan's large lakes are found.

The lake is composed of liquid methane and ethane , [ 2 ] and was detected by the space probe Cassini . It was named in 2007 after Lake Sotonera in Spain .
