In [logic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Logic "Logic"), a set of [symbols](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Symbol_(logic) "Symbol (logic)") is commonly used to express logical representation. The following table lists many common symbols, together with their name, how they should be read out loud, and the related field of [mathematics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mathematics "Mathematics"). Additionally, the subsequent columns contains an informal explanation, a short example, the [Unicode](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Unicode "Unicode") location, the name for use in [HTML](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HTML "HTML") documents,[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_logic_symbols#cite_note-1) and the [LaTeX](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/LaTeX "LaTeX") symbol.
