12,000 Regions with significant populations Sydney and Melbourne Languages Australian English , Slovak Religion Roman Catholic (Majority) Protestantism , Judaism Related ethnic groups Slovaks · Czechs · Hungarians Slovak Australians are Australian citizens of full or partial Slovak ancestry or a person born in Slovakia who resides in Australia .

[ 1 ] Approximately 12,000 residents in Australia report to have Slovak ancestry.
