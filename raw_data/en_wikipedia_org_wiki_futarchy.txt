Democracy indices Democratic transition Autocratization Democratisation Hybrid regimes Politics portal v t e Futarchy is a form of government proposed by economist Robin Hanson , in which elected officials define measures of national wellbeing, and prediction markets are used to determine which policies will have the most positive effect.

[ 1 ] It was named by The New York Times as a buzzword of 2008.

[ 2 ] The idea of futarchy was later proposed in the context of blockchains and the DAO .

[ 3 ] Criticism [ edit ] I would bet against the future of futarchy, or its likelihood of succeeding were it in place. Robin says "vote on values, bet on beliefs", but I don't think values and beliefs can be so easily separated.
