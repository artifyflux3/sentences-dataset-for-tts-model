Wheelis is an American microbiologist . Wheelis is currently a professor in the College of Biological Sciences, University of California, Davis .

[ 1 ] Carl Woese and Otto Kandler with Wheelis wrote the important paper Towards a natural system of organisms: proposal for the domains Archaea, Bacteria, and Eucarya that proposed a change from the Two-empire system of Prokaryotes and Eukaryotes to the Three-domain system of the domains Eukaryota, Bacteria and Archaea .

[ 2 ] Wheelis's research interests include the history of biological warfare .

[ 1 ] He co-authored (with Larry Gonick ) The Cartoon Guide to Genetics (1983).  Wheelis provided the scientific knowledge and text, while Gonick contributed the illustrations and humor.
