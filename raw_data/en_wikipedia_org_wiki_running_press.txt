Publication types Books Imprints Black Dog & Leventhal Official website www .runningpress .com Running Press is an American publishing company and member of the Perseus Books Group , a division of the Hachette Book Group . The publisher's offices are located in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania , with many of the corporate functions taking place in Hachette's New York City headquarters. It was co-founded by Stuart "Buz" Teacher and his brother,  Lawrence "Larry" Teacher, who died in March 2014.

[ 1 ] Black Dog & Leventhal Publishers became an imprint of Running Press in 2017.
