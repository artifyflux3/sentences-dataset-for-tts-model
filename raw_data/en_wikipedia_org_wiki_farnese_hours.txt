Considered the masterpiece of Clovio, it is now in the Morgan Library & Museum in New York City.

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] It is often regarded as the last major manuscript book of hours.  It contains the usual texts and prayers, and illustrations with architectural borders and classical nudes.
