A ketenimine is a cumulated alkene and imine and is related to an allene and a ketene .

The parent compound is ketenimine or CH 2 CNH. The most recent work by Bane et al.

investigates the rovibrational structure of the ν 8 and ν 12 bands in the high-resolution FTIR spectrum, complementing the earlier analysis of the pure rotational spectrum. This pair of Coriolis coupled bands provide a rare example where intensity sharing between bands yields sufficient intensity for an otherwise invisible band (ν 12 ).
