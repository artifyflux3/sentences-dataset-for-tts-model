Howland Subject Archaea Publisher Oxford University Press Publication date 2000 The Surprising Archaea: Discovering Another Domain of Life is a popular science book written about the domain Archaea .  It was written by John L. Howland and first published in 2000 by the Oxford University Press .  The book records the, "archaeal rise from obscurity...to their current prominent place in molecular and evolutionary biology ."
