Various forms of racism are practiced in most countries on Earth.

[ 1 ] In individual countries, the forms of racism which are practiced may be motivated by historic, cultural, religious, economic or demographic reasons. Wars triggered sentiments of ultra-nationalism , ethnic pride and racism.

Racism is widely condemned throughout the world, and as a result, 89 states are signatories of the International Convention on the Elimination of All Forms of Racial Discrimination as of 7 March 2013 [update] .
