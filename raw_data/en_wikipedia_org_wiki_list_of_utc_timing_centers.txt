[ 1 ] Below are links to some of the more notable time centers which contribute to the calculation of UTC. UTC is calculated by the International Bureau of Weights and Measures (BIPM) [ 2 ] using weighted averages of the various times as reported by these 70+ listed timing centers.

[ 3 ] [ 4 ] BIPM lists the time differences between the UTC timing centers in a monthly publication called Circular T, which contains the most up to date list of contributors to 
UTC.
