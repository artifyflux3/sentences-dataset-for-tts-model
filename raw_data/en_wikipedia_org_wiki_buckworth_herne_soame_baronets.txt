It was created on 1 April 1697 for John Buckworth, High Sheriff of London in 1704.

[ 2 ] The second Baronet sat as Member of Parliament for Weobley . The third Baronet was Assistant Gentleman Usher to George II . The fifth Baronet was Gentleman-Pensioner and Exon of the Guard during the reign of George III . He married Anne, daughter of Paston Herne, of Haveringland Hall, Norfolk , and assumed by Royal licence the additional surname of Herne. The sixth Baronet assumed in 1806 by Royal licence the additional surname of Soame in compliance with the will of Sir Peter Soame, 4th Baronet, of Thurlow (see Soame baronets ). The ninth Baronet was a member of the Shropshire County Council .
