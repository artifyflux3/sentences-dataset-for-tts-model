Find sources: "Richard Cathcart" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( March 2019 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) Richard Brook Cathcart (born 1943) is an American geographer who specializes in macro-engineering .

He is co-author of Macro-Engineering: A Challenge for the Future, published 2007 by Springer Science & Business Media.
