[ 1 ] It was founded in January 1985 [ 2 ] to facilitate authors and publishers to register for ISBN in India.

[ 3 ] It gives free ISBN to Indian citizens .

[ 4 ] Online Verification and Authentication of ISBN Numbers [ edit ] ISBN issued by the agency can be verified on its website: https://isbn.gov.in/ There is also an old portal link for checking ISBN allotted before the revamping of the current website.
