District and municipality in Samsun, Turkey Terme District and municipality Bus station of Terme Map showing Terme District in Samsun Province Terme Location in Turkey Coordinates: 41°12′33″N 36°58′26″E ﻿ / ﻿ 41.20917°N 36.97389°E ﻿ / 41.20917; 36.97389 Country Turkey Province Samsun Government • Mayor Ali Kılıç ( AKP ) Area 548 km 2 (212 sq mi) Population (2022) [ 1 ] 71,092 • Density 130/km 2 (340/sq mi) Time zone UTC+3 ( TRT ) Postal code 55600 Area code 0362 Climate Cfa Website www .terme .bel .tr Terme (formerly spelled Termeh ; Ancient Greek : Thèrmae, Θέρμαι) is a municipality and district of Samsun Province , Turkey .

[ 2 ] Its area is 548 km 2 , [ 3 ] and its population is 71,092 (2022).

[ 1 ] Terme is located on Terme River , about 5 km from its mouth, on the eastern end of the Çarşamba Plain .

Terme or its environs are the site of the ancient city of Themiscyra , Θεμίσκυρα.

[ 4 ] Terme District is the site of an annual festival celebrating the Amazons , an ancient nation of all-female warriors who, according to Greek myth, were believed to have lived in the Samsun region.
