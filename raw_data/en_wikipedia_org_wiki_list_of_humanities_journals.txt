The list given here is far from exhaustive, and contains only the most influential, currently publishing journals in each field. As a rule of thumb, each field should be represented by about 5 examples, chosen for their current academic importance.

Note : there are many important academic magazines that are not true peer-reviewed journals. They are not listed here.
