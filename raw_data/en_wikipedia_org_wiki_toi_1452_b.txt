[ 2 ] [ 3 ] The exoplanet is about 70% larger in diameter than Earth, and roughly five times as massive.

[ 4 ] System [ edit ] The TOI-1452 star system is 99 light-years away from Earth, [ 5 ] located in the constellation of Draco . It is a binary pair of dim red dwarf stars separated by only 96 astronomical units (AU). A notable feature of this system is the presence of an exoplanet around one of the stars, designated as TOI-1452 b.

[ 6 ] It is two M4 dwarf stars that were observed by TESS as a priority, since they are on the cool dwarf list , a list of high-priority orange-red and red dwarf stars, that was uploaded to TESS. It is a flare star, with a flare observed by TESS where the star brightened by 5%. The secondary star is often distinguished from the first with the name " TOI-1760 ".
