Feature type Lacus Coordinates 78°19′N 97°32′W ﻿ / ﻿ 78.32°N 97.53°W ﻿ / 78.32; -97.53 Diameter 180 km [ note 1 ] Eponym Lake Mackay Mackay Lacus is the seventh largest of a number of hydrocarbon seas and lakes found on Saturn's largest moon, Titan . The lake is composed of liquid methane and ethane , [ 1 ] and was detected by the Cassini space probe .

Mackay Lacus is located at the coordinates 78.32° N and 97.53° W on Titan's globe and is 180 km in length.

[ 2 ] [ note 1 ] It is named after Lake Mackay in Western Australia .

[ 2 ] Notes [ edit ] ^ a b The USGS web site gives the size as a "diameter", but it is actually the length in the longest dimension.
