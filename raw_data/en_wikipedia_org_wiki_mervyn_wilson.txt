Wilson was born in 1922 in East Belfast .

[ 1 ] He was educated at Everton Elementary School and Sullivan Upper School , [ 2 ] followed by the University of Bristol and ordination training at Tyndale Hall , Bristol .

[ 3 ] He was ordained deacon in 1952 and priest in 1953.

[ 4 ] He was successively curate of Ballymacarrett (1952–56), [ 5 ] Donaghcloney (1956–59); and Newtownards (1959–61).

[ 6 ] He was then Rector of Ballyphilip with Ardquin (1961–70); St Philip's, Newry (1970–92); Rural Dean of Kilbroney and Newry and Mourne (1977–90); Prebendary of Dromore Cathedral (1983–85); Canon of Belfast Cathedral (1985–89); and finally Dean of Dromore (1990–92).

[ 7 ] He died in 2022, aged 100.
