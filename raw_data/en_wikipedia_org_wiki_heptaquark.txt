[ 1 ] [ 2 ] [ 3 ] [ 4 ] Properties [ edit ] One model predicts that the lowest-energy heptaquark state would be a spin-1/2 or spin-3/2 state of energy roughly 2.5 GeV.

[ 5 ] Another study found that the most stable heptaquark would include three strange quarks and two strange antiquarks.
