The Lars Onsager Prize is a prize in theoretical statistical physics awarded annually by the American Physical Society . Prize recipients receive a medal, certificate, and $10,000.

[ 1 ] It was established in 1993 by Drs. Russell and Marian Donnelly [ 2 ] in memory of Lars Onsager .
