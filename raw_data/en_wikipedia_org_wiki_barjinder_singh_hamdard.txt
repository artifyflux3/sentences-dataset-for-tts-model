[ 2 ] He is the son of famous Punjabi journalist and writer Sadhu Singh Hamdard .

Punjabi politics [ edit ] Hamdard led a coalition in opposition to Amarinder Singh after Singh took office and withdrew government advertising from Ajit newspapers.
