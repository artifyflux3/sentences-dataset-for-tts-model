[ 1 ] [ 2 ] Biography [ edit ] Francesco Bandini Piccolomini was born in 1505.

[ 2 ] On 7 April 1529, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Clement VII as Archbishop of Siena .

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] On 25 October 1538, he was consecrated bishop.

[ 2 ] He served as Archbishop of Siena until his death in 1588.
