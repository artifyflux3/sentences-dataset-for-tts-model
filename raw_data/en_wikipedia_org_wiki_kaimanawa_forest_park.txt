It is situated in the Rangitikei District and Manawatū-Whanganui Region of New Zealand's North Island .

[ 1 ] The park is managed by the New Zealand Department of Conservation .

[ 1 ] Geography [ edit ] The park covers 75,957 hectares (187,690 acres).

[ 2 ] History [ edit ] The park was established in 1969.
