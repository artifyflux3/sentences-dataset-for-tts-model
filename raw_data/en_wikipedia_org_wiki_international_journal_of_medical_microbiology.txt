44107614 Links Journal homepage Online access The International Journal of Medical Microbiology , formerly the Zentralblatt für Bakteriologie , is a peer-reviewed medical journal covering research on microbiology published by Elsevier .

[ 1 ] It was established in 1887 by Friedrich Loeffler .

[ 2 ] The current editor-in-chief is Sebastian Suerbaum (Max von Pettenkofer-Institut). According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2022 impact factor of 4.1.
