They reproduce by budding and are found in samples of algae in marine water .

[ 1 ] Organisms in this group are spherical [ 1 ] and have a holdfast , at the tip of a thin cylindrical extension from the cell body called the stalk, at the nonreproductive end that helps them to attach to each other during budding.
