[ 1 ] Originally without a home city for the Big3's first seven seasons, the Ball Hogs were announced to be based in Boston when the league switched to a city-based model for the 2025 season.

[ 2 ] In 2022, the Web3 Decentralized Autonomous Organization "Krause House" purchased all available ownership NFTs available through the Big3's sale.
