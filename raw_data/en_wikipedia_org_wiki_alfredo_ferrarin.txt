) Scuola Normale Superiore di Pisa ( PhD ) Thesis Hegel interprete di Aristotele (1990) Doctoral advisor Remo Bodei Other advisors Adriaan Peperzak Academic work Era Contemporary philosophy Region Western philosophy School or tradition German Idealism Institutions Scuola Normale Superiore di Pisa Alfredo Ferrarin (born 1960) is a professor of philosophy at the Scuola Normale Superiore di Pisa .

[ 1 ] He formerly was professor of philosophy at University of Pisa and Boston University .

[ 2 ] Life and work [ edit ] Ferrarin's 2001 book Hegel and Aristotle has been reviewed by Angelica Nuzzo , [ 3 ] Dermot Moran , [ 4 ] Franco Trabattoni, [ 5 ] L. De Vos [ 6 ] and Helen S. Lang [ 7 ] among others.
