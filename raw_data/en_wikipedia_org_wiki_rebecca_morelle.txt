Early life [ edit ] She attended Goffs School (now Goffs Academy ) in Cheshunt in Borough of Broxbourne , south-east Hertfordshire .

Career [ edit ] Morelle graduated from Oxford University in July 2001, with a first class degree in chemistry, and then worked as senior press officer at the Science Media Centre .

[ 1 ] In late 2005, Morelle was the first recipient of the Ivan Noble Bursary [ 2 ] at BBC News .
