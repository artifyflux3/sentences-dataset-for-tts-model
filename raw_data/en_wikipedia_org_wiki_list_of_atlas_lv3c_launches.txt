Find sources: "List of Atlas LV3C launches" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( May 2021 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) About the Atlas LV3C [ edit ] The Atlas-Centaur was a United States expendable launch vehicle derived from the SM-65 Atlas D missile. Launches were conducted from Launch Complex 36 at the Cape Canaveral Air Force Station (CCAFS) in Florida .
