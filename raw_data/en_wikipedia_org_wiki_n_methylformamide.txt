**_N_-Methylformamide** (NMF) is a colorless, nearly odorless, [organic compound](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Organic_compound "Organic compound") and [secondary amide](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Secondary_amide "Secondary amide") with molecular formula CH 3 NHCHO, which is a liquid at room temperature. NMF is mainly used as a reagent in various organic syntheses with limited applications as a highly [polar solvent](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Polar_solvent "Polar solvent").[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/N-Methylformamide#cite_note-NMF-1)

NMF is closely related to other formamides, notably [formamide](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Formamide "Formamide") and [dimethylformamide](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dimethylformamide "Dimethylformamide") (DMF). However, industrial use and production of NMF are far less than for either of these other formamides. DMF is favored over NMF as a [solvent](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Solvent "Solvent") due to its greater stability.[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/N-Methylformamide#cite_note-NMF-1) Annual production of NMF can be assumed to be significantly less than the production of either formamide (100,000 tons) or DMF (500,000 tons).[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/N-Methylformamide#cite_note-NMF-1)

Structure and properties
------------------------

[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=N-Methylformamide&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Structure and properties")]

Because of slow rotation about the N-C(O) bond, NMF exists as two rotamers that can be distinguished by [NMR spectroscopy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/NMR_spectroscopy "NMR spectroscopy"). The two principal [resonance structures](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Resonance_structure "Resonance structure") for one of these rotamers is shown:

[![Image 16: Principal resonance structures for one rotamer of NMF](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c8/NMFresonanceStr%27s.png/250px-NMFresonanceStr%27s.png)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:NMFresonanceStr%27s.png "Principal resonance structures for one rotamer of NMF")
This description highlights the partial [double bond](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Double_bond "Double bond") that exists between the [carbonyl](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carbonyl "Carbonyl") carbon and nitrogen, which raises the [rotational barrier](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rotational_barrier "Rotational barrier"). Thus, the molecule is not able to freely rotate around its main axis and the [(_E_)-configuration](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/E-configuration "E-configuration") is preferred due to [steric repulsion](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Steric_repulsion "Steric repulsion") of the larger substituents.[[2]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/N-Methylformamide#cite_note-2)

This molecule has been tentatively identified in [interstellar space](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Interstellar_space "Interstellar space") by the [ALMA](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Atacama_Large_Millimeter_Array "Atacama Large Millimeter Array") radio telescope. It may have formed on dust grains. This could prove to be a key molecule for interstellar pre-biotic chemistry due to its [peptide bond](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peptide_bond "Peptide bond").[[3]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/N-Methylformamide#cite_note-Belloche2017-3)

NMF is typically prepared by allowing [methylamine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Methylamine "Methylamine") to react with [methyl formate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Methyl_formate "Methyl formate"):[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/N-Methylformamide#cite_note-NMF-1)

CH 3 NH 2 + HCOOCH 3 → HCONHCH 3 + CH 3 OH
A less common alternative to this process is [transamidation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Transamidation "Transamidation") involving [formamide](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Formamide "Formamide"):[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/N-Methylformamide#cite_note-NMF-1)

HCONH 2 + CH 3 NH 2 → HCONHCH 3 + NH 3
NMF is a specialized solvent in oil refineries. It is a precursor in specialized amidation reactions where formamide would not be suitable. These reactions can generally be categorized by the following equation:

R-Lg + CH 3 NHCHO → R-NCH 3 CHO + H-Lg (Where Lg is a [leaving group](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leaving_group "Leaving group")).[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/N-Methylformamide#cite_note-NMF-1)[![Image 17](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/a4/Formothion.svg/120px-Formothion.svg.png)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Formothion.svg)

[Formothion](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Formothion "Formothion") is an insecticide prepared from N-methylformamide.

NMF is the precursor to [methyl isocyanide](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Methyl_isocyanide "Methyl isocyanide"), a ligand in [coordination chemistry](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coordination_chemistry "Coordination chemistry").[[4]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/N-Methylformamide#cite_note-4) NMF is used as a solvent in aluminum electrolytic capacitors.
