When full, the lake would be composed of liquid methane and ethane .

[ 1 ] It was detected in 2007 by the Cassini–Huygens space probe .

Ngami Lacuna is located at coordinates 66.7° N and 213.9° W on Titan's globe and is 37.2 km in diameter .

[ 2 ] It is named after Lake Ngami , in Botswana , [ 2 ] and like its terrestrial namesake is considered to be endorheic .
