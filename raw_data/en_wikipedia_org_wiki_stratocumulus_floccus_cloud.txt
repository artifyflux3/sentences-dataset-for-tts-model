Find sources: "Stratocumulus floccus cloud" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( February 2022 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) Stratocumulus floccus Stratocumulus floccus Abbreviation Sc flo [ 1 ] Genus Stratocumulus Species Floccus Altitude 500-2,000 m (2,000-6,500 [ 2 ] ft) Classification Family C (Low-level) Appearance Tufted, somewhat ragged low-level cloud [ 3 ] Precipitation Uncommon, usually Virga [ 1 ] Stratocumulus floccus is a species of cloud belonging to Stratocumulus cloud genus. Stratocumulus floccus is usually a sign of instability at that altitude.

[ 1 ] This cloud is usually formed due to the remaining dissipation of a stratocumulus castellanus cloud base.
