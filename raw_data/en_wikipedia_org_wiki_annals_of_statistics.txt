Indexing CODEN ( alt · alt2 ) · JSTOR ( alt ) · LCCN ( alt ) MIAR · NLM ( alt ) · Scopus · W&L ISSN 0090-5364 JSTOR 00905364 Links Journal homepage Project Euclid The Annals of Statistics is a peer-reviewed statistics journal published by the Institute of Mathematical Statistics . It was started in 1973 as a continuation in part of the Annals of Mathematical Statistics (1930) , which was split into the Annals of Statistics and the Annals of Probability .

[ 1 ] The journal CiteScore is 5.8, and its SCImago Journal Rank is 5.877, both from 2020.

[ 2 ] Articles older than 3 years are available on JSTOR , and all articles since 2004 are freely available on the arXiv .
