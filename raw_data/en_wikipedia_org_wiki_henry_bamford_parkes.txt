He was born in Sheffield, England .

Background [ edit ] After reading history at Oxford University , Parkes came to the United States to pursue his graduate studies at the University of Michigan . He received his Ph.D. from Michigan in 1929 and joined the history faculty of New York University in 1930. He had also lectured at Barnard College , the University of Wyoming , the New School for Social Research and the University of Washington . From 1956 to 1957, Parkes was a Fulbright Fellow , working at the University of Athens in Greece .
