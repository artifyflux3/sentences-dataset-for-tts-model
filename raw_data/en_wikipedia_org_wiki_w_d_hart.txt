He taught at the University of Michigan from 1969 to 1974, the University College London from 1974 to 1991, and the University of New Mexico from 1992 to 1993.

[ 1 ] Hart is known for his research on logic , philosophy of mathematics , metaphysics , and epistemology .

[ 2 ] [ 3 ] Hart has defended substance dualism .
