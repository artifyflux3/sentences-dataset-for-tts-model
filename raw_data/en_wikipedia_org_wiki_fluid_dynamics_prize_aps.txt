Find sources: "Fluid Dynamics Prize" APS – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( January 2020 ) Award of the American Physical Society The Fluid Dynamics Prize is a prize that has been awarded annually by the American Physical Society (APS) since 1979. The recipient is chosen for "outstanding achievement in fluid dynamics research". The prize is currently valued at US$10,000 . In 2004, the Otto Laporte Award —another APS award on fluid dynamics—was merged into the Fluid Dynamics Prize.
