[ 1 ] On 29 January 2022 with an observation arc of 22 days it was rated with a Torino scale of 1 for a virtual impactor on 11 July 2061 21:22 UTC .

[ 3 ] The 2061 virtual impactor was ruled out on 9 February 2022 with a 32.9 day observation arc.

Nominal approach is expected to occur 18 June 2061.
