F8 15 November 1999 07:29 H-II Tanegashima , LA-Y1 MTSAT (Multi-functional Transport Satellite) GEO Failure Cavitation in the first stage hydrogen turbopump impeller caused an impeller blade to fracture, resulting in loss of fuel and rapid shutdown of the engine at T+239 seconds. The vehicle impacted the ocean 380 km at the northwest of Chichijima .
