2449902 Links Journal homepage Online access Progress of Theoretical and Experimental Physics is a monthly peer-reviewed scientific journal published by Oxford University Press on behalf of the Physical Society of Japan .

[ 1 ] It was established as Progress of Theoretical Physics in July 1946 by Hideki Yukawa and obtained its current name in January 2013.

The journal is part of the SCOAP 3 initiative.
