2023 Microcaldus is a monotypic genus of ultra-small archaea . It contains one species , Microcaldus variisymbioticus . It is in the monotypic family Microcaldaceae , monotypic order Microcaldales , monotypic class Microcaldia and monotypic phylum Microcaldota .
