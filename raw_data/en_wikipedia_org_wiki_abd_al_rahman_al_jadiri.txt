[ 1 ] He wrote a treatise on the determination of time of day and night, an urzija (verse composition) in 26 chapters and 335 verses, entitled Raudat al-azhar fl 'ilm waqt al-lail wal-nahar (1391/2), and a calendar adapted to the latitude of Fez , Tanbllt al-afliím 'ala mii ya/:tduthu ji ayyiim al-'iim .
