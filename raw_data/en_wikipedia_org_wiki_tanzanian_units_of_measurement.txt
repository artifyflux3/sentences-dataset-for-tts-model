[ 1 ] System before metric system [ edit ] A number of units were used.

Zanzibar [ edit ] Several units were used in Zanzibar Length [ edit ] One ohra was equal to 0.571 m (22.48 in).

[ 2 ] Weight [ edit ] One bazla was equal to 15.525 kg (32.226 lb).

[ 2 ] One mane was equal to 2.0071 lb.

[ 2 ] One franzella of 36 rotoli was equal to 35.2822 lb.

[ 2 ] Capacity [ edit ] One djezla was equal to 257.4 L (7.305 bushels).
