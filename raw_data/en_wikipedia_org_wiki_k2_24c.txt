[ 1 ] [ 2 ] Discovery site Kepler Space Observatory Discovery date Nov. 17, 2015 Detection method Transit Orbital characteristics Semi-major axis 0.247 (± 0.004) [ 1 ] AU Eccentricity 0 [ 1 ] Orbital period (sidereal) 42.3633 (± 0.0006) [ 1 ] d Inclination 89.76 (± 0.2) [ 1 ] Star K2-24 Physical characteristics Mean radius 7.82 ± 0.72 [ 2 ] R 🜨 Mass 27.0 ± 6.9 [ 2 ] M 🜨 Mean density 0.31 ± 0.12 g/cm 3 Temperature 606.0 K (332.9 °C) K2-24c [ 1 ] also known as EPIC 203771098 c is an exoplanet orbiting the Sun-like star K2-24 every 42 days. It has a density far lower than that of Saturn , [ 2 ] which indicates that the planet is clearly a gas giant .
