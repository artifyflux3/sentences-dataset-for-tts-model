**Spaces** may refer to:

*   Google [Spaces (app)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spaces_(app) "Spaces (app)"), a cross-platform application for group messaging and sharing
*   [Windows Live Spaces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Windows_Live_Spaces "Windows Live Spaces"), the next generation of MSN Spaces
*   [Spaces (software)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spaces_(software) "Spaces (software)"), a virtual desktop manager implemented in Mac OS X Leopard
*   [Spaces (social network)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spaces_(social_network) "Spaces (social network)"), a Russian social network for mobile phones
*   [Gaps](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaps "Gaps"), a solitaire card game
*   _[Spaces: The Architecture of Paul Rudolph](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spaces:\_The\_Architecture\_of\_Paul\_Rudolph "Spaces: The Architecture of Paul Rudolph")_, a 1983 documentary film
*   [IWG plc](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IWG_plc "IWG plc"), parent company of the Spaces [coworking](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coworking "Coworking") office workspace chain
*   [Twitter Spaces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Twitter_Spaces "Twitter Spaces"), a social audio feature in Twitter
