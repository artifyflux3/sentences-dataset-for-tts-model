1026611551 Links Journal homepage Online archive International Microbiology is a quarterly peer-reviewed scientific journal published by Springer Science+Business Media and the official journal of the Spanish Society of Microbiology [ es ] . It covers all aspects of microbiology .

[ 1 ] The journal was established in 1947 as Microbiología Española [ 2 ] and renamed Microbiología SEM in 1985 [ 3 ] before obtaining its current title in 1998.

[ 4 ] Abstracting and indexing [ edit ] The journal is abstracted and indexed in Science Citation Index Expanded , Scopus , Biological Abstracts , MEDLINE , and Excerpta Medica . According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2023 impact factor of 2.3.
