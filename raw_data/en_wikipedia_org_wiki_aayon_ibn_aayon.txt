[ 1 ] He moved from Kairouan to Egypt with Al-Mu'izz li-Din Allah , [ 2 ] and he was known in Egypt during the caliphate of Al-Aziz Billah .

[ 3 ] His most notable work is Of eye diseases and their treatment ( Arabic : في أمراض العين ومداواتها ).

[ 1 ] Ibn Aayon was mentioned on Uyūn ul-Anbāʾ fī Ṭabaqāt al-Aṭibbā ( Arabic : عيون الأنباء في طبقات الأطباء ), a book written by Ibn Abi Usaibia .
