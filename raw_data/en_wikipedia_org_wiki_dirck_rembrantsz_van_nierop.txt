[ 1 ] [ 2 ] Life [ edit ] Van Nierop was born and died at Nieuwe Niedorp ("Nierop"), Holland (now in North Holland ). Several times he visited René Descartes in Egmond-Binnen . The philosopher was so impressed by Van Nierop's knowledge that he even brought him to the attention of Constantijn Huygens and Frans van Schooten .

[ 3 ] He has more than thirty scientific publications to his name. He also has many designs of sundials to his name.  In addition he was a consultant in the field of navigational charts and active for the Dutch VOC.
