The publisher in lieu is Danielle Loughlin.

The Trends series was established in 1976 with Trends in Biochemical Sciences , rapidly followed by Trends in Neurosciences , Trends in Pharmacological Sciences , and Immunology Today .

Immunology Today , Parasitology Today , and Molecular Medicine Today changed their names to Trends in...

in 2001.

Drug Discovery Today was spun off as an independent brand.
