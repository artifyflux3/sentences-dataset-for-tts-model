Michael Dominic Richard Guiry (born 1949), is an Irish botanist, [ 1 ] who specialises in phycology (algae). See for example the articles.

[ 2 ] [ 3 ] [ 4 ] [ 5 ] He is the founder and director of the algal database, AlgaeBase .

[ 6 ] He is a graduate of both University College Cork and the University of London . In addition to his interest in the taxonomy and the databasing of algae, his algal site promotes the sustainable use of seaweed resources.

[ 6 ] Since 2009, he has been an emeritus professor at the Ryan Institute, National University of Ireland , in Galway .

[ 7 ] The standard author abbreviation Guiry is used to indicate this person as the author when citing a botanical name .
