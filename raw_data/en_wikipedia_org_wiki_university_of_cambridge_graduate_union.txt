[ 1 ] Until 2020, it was responsible for supporting graduate students and advocating issues at the University of Cambridge via University committees and beyond.

[ 2 ] In November 2019, students voted by referendum to dissolve both the Graduate Union and Cambridge University Students' Union to form one student union, Cambridge SU.

[ 3 ] The Union dissolved in July 2020.

[ 4 ] The University of Cambridge Graduate Union was the first students' union in Britain catering mostly to graduates.

[ citation needed ] It published an annual Handbook with useful information on adjusting to life as a Cambridge graduate student, an online 'Alternative Prospectus' of the University of Cambridge (mostly for new members arriving in Michaelmas Term) and a weekly bulletin circulated to members throughout the year.
