Indexing CODEN ( alt · alt2 ) · JSTOR ( alt ) · LCCN ( alt ) MIAR · NLM ( alt ) · Scopus · W&L ISSN 0079-6425 Links Journal page Journal access Progress in Materials Science is a journal publishing review articles covering most areas of materials science , published by the Pergamon imprint of Elsevier . It was started in 1949 with the title Progress in Metal Physics with Bruce Chalmers serving as first editor. It was changed to the current title in 1961.
