Find sources: "Martin Brendel" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( May 2008 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) Otto Rudolf Martin Brendel (12 August 1862 – 6 September 1939) was a German astronomer . Born in Berlin-Niederschönhausen , he obtained the first successful photograph of the aurora borealis at Bossekop in northern Norway in 1892.

[ 1 ] He died in Freiburg .

The asteroid 761 Brendelia is named after Brendel.
