50515202 Links Journal homepage Online access Online archive The International Journal of Astrobiology (IJA) is a peer-reviewed scientific journal established in 2002 and published by Cambridge University Press that covers research on the prebiotic chemistry, origin , evolution , distribution, and future of life on Earth and beyond, SETI ( Search for extraterrestrial intelligence ), societal and educational aspects of astrobiology. It also contains papers in astronomy , space science , planetary science , and biology that have a strong connection to astrobiology . Occasional issues are dedicated to research papers from international astrobiology meetings. The editor-in-chief is Rocco Mancinelli ( NASA ). According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2019 impact factor of 2.026.
