Cockroach sent into space by Russia

**Nadezhda** ([Russian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russian_language "Russian language"): Надежда, **Hope**) was a [cockroach](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cockroach "Cockroach") that was sent into space during the [Foton-M 3](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Foton_(satellite) "Foton (satellite)") bio-satellite flight between September 14 and 26, 2007 by [Russian](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russia "Russia") scientists. Scientists monitoring the mission from [Voronezh](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Voronezh "Voronezh") announced that Nadezhda had successfully produced 33 offspring on Earth.[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nadezhda_(cockroach)#cite_note-1)[[2]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nadezhda_(cockroach)#cite_note-:0-2) While it was reported that Nadezhda's offspring were the first earthlings to be conceived in [microgravity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Weightlessness "Weightlessness"), [Japanese rice fish](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japanese_rice_fish "Japanese rice fish") successfully conceived and produced offspring in microgravity as part of the IML-2 experiment aboard [STS-65](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/STS-65 "STS-65") in 1994.[[3]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nadezhda_(cockroach)#cite_note-3)[[4]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nadezhda_(cockroach)#cite_note-4) Nadezhda and the rest of the insects were traveling inside a sealed special container, and a video-camera was filming the whole process.[[5]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nadezhda_(cockroach)#cite_note-en-5)

What was considered unnatural for the newborn cockroaches was that their [carapace](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carapace "Carapace") had darkened in colour much earlier, in comparison with natural-condition cockroaches who develop that darker tone later in their life cycle. But the rest of the conditions and capacities of the cockroaches remained normal.[[2]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nadezhda_(cockroach)#cite_note-:0-2) Later it was reported that Nadezhda's grandchildren, born to one of the space-born insects, had given birth on Earth to normal cockroaches, with a life cycle and development similar to that of any other cockroach.[[6]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nadezhda_(cockroach)#cite_note-6)
