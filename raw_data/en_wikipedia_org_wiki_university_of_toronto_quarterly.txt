Indexing CODEN ( alt · alt2 ) · JSTOR ( alt ) · LCCN ( alt ) MIAR · NLM ( alt ) · Scopus · W&L ISSN 0042-0247 (print) 1712-5278 (web) Links Journal homepage The University of Toronto Quarterly , also abbreviated as UTQ , [ 1 ] is an interdisciplinary academic journal of the humanities published by the University of Toronto Press . It was established in 1931 under the editorship of the philosopher George Sidney Brett .

[ 2 ] [ 3 ] The current editor-in-chief is Colin Hill. The journal accepts submissions in either English or French. The journal is abstracted and indexed in Academic Search , the Arts and Humanities Citation Index , CrossRef , Current Contents , EBSCO databases , MLA International Bibliography , Project MUSE , Scopus , and Ulrich's Periodicals Directory .
