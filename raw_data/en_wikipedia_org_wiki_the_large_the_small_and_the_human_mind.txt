ISBN 978-0521785723 The Large, the Small, and the Human Mind is a popular science book by British theoretical physicist Roger Penrose .  The book was published by Cambridge University Press in 1997.

Overview [ edit ] The book includes criticism of his work on physics and consciousness by Abner Shimony , Nancy Cartwright , and Stephen Hawking .

[ 1 ] The book was preceded by The Emperor's New Mind , published in 1989, and Shadows of the Mind , published in 1994.
