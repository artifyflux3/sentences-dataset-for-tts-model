Hindbrain (rhombencephalon) [ edit ] Main article: Hindbrain Embryonic vertebrate subdivisions of the developing human brain hindbrain or rhombencephalon is a developmental categorization of portions of the central nervous system in vertebrates . It includes the medulla , pons , and cerebellum .
