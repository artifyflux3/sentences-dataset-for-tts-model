2017 Vulcanimicrobiota is a monotypic phylum of incertae sedis bacteria . It has one valid class Vulcanimicrobiia .

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] Vulcanimicrobiota is one of the two valid bacterial phyla that does not belong to any kingdom , the other being Fidelibacterota .
