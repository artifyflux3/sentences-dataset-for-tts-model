[ 1 ] These may be pure elements, but more commonly are metallic alloys .

Lithium and then potassium are the two lightest metallic elements.

Magnesium , aluminium and titanium alloys are light metals of significant commercial importance.

[ 2 ] Their densities of 1.7, 2.7 and 4.5 g/cm 3 range from 19 to 56% of the densities of other structural metals, [ 3 ] such as iron (7.9) and copper (8.9).
