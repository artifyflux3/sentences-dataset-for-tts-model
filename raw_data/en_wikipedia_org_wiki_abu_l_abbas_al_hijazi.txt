[ 1 ] His seven sons whom he positioned in seven different commercial centers from his base in Yemen , enhanced his trading business by sending goods from foreign markets.

[ 1 ] He had lost 10 ships in the Indian Ocean but recovered his fortunes when his 11th ship safely arrived from China which carried porcelain and aloewood .
