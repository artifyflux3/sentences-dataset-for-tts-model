[ 1 ] Currently, this class is represented by a single order of bacteria, with only one family to which belongs a genus ( Coprothermobacter ) of non-motile, rod-shaped microorganisms that stain Gram-negative, are non-spore-forming, strictly anaerobic , thermophilic and chemoautotroph .
