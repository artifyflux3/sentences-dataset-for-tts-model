Life [ edit ] Born in 1902, he was educated at Trinity College, Dublin and ordained in 1926.

[ 3 ] Hayes was a curate at Derry Cathedral , the rector of Donegal from 1930 to 1938 and then of Conwal and Leck [ 4 ] until his appointment to the deanery.
