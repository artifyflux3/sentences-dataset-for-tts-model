Find sources: "Institute for Manufacturing" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( January 2025 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) The Institute for Manufacturing ( IfM ) is part of the Department of Engineering of the University of Cambridge .

Location [ edit ] The IfM is located in the Alan Reece building on the University's West Cambridge Site [ 1 ] in the United Kingdom . Previously, the Institute was located in the former Cambridge University Press building in Mill Lane before relocating to its current building in West Cambridge in June 2009.
