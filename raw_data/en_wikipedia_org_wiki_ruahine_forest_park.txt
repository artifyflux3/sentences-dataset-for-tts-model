[ 1 ] The park is managed by the New Zealand Department of Conservation .

[ 1 ] Geography [ edit ] The park covers 95,268 hectares (235,410 acres) in and around the Ruahine Ranges .

[ 2 ] [ 1 ] History [ edit ] The park was established in 1976.
