Find sources: "List of Thor DM-18 launches" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( December 2021 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) Thor DM-18 with Pioneer 1 at Cape Canaveral in Florida Thor was a US space launch vehicle derived from the PGM-17 Thor intermediate-range ballistic missile . The Thor rocket was the first member of the Delta rocket family of space launch vehicles.
