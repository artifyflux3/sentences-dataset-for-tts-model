Gilg 1924 Genera [ 1 ] [ 2 ] Drechslerosporium Schizangiella Basidiobolus The Basidiobolaceae are a family of fungi in the monotypic order Basidiobolales .

[ 3 ] All fungal cells of this family are exclusively uninucleate and their relatively large nuclei contain a nucleolus, but no heterochromatin.

[ 4 ] They were formerly in the order Entomophthorales .

[ 5 ] The transfer occurred in 2018.
