[ 2 ] Cartographica is published four times a year by the University of Toronto Press .

[ 1 ] The journal was first published in 1965 as The Cartographer , was renamed The Canadian Cartographer in 1968 and was renamed Cartographica in 1980. It is an international and interdisciplinary peer-reviewed academic journal that publishes transformative research, education, and practice contributions to the social, political, technological, and historical aspects of cartography and geovisualization.
