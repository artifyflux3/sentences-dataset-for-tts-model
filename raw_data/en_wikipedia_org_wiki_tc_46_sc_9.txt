The scope of the committee is 'standardization of practices relating to libraries , documentation and information centres , publishing , archives , records management , museum documentation, indexing and abstracting services , and information science '.

[ 1 ] Leadership [ edit ] Since 2022, the Technical Committee Chair is Grégory Miura, from France.

[ 2 ] For 2018–2022, the chair was Gaëlle Béquet, from France, who served two term as chairperson (the first was 2014–2017).

[ 3 ] Françoise Pellé, also French, served as chair from 2008 to 2014.

[ 4 ] Subcommittees [ edit ] Most work of the Technical Committee is done by Subcommittees (SC) dealing with a particular field.
