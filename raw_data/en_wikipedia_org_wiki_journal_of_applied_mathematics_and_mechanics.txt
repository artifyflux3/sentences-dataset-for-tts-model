01590219 Links Journal homepage Journal at Wiley Online Library The Journal of Applied Mathematics and Mechanics , also known as Zeitschrift für Angewandte Mathematik und Mechanik or ZAMM is a monthly peer-reviewed scientific journal dedicated to applied mathematics . It is published by Wiley-VCH on behalf of the Gesellschaft für Angewandte Mathematik und Mechanik . The editor-in-chief is Holm Altenbach ( Otto von Guericke University Magdeburg ).

[ 1 ] According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2022 impact factor of 2.3.

[ 2 ] Publication history [ edit ] The journal's first issue appeared in 1921, published by the Verein Deutscher Ingenieure and edited by Richard von Mises .
