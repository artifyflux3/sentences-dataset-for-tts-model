The Global Northwest constitutes Europe (sometimes considered to only include Western Europe ) [ 1 ] and the Western countries of North America with Western countries in Oceania sometimes included.

[ 2 ] [ 3 ] [ 4 ] [ 5 ] Much of modern scholarship around various topics has centered around the Global Northwest, to the detriment of understanding other parts of the world.
