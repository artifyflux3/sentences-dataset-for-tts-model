Find sources: "Metallotolerant" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( April 2017 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) Metallotolerants are extremophile organisms that are able to survive in environments with a high concentration of dissolved heavy metals .  They can be found in environments containing arsenic , cadmium , copper , and zinc .  Known metallotolerants include Ferroplasma sp. and Cupriavidus metallidurans .

Metallotolerants adapt to their environment by reducing energy loss by excreting less. Many metallotolerant microbes utilise strategies to perform bioremediation, which is seen as a productive way of survival.

[ 1 ] Sinorhizobium sp. M14 is a metallotolerant bacterium.

[ 2 ] Plants can also survive in highly metallic conditions.

[ 3 ] For example, Noccaea caerulescens is a metallotolerent plant.
