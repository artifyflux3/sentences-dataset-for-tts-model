2012 " Candidatus Acetithermum" [ 1 ] is a thermophilic [ 2 ] bacterial genus , belonging to the monotypic phylum " Candidatus Acetithermota" [ 3 ] and non-monotypic kingdom Thermotogati . It has the sole species " Candidatus Acetithermum autotrophicum" .
