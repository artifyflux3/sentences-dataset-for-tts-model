↦ Maps to The maps to symbol, ↦, is a rightward arrow protruding from a vertical bar.  It is used in mathematics and in computer science to denote functions . In Z notation , a specification language used in software development, [ 1 ] this symbol is called the maplet arrow and the expression x ↦ y is called a maplet . In separation logic it denotes the contents of a specific cell of memory.

In the Unicode character set, the symbol is code point U+21A6.
