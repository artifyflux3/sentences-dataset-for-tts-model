NRSC manages data from aerial and satellite sources.

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] Finances [ edit ] NRSC is mostly financed by State and Central Government India funds but also do commercial business by selling satellite images and provide Consultancy to various government, private organisations. According to RTI number: NRESC/R/E/20/00021, revenues and grants values are published for 2014 to 2019 year.

[ citation needed ] In 2025 Government of India announced ₹ 1,706.79 crore, for supporting centers.
