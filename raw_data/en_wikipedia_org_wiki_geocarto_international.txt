[ 1 ] It focuses on remote sensing , GIS , geoscience and environmental sciences . Its editor-in-chief is Kamlesh Lulla. The 2019-2020 Journal Impact IF of Geocarto International is 4.889, [ 2 ] which is updated in 2020.
