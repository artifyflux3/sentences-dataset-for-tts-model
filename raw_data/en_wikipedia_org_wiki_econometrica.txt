01567366 Links Journal homepage Online access Online archive Econometrica is a peer-reviewed academic journal of economics , publishing articles in many areas of economics , especially econometrics . It is published by Wiley-Blackwell on behalf of the Econometric Society . The current editor-in-chief is Marina Halac .

History [ edit ] Econometrica was established in 1933. Its first editor was Ragnar Frisch , recipient of the first Nobel Memorial Prize in Economic Sciences in 1969, who served as an editor from 1933 to 1954. Although Econometrica is currently published entirely in English, the first few issues also contained scientific articles written in French.

Indexing and abstracting [ edit ] Econometrica is abstracted and indexed in: Scopus [ 1 ] EconLit [ 2 ] Social Sciences Citation Index According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2020 impact factor of 5.844, ranking it 22/557 in the category "Economics".

[ 3 ] Awards issued [ edit ] The Econometric Society aims to attract high-quality applied work in economics for publication in Econometrica through the Frisch Medal . This prize is awarded every two years for an empirical or theoretical applied article published in Econometrica during the past five years.
