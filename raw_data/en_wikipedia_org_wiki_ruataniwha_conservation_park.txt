[ 1 ] The park is managed by the New Zealand Department of Conservation .

[ 1 ] Geography [ edit ] The park covers 37,145 hectares (91,790 acres), including Dobson Valley, Hopkins Valley, Huxley Valley, Temple Valley and Maitland  Valley and the Ben Ohau Range .

[ 2 ] [ 1 ] History [ edit ] The park was established in 2006.
