[ 1 ] In gardening , the term free-flowering is used to describe flowering plants that have a long bloom time and may often lack a defined blooming season , whereby producing flowers profusely over an extended period of time, at times all-year round.

[ 2 ] The terms long-flowering and long-blooming are also used for perennial plants that bloom for much of the year.

[ 3 ] Examples [ edit ] Examples of free-flowering or long flowering plants include salvias , [ 4 ] [ 5 ] thunbergias , [ 6 ] loniceras , [ 7 ] roses , [ 8 ] lavenders , [ 9 ] periwinkles , [ 10 ] gaillardias , [ 11 ] oleanders , [ 12 ] begonias , [ 13 ] bougainvilleas , [ 14 ] morning glories , [ 15 ] geraniums / pelargoniums , [ 16 ] hibiscuses , [ 17 ] and lantanas .
