Euglypha Scientific classification Domain: Eukaryota (unranked): SAR (unranked): Rhizaria Phylum: Cercozoa Class: Imbricatea Order: Euglyphida Family: Euglyphidae Genus: Euglypha Dujardin, 1840 Euglypha is a genus of cercozoa . It includes the species Euglypha rotunda .
