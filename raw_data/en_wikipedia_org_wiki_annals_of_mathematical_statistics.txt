Indexing CODEN ( alt · alt2 ) · JSTOR ( alt ) · LCCN ( alt ) MIAR · NLM ( alt ) · Scopus · W&L ISSN 0003-4851 JSTOR 00034851 Links Journal homepage The Annals of Mathematical Statistics was a peer-reviewed statistics journal published by the Institute of Mathematical Statistics from 1930 to 1972. It was superseded by the Annals of Statistics and the Annals of Probability .  In 1938, Samuel Wilks became editor-in-chief of the Annals and recruited a remarkable editorial staff: Fisher , Neyman , Cramér , Hotelling , Egon Pearson , Georges Darmois , Allen T. Craig, Deming , von Mises , H. L. Rietz , and Shewhart .
