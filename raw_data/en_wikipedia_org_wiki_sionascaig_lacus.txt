Feature type Lacus Coordinates 42°31′34″S 278°07′12″W ﻿ / ﻿ 42.526°S 278.12°W ﻿ / -42.526; -278.12 Diameter 200km [ 1 ] Eponym Loch Sionascaig Sionascaig Lacus is one of a number of hydrocarbon seas and lakes found on Saturn's largest moon, Titan .

[ 2 ] [ 3 ] The lake is located at latitude 41.52° S and longitude 278.12° W on Titan 's globe, and is composed of liquid methane and ethane .

[ 4 ] [ 5 ] The feature is named for the Earth lake, Loch Sionascaig , in Scotland .
