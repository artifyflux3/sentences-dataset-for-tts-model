Triangulation is the process of determining the location of a point by forming triangles to it from known points.

Triangulation may also refer to: Arts, entertainment, and media [ edit ] Triangulation (chess) , a maneuver in which one player moves a piece (often a king) in a triangular pattern in order to force the opponent to make a weakening move Triangulation (TWiT.tv) , an interview podcast Triangulation (novel) , a 1999 novel by Phil Whitaker Mathematics and technology [ edit ] Graph theory [ edit ]
