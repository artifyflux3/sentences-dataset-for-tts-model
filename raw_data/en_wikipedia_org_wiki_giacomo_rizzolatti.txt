Born in Kyiv , UkSSR , he is the Senior Scientist of the research team that discovered mirror neurons in the frontal and parietal cortex of the macaque monkey , and has written many scientific articles on the topic. He also proposed the premotor theory of attention .

[ 2 ] He is a past president of the European Brain and Behaviour Society . Rizzolatti was the 2007 co-recipient, with Leonardo Fogassi and Vittorio Gallese, for the University of Louisville Grawemeyer Award for Psychology.

[ 3 ] He is an elected member of the Academia Europaea , [ 4 ] National Academy of Sciences , [ 5 ] and Royal Society [ 4 ] Awards [ edit ] 2011 Prince of Asturias Award for Technical and Scientific Research.
