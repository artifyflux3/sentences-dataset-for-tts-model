Feature type Lacus Coordinates 48°36′N 308°17′W ﻿ / ﻿ 48.6°N 308.29°W ﻿ / 48.6; -308.29 Diameter 200 km [ note 1 ] Eponym Hammar Lake Hammar Lacus is one of a number of hydrocarbon seas and lakes found on Saturn's largest moon, Titan .

[ 1 ] The lake is located at latitude 48.6° N and longitude 308.29° W on Titan 's globe, [ 2 ] and is composed of liquid methane and ethane , [ 3 ] At 200 km in diameter it is the third largest lake on Titan and is named after Hammar Lake in southern Iraq , its name being adopted by the IAU on Dec 3, 2013.

[ 2 ] Its area is given as 18600 km 2 .

[ 4 ] Notes [ edit ] ^ The USGS web site gives the size as a "diameter", but it is actually the length in the longest dimension.
