It is centered at 40°S 10°W ﻿ / ﻿ 40°S 10°W ﻿ / -40; -10 .

[ 1 ] Tsegihi is named for a sacred place of the Navajo . The first line in the Navajo Nightsong Tsegihi, The House Made of Dawn runs: [ 2 ] In Tsegihi, oh you who dwell In the house made of the dawn...
