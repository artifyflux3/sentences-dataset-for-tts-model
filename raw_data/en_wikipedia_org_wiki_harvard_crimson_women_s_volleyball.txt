Harvard competes as a member of the Ivy League and plays its home games at the Malkin Athletic Center (MAC) in Cambridge, Massachusetts .

[ 2 ] History [ edit ] Harvard's first team took the court in 1981. The Crimson has won the Ivy League championship once in 2004.
