780556277 Links Journal homepage Online archive Nature Reviews Microbiology is a monthly peer-reviewed review journal published by Nature Portfolio . It was established in 2003.

[ 1 ] The journal publishes reviews and perspectives on microbiology , bridging fundamental research and its clinical, industrial, and environmental applications. The editor-in-chief is Ashley York.

[ 2 ] Abstracting and indexing [ edit ] This journal is indexed and abstracted by the following databases: BIOSIS Previews [ 3 ] Current Contents /Life Sciences [ 3 ] Science Citation Index [ 3 ] Medline [ 4 ] PubMed [ 4 ] Index Medicus [ 4 ] Other services that index this journal are: Scopus , Academic Search Premier , Biotechnology Research Abstracts, CAB Abstracts , Chemical Abstracts Service , and EMBASE .

[ 5 ] According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2023 impact factor of 69.2, ranking it 1st out of 135 journals in the "Microbiology" category.
