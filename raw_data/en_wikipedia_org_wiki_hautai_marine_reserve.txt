[ 1 ] It is the country's most remote mainland main reserve, located south of Haast , a two day walk from the nearest road, in an area with no walking tracks.

[ 1 ] The reserve was established to protect marine habitats and animals of the southern West Coast. Marine mammals like New Zealand fur seals , Fiordland crested penguins and blue penguins are abundant.
