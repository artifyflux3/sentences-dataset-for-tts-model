In botany and plant taxonomy , a series is a subdivision of a genus , a taxonomic rank below that of section (and subsection ) but above that of species .

[ 1 ] Sections and/or series are typically used to help organize very large genera , which may have hundreds of species.

Cultivar marketing [ edit ] The term "series" is also used (in seed marketing) for groupings of cultivars , but this term has no formal status with that meaning in the ICNCP .
