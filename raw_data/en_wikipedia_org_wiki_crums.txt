2018 [ 1 ] Orders Diphylleida Rigifilida Mantamonadida Glissandrida Synonyms Crumalia Zmitrovich, Perelygin & Zharikov 2022 [ 2 ] Varisulca Cavalier-Smith 2013 emend. 2021 [ 3 ] CRuMs is a clade of microbial eukaryotes , whose name is an acronym of the following constituent groups: Collodictyonids , rigifilids , and mantamonads as sister of the Amorphea .

[ 4 ] [ 1 ] A new CRuMs order Glissandrida was proposed in 2025 to place the yet unclassified genus Glissandra .

[ 5 ] It more or less supersedes Varisulca , as Ancyromonadida are inferred not to be specifically related to the orders Diphylleida / Collodictyonida, Rigifilida and Mantamonadida.
