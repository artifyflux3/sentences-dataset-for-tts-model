Find sources: "List of English words of Australian Aboriginal origin" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( July 2007 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) This is a list of English words derived from Australian Aboriginal languages . Some are restricted to Australian English as a whole or to certain regions of the country. Others, such as kangaroo and boomerang , have become widely used in other varieties of English , and some have been borrowed into other languages beyond English.

This list is incomplete ; you can help by adding missing items .
