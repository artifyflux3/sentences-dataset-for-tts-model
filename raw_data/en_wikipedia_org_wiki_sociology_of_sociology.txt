[ citation needed ] For the French sociologist Pierre Bourdieu , the task of the sociology of sociology is to debrief accepted truths, focusing on the questioning of canons and acting towards new epistemologies.

[ 1 ] In his book A History of Sociology in Britain , published 2004, British sociologist Andrew Halsey outlines a sociology of sociology. He suggests a connection between political economic regimes in the 20th century and the development of sociology as an academic discipline.
