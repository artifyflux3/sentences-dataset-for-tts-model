969749138 Links Journal homepage Online access Humanities and Social Sciences Communications is a peer-reviewed open access academic journal published by Nature Portfolio . It covers all areas of the social sciences and humanities . It was established in 2014 as Palgrave Communications (ISSN 2055-1045), obtaining its current name in 2020.

Abstracting and indexing [ edit ] The journal is abstracted and indexed in: Arts and Humanities Citation Index [ 1 ] Current Contents /Arts & Humanities [ 1 ] Current Contents/Social and Behavioral Sciences [ 1 ] Index Islamicus [ 2 ] Scopus [ 3 ] Social Sciences Citation Index [ 1 ] According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2023 impact factor of 3.7.
