term used in the nuclear power industry that refers to the isotopes of plutonium (239 Pu) uranium (235 U, 238 U) and thorium (232 Th) present in nuclear fuel , as opposed to the minor actinides neptunium , americium , curium , berkelium , and californium , including other isotopes of uranium and plutonium and other actinides.
