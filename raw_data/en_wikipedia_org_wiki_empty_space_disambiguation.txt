**Empty space** may refer to:

Physics
-------

[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Empty_space&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Physics")]

*   [Outer space](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Outer_space "Outer space"), especially the relatively empty regions of the universe outside the atmospheres of celestial bodies
*   [Vacuum](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vacuum "Vacuum"), a volume of space that is essentially empty of matter, such that its gaseous pressure is much less than atmospheric pressure
*   [Free space](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Free_space "Free space"), a perfect vacuum as expressed in the classical physics model
*   [Vacuum state](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vacuum_state "Vacuum state"), a perfect vacuum based on the quantum mechanical model
*   In [mathematical physics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mathematical_physics "Mathematical physics"), the homogeneous equation may correspond to a physical theory formulated in empty space
*   [Void](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Void_(astronomy) "Void (astronomy)"), empty space.

*   _[The Empty Space](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The\_Empty\_Space "The Empty Space")_, a 1968 book by the British director Peter Brook
