Goetz obtained a PhD in philosophy from the University of Notre Dame .

[ 1 ] He a professor in Philosophy and Religion at Ursinus College .

[ 1 ] He has defended substance dualism .

[ 2 ] [ 3 ] Goetz is a member of the Board of Advisors for the Center on Culture and Civil Society at the Independent Institute .

[ 4 ] He has debated physicalist Andrew Melnyk on consciousness and free will at Internet Infidels .
