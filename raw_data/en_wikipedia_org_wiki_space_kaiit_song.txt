"**Space**" is a song made by Australian recording artist [Kaiit](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kaiit "Kaiit"). The song was released on 29 April 2024.[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_(Kaiit_song)#cite_note-release-1)

At the [2024 Music Victoria Awards](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2024_Music_Victoria_Awards "2024 Music Victoria Awards"), the song was nominated for Best Song or Track.[[2]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_(Kaiit_song)#cite_note-2024MVAnom-2)

At the [APRA Music Awards of 2025](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/APRA_Music_Awards_of_2025 "APRA Music Awards of 2025"), the song won the Most Performed R&B / Soul Work.[[3]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_(Kaiit_song)#cite_note-APRA25_wins-3)

Reception
---------

[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Space_(Kaiit_song)&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Reception")]

Sarah Downs from _Brag Media_ said "'Space' showcases Kaiit's soulful vocals and introspective lyrics, offering listeners a moment of contemplation before action."[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_(Kaiit_song)#cite_note-release-1)

Laura McInnes from _Sniffers_ said "With her serene, soothing neo-soul melodies dazzling over a down tempo percussive groove and slinky guitar chords, Kaiit sings of a spiritual sanctuary of protected energy with her tranquil tone offering mellow lyrical meditations."[[4]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_(Kaiit_song)#cite_note-4)
