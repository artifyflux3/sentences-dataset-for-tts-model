The song was released on 29 April 2024.

[ 1 ] At the 2024 Music Victoria Awards , the song was nominated for Best Song or Track.

[ 2 ] At the APRA Music Awards of 2025 , the song won the Most Performed R&B / Soul Work.

[ 3 ] Reception [ edit ] Sarah Downs from Brag Media said "'Space' showcases Kaiit's soulful vocals and introspective lyrics, offering listeners a moment of contemplation before action." [ 1 ] Laura McInnes from Sniffers said "With her serene, soothing neo-soul melodies dazzling over a down tempo percussive groove and slinky guitar chords, Kaiit sings of a spiritual sanctuary of protected energy with her tranquil tone offering mellow lyrical meditations."
