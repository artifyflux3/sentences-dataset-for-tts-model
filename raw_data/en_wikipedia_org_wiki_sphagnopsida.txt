It is estimated it originated about 465 million years ago, along with Takakia .

[ 1 ] The order Sphagnales contains four living genera: Ambuchanania , Eosphagnum , and Flatbergium , which counts four species in total, and Sphagnum which contains the rest of the species. The extinct Protosphagnales contains a single fossil species.
