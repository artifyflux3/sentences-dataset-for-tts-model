Indexing CODEN ( alt · alt2 ) · JSTOR ( alt ) · LCCN ( alt ) MIAR · NLM ( alt ) · Scopus · W&L ISSN 1523-0406 (print) 1545-0465 (web) Links Journal homepage Cartography and Geographic Information Science is an academic journal about cartography and geographic information science published by Taylor & Francis on behalf of the U.S.

Cartography and Geographic Information Society , [ 1 ] in affiliation with the International Cartographic Association .

[ 2 ] Its editor-in-chief is Eric Delmelle;
its 2023 impact factor is 2.6.

It started in 1974 as The American Cartographer , was renamed in 1990 to Cartography and Geographic Information Systems , and in 1999 received its current name.
