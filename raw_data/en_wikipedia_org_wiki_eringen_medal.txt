Eringen Medal or Eringen Medal is an award given annually by the Society of Engineering Science (SES) to an individual "in recognition of sustained outstanding achievements in Engineering Science".

[ 1 ] This award was established in 1976.  The actual award consists of a medal and an honorarium.
