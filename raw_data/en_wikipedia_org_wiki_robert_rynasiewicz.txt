Find sources: "Robert Rynasiewicz" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( March 2011 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) Robert Rynasiewicz is a professor of philosophy at Johns Hopkins University [ 1 ] and an adjunct professor in philosophy and the Committee on History and Philosophy of Science at the University of Maryland .

Rynasiewicz earned his ScB. in physics from Brown University , and his PhD from the University of Minnesota . He has held NSF , NEH , and Mellon fellowships.

His research interests include Philosophy of Physics , [ 2 ] Logic , Philosophy of Language , and Philosophy of psychology . His publications have chiefly addressed the history and foundations of space-time physics.
