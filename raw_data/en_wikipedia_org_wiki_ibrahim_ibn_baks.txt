He became blind towards the end of his life.

[ 2 ] According to Ibn Abi Usaibi'a , Ibn Baks translated many works into the Arabic-language.

[ 2 ] Works [ edit ] Among his works are a large and a small compendium of medicine and monographs on diseases of the skin , of the eyes , on anatomy and on antidotes .
