location of Uzbekistan An enlargeable map of Uzbekistan The following outline is provided as an overview of and topical guide to Uzbekistan: Uzbekistan – doubly landlocked [ 1 ] sovereign country located in Central Asia .

[ 2 ] It borders Kazakhstan to the west and to the north, Kyrgyzstan and Tajikistan to the east, and Afghanistan and Turkmenistan to the south.
