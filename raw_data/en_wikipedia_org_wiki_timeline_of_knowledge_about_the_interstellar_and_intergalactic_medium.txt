From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

[Timeline](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline "Timeline") of knowledge about the [interstellar medium](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Interstellar_medium "Interstellar medium") and [intergalactic medium](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Outer_space#Intergalactic_space "Outer space"):

*   1848— [Lord Rosse](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/William_Parsons,_3rd_Earl_of_Rosse "William Parsons, 3rd Earl of Rosse") studies [M1](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Messier_object "Messier object") and names it the [Crab Nebula](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Crab_Nebula "Crab Nebula"). The telescope is much larger than the small refactors typical of this period and it also reveals the spiral nature of M51.
*   1864— [William Huggins](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/William_Huggins "William Huggins") studies the [spectrum](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Optical_spectrum "Optical spectrum") of the [Orion Nebula](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Orion_Nebula "Orion Nebula") and shows that it is a cloud of gas
*   1904— Interstellar calcium detected on spectrograph at Potsdam[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_knowledge_about_the_interstellar_and_intergalactic_medium#cite_note-:5-1)
*   1909— Slipher confirms Kapteyn's theory of interstellar gas[[2]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_knowledge_about_the_interstellar_and_intergalactic_medium#cite_note-:0-2)
*   1912— Slipher confirms interstellar dust[[2]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_knowledge_about_the_interstellar_and_intergalactic_medium#cite_note-:0-2)
*   1927— [Ira Bowen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ira_Sprague_Bowen "Ira Sprague Bowen") explains unidentified [spectral lines](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spectral_line "Spectral line") from space as [forbidden transition lines](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Forbidden_line "Forbidden line")
*   1930— [Robert Trumpler](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_Julius_Trumpler "Robert Julius Trumpler") discovers absorption by [interstellar dust](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Interstellar_dust "Interstellar dust") by comparing the angular sizes and brightnesses of [globular clusters](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Globular_cluster "Globular cluster")
*   1944— [Hendrik van de Hulst](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hendrik_C._van_de_Hulst "Hendrik C. van de Hulst") predicts the [21 cm hyperfine line](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/21_cm_line "21 cm line") of neutral interstellar [hydrogen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hydrogen "Hydrogen")
*   1951— [Harold I. Ewen](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Harold_I._Ewen&action=edit&redlink=1 "Harold I. Ewen (page does not exist)") and [Edward Purcell](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edward_Mills_Purcell "Edward Mills Purcell") observe the 21 cm [hyperfine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hyperfine "Hyperfine") line of neutral interstellar hydrogen[[3]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_knowledge_about_the_interstellar_and_intergalactic_medium#cite_note-nature168_4270-3)
*   1956— [Lyman Spitzer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lyman_Spitzer "Lyman Spitzer") predicts [coronal gas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coronal_cloud "Coronal cloud") around the [Milky Way](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Milky_Way "Milky Way")
*   1965— [James Gunn](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/James_Gunn_(astronomer) "James Gunn (astronomer)") and [Bruce Peterson](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bruce_Peterson "Bruce Peterson") use observations of the relatively low absorption of the blue component of the [Lyman-alpha line](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lyman-alpha_line "Lyman-alpha line") from [3C9](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=3C9&action=edit&redlink=1 "3C9 (page does not exist)") to strongly constrain the density and ionization state of the intergalactic medium[[4]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_knowledge_about_the_interstellar_and_intergalactic_medium#cite_note-apj142_1633-4)
*   1969— [Lewis Snyder](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Lewis_Snyder&action=edit&redlink=1 "Lewis Snyder (page does not exist)"), [David Buhl](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=David_Buhl&action=edit&redlink=1 "David Buhl (page does not exist)"), [Ben Zuckerman](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Benjamin_Zuckerman "Benjamin Zuckerman"), and [Patrick Palmer](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Patrick_Palmer_(astronomer)&action=edit&redlink=1 "Patrick Palmer (astronomer) (page does not exist)") find [interstellar formaldehyde](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Interstellar_formaldehyde "Interstellar formaldehyde")
*   1970— [Arno Penzias](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arno_Allan_Penzias "Arno Allan Penzias") and [Robert Wilson](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_Woodrow_Wilson "Robert Woodrow Wilson") find interstellar [carbon monoxide](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carbon_monoxide "Carbon monoxide")[[5]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timeline_of_knowledge_about_the_interstellar_and_intergalactic_medium#cite_note-apj161_L43-5)
*   1970— [George Carruthers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/George_Robert_Carruthers "George Robert Carruthers") observes [molecular hydrogen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Molecular_hydrogen "Molecular hydrogen") in space
*   1977— [Christopher McKee](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Christopher_McKee "Christopher McKee") and [Jeremiah Ostriker](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jeremiah_P._Ostriker "Jeremiah P. Ostriker") propose a three component theory of the interstellar medium
*   1990— Foreground "contamination" data from the [COBE](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cosmic_Background_Explorer "Cosmic Background Explorer") spacecraft provides the first all-sky map of the ISM in [microwave](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Microwave "Microwave") bands.
