A polymath is a person (also known as Renaissance Person ), whose expertise spans a significant number of different subject areas and who has extraordinarily broad and comprehensive knowledge.

Polymath may also refer to: Polymath (novel) , 1974 novel by John Brunner The Polymath , a non-fiction book by Waqas Ahmed, first published 2018 Polymath Park , resort in the Laurel Highlands of Pennsylvania, USA Polymath Project , a collaborative mathematics project Topics referred to by the same term This disambiguation page lists  articles associated with the title Polymath .
