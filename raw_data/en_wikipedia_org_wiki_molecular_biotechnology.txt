213883990 Links Journal homepage Online access Molecular Biotechnology is a peer-reviewed scientific journal published by Springer Science+Business Media . It publishes original research papers and review articles on the application of molecular biology to biotechnology . It was established in 1994 with John M. Walker as founding editor-in-chief . Prof Aydin Berenjian is the current editor-in-chief of the journal.
