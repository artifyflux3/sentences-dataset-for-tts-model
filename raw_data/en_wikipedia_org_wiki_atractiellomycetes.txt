[ 2 ] Order: Atractiellales Oberw. & Bandoni [ 1 ] Families Atractogloeaceae Hoehnelomycetaceae Phleogenaceae The Atractiellomycetes are class of fungi in the Pucciniomycotina subdivision of the Basidiomycota . The class consists of a single order , the Atractiellales , which contains 3 families , 10 genera , and 58 species.
