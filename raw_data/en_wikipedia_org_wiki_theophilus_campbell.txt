[ 3 ] He was educated at Trinity College, Dublin , graduating  in 1838; [ 4 ] and his first post was a curacy at Munterconnaught . He held incumbencies in Tunstall , Belfast and Finvoy . In 1886, he became Archdeacon of Dromore .
