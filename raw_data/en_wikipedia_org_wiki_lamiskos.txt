Born in Tarentum in Magna Graecia , Lamiskos was a follower of the Pythagorean philosopher Archytas . Around 360 BC, when Plato was imprisoned by Dionysius the Younger , Archytas sent Lamiskos to secure his release, and Lamiskos successfully persuaded Dionysius to release the Athenian philosopher, allowing him to return to his homeland.
