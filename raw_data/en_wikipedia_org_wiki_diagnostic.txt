Diagnosis ( pl.

: diagnoses ) is the identification of the nature and cause of a certain phenomenon. Diagnosis is used in a lot of different disciplines , with variations in the use of logic , analytics , and experience, to determine " cause and effect ". In systems engineering and computer science , it is typically used to determine the causes of symptoms, mitigations, and solutions.
