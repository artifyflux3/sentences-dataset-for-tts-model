889252485 Links Journal homepage Online archive Energy Research & Social Science is a peer-reviewed academic journal covering social science research on energy systems and energy and society , including anthropology , economics , geography , psychology , political science , social policy , sociology , science and technology studies and legal studies . It was established in 2014 and is now among the most highly ranked journals on energy and social sciences.

[ 1 ] It is published by Elsevier . The editor-in-chief is Benjamin K. Sovacool ( Aarhus University and University of Sussex ).
