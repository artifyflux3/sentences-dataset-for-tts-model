Find sources: "Association of Independent Colleges and Universities in Massachusetts" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( July 2024 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) The Association of Independent Colleges and Universities in Massachusetts ( AICUM ) is a grouping of accredited , independent, private colleges and universities in the state of Massachusetts . The Association advocates in public policy forums on behalf of its member institutions.

[ 1 ] AICUM is headquartered in Boston, Massachusetts , and it was established by a panel of university presidents. Its current president is Richard Doherty.
