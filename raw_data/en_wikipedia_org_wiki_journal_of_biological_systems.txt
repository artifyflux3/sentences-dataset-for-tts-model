MathSciNet J. Biol. Systems Indexing CODEN ( alt · alt2 ) · JSTOR ( alt ) · LCCN ( alt ) MIAR · NLM ( alt ) · Scopus · W&L ISSN 0218-3390 (print) 1793-6470 (web) Links Journal homepage The Journal of Biological Systems was founded in 1993 and is published quarterly by World Scientific .

[ 1 ] The journal aims to "promote interdisciplinary approaches in Biology and in Medicine, and the study of biological situations with a variety of tools, including mathematical and general systems methods." [ 2 ] It includes articles on complex systems studies, interdisciplinary approaches in biology and medicine , environmental studies , evolutionary biology , medical systems , numerical simulations and computations , and epidemiology .
