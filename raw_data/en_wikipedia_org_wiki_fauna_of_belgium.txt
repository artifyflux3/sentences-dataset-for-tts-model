Birds [ edit ] Main article: List of birds of Belgium Belgium is home to over 400 species of birds.

[ 1 ] The wetlands in the High Fens have many species of migratory birds.

[ 2 ] The North Sea area itself has around 60 seabird species.

[ 3 ] Mammals [ edit ] Main article: List of mammals of Belgium Belgium is home to at least 73 species of mammals.

[ 4 ] In 2024 2.4% of these were classified as threatened.

[ 2 ] The forests of the Ardennes region contain red deer, European badgers, Eurasian beavers, and others.

[ 2 ] Marine fauna [ edit ] The North Sea region of Belgium has a wide variety of fauna, with 140-150 different fish and many harbour porpoises and seals.
