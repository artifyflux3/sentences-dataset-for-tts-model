Coordinates: 40°04′25″N 104°24′54″W ﻿ / ﻿ 40.07361°N 104.41500°W ﻿ / 40.07361; -104.41500 Country United States State Colorado County Weld [ 1 ] Elevation [ 1 ] 4,846 ft (1,477 m) Time zone UTC-7 ( MST ) • Summer ( DST ) UTC-6 ( MDT ) ZIP code [ 2 ] 80643 ( Keenesburg ) GNIS feature ID 180877 Prospect Valley is a populated place in Weld County , Colorado , United States. The Post Office at Keenesburg serves the Prospect Valley postal address.

[ 2 ] SH 52 runs east and west through the community.

Geography [ edit ] Prospect Valley is located at 40°4′25″N 104°24′54″W ﻿ / ﻿ 40.07361°N 104.41500°W ﻿ / 40.07361; -104.41500 (40.0735938, -104.4149556), [ 3 ] along State Highway 52 , 11 miles east of Keenesburg.
