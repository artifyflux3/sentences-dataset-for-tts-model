3°45′N 101°45′W ﻿ / ﻿ 3.75°N 101.75°W ﻿ / 3.75; -101.75 [ 1 ] It is rotating counterclockwise between three much larger crustal plates around it, the Nazca , Cocos and Pacific plates. To its south, another small microplate, the Galapagos Microplate is likewise rotating, but clockwise.

[ 2 ] Both microplates "mesh" along the interface between them.
