Phylogenomic studies indicate that Haptista, together with Ancoracysta twista , forms a sister clade to the TSAR supergroup , but it may also be sister to the Cryptista (+ Archaeplastida ).

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] [ 3 ] [ 4 ] It is thus one of the earliest diverging Diaphoretickes .
