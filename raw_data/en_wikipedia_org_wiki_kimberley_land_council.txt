The land council was formed at a meeting at Noonkanbah Station in May 1978.

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] The corporation is registered with the Office of the Registrar of Indigenous Corporations as ICN (Indigenous Corporation Number) 21.

[ 3 ] The introduction of the Native Title Act 1993 saw the KLC as the native title representative body for Kimberley traditional owners .

[ 4 ] In the years 1998 to 2007, Federal Court native title litigation was successful for the following claims: [ 5 ] Miriuwung and Gajerrong Karajarri Tjurabalan Bardi Jawi Wanjina Wunggurr , for the Ngarinyin /Wilinggin people Rubibi (Broome), for the Yawuru people The Uunguu (Wunambal) and Dambimangari (Worrorra) determinations, which formed part of Wanjina Wunggurr claim, were made in May 2011.
