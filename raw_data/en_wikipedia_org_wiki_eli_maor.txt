[ 1 ] Eli Maor received his PhD at the Technion – Israel Institute of Technology . He taught history of mathematics at Loyola University Chicago .

[ 2 ] Maor was the editor of the article on trigonometry for the Encyclopædia Britannica .

[ 3 ] Asteroid 226861 Elimaor , discovered at the Jarnac Observatory in 2004, was named in his honor.
