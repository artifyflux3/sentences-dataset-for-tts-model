N verify ( what is Y N ?) Infobox references Chemical compound Cyanoacetylene is an organic compound with the formula C 3 H N or H−C≡C−C≡N . It is the simplest cyanopolyyne . Cyanoacetylene has been detected by spectroscopic methods in interstellar clouds , [ 2 ] in the coma of comet Hale–Bopp and in the atmosphere of Saturn 's moon Titan , [ 3 ] where it sometimes forms expansive fog-like clouds.

[ 4 ] Cyanoacetylene is one of the molecules that was produced in the Miller–Urey experiment .

[ 5 ] H−C≡C−H + H−C≡N → H−C≡C−C≡N + H 2 Nickel carbonyl catalyzes cyanoacetylene carboalkoxylation to cyanoacrylate esters.
