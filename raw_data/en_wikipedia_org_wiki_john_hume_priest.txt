[ 1 ] He was born in Oxford and educated at Christ Church, Oxford , getting a BA in 1765 and a MA in 1769.

[ 2 ] He held livings at Gillingham, Dorset and West Lavington, Wiltshire . He was Dean of Derry from 1783 until his death.
