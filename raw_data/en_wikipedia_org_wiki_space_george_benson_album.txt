_**Space**_ is a compilation album by [George Benson](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/George_Benson "George Benson") released in 1978 on [CTI Records](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/CTI_Records "CTI Records").[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_(George_Benson_album)#cite_note-1) It features his rendition of [Sam & Dave](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sam_%26_Dave "Sam & Dave")'s "[Hold On, I'm Coming](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hold_On,_I%27m_Comin%27_(song) "Hold On, I'm Comin' (song)")" recorded during the _[Good King Bad](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Good\_King\_Bad "Good King Bad")_ sessions as well as some additional songs from his _[Carnegie Hall](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/In\_Concert-Carnegie\_Hall "In Concert-Carnegie Hall")_ performance.

2.   "Summertime" (7:16)  from _[In Concert-Carnegie Hall](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/In\_Concert-Carnegie\_Hall "In Concert-Carnegie Hall")_
3.   "Sky Dive" (6:51)  Previously Unreleased

[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Space_(George_Benson_album)&action=edit&section=3 "Edit section: Side two")]
