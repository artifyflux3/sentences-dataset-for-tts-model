(1978) Space (1978) Livin' Inside Your Love (1979) Space is a compilation album by George Benson released in 1978 on CTI Records .

[ 1 ] It features his rendition of Sam & Dave 's " Hold On, I'm Coming " recorded during the Good King Bad sessions as well as some additional songs from his Carnegie Hall performance.

Track listing [ edit ] All tracks recorded live at Carnegie Hall , except as noted.
