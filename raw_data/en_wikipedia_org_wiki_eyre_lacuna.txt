[ 1 ] Eponym Lake Abaya Eyre Lacuna is a feature on Saturn's largest moon, Titan , believed to be a currently dry bed of an intermittent hydrocarbon lake .

When full, the lake would be composed of liquid methane and ethane .

[ 2 ] It was detected in 2007 by the Cassini–Huygens space probe .
