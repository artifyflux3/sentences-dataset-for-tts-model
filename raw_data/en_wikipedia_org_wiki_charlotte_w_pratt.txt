Pratt Alma mater University of Notre Dame Scientific career Fields Biochemistry Institutions Seattle Pacific University Charlotte W. Pratt is an American biochemist and author. She is the co-author with Judith G. Voet and Donald Voet of the popular standard biochemistry textbook Fundamentals of Biochemistry .

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] [ 3 ] As of 2016, she is an associate professor of biochemistry at Seattle Pacific University .

[ 4 ] She has also written the textbook Essential Biochemistry .

[ 5 ] [ 6 ] Dr. Pratt attended the University of Notre Dame for her undergraduate degree and graduated with a Ph.D. from Duke University .
