Born in Genoa to musicologist Remo Giazotto , Adalberto Giazotto earned his degree in physics from the Sapienza University of Rome . He helped design the Virgo interferometer , which first detected gravitational waves in 2017.

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] He won the Caterina Tomassoni e Felice Pietro Chisesi Prize , a Matteucci Medal , and shared the Enrico Fermi Prize with Barry Barish in 2016.
