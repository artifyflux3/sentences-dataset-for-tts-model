Find sources: "Claire Johnston" film theorist – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( March 2023 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) Claire Johnston (1940–1987) was a feminist film theoretician .

[ 1 ] She wrote seminal essays on the construction of ideology in mainstream cinema ( Hollywood and European auteur cinema ).
