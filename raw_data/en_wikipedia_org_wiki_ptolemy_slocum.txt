Slocum was born in the Kenyan capital Nairobi . He was named after the Greek astronomer Claudius Ptolemy and has been active as an actor since 2003.

[ 1 ] He first starred in several short films, before his first film appearance in 2005, portraying Ron in Hitch . As a result, he began to take on guest roles in numerous well-known US series, such as The Sopranos , The Wire , [ 3 ] Fringe , Burn Notice , How I Met Your Mother , Criminal Minds , Emergence and others.

Slocum has taken on more recurring roles, such as in Looking and Preacher . He played the role of Sylvester in the science fiction series Westworld from 2016 until the series was cancelled in 2022.

[ 4 ] [ 5 ] [ 6 ] [ 7 ] Slocum is an alumnus of the George Washington University and college comedy group receSs.
