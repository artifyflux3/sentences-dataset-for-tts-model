Rosemary Hennessy (born March 2, 1950) [ 1 ] is an American academic and socialist feminist . She is a professor of English and Director of the Center for the Study of Women, Gender, and Sexuality at Rice University . She has been a part of the faculty at Rice since 2006.

[ 2 ] She has written extensively on materialist feminism .

[ 3 ] Education [ edit ] Hennessy received her Ph.D in English from Syracuse University , her M.A.

in English from Temple University , and her B.A.

in English from the University of Pennsylvania .
