Because the latter felt it was important to encourage and recognize new artists in the field, the New England Science Fiction Association , Inc., presents the Gaughan Award annually to an emerging artist (an artist who has become a professional within the past five years) chosen by a panel of judges.

The winner of the Gaughan Award is announced during Boskone , NESFA 's annual convention.
