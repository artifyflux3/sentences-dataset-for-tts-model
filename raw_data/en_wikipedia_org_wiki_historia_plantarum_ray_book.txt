Historia Plantarum ( The History of Plants ) is a botany book by John Ray , published in 1686.

Publication [ edit ] Historia Plantarum was published in three volumes: vol 1 in 1686, vol 2 in 1688, vol 3 in 1704. The third volume lacked plates, so Ray's assistant, the apothecary James Petiver , published Petiver's Catalogue , effectively a supplement containing the plates, in parts in 1715–1764. The work on the first two volumes was supported by subscriptions from the President and Fellows of the Royal Society .
