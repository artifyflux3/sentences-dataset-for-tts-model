It was situated in the northeastern part of the governorate. Its administrative centre was Makaryevo .

Demographics [ edit ] At the time of the Russian Empire Census of 1897, Makaryevsky Uyezd had a population of 108,994. Of these, 98.5% spoke Russian , 1.2% Mari and 0.1% Tatar as their native language.
