[ 1 ] [ 2 ] [ 3 ] [ 4 ] The ICoMM program, led by Mitchell Sogin , has discovered that marine microbial diversity is some 10 to 100 times more than expected, and the vast majority are previously unknown, low abundance organisms thought to play an important role in the oceans.
