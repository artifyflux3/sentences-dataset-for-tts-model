She has also contributed to applications of statistics in studying additive manufacturing , invasive species , climate models , and educational evaluation. She is a professor of statistics at Texas A&M University .

[ 1 ] Sang has a 2004 bachelor's degree from Peking University .

[ 1 ] She completed a Ph.D. at Duke University in 2008, with the dissertation Extreme value modeling for space-time data with meteorological applications supervised by Alan E. Gelfand .

[ 2 ] She has been a faculty member at Texas A&M University since 2008.

[ 3 ] Sang was elected as a Fellow of the American Statistical Association in 2024.
