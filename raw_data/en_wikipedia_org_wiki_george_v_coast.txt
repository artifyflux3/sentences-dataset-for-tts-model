Portions of this coast were sighted by the US Exploring Expedition in 1840.

[ 1 ] It was explored by members of the Main Base party of the Australasian Antarctic Expedition (1911–14) under Douglas Mawson who named this feature for King George V of the United Kingdom .

[ 2 ] The segment of land between these lines of longitude is referred to as George V Land .

Mertz Glacier is located in this area.
