& Vánky (1997) [ 1 ] Orders Urocystidales Ustilaginales Ustilaginomycetes is the class of true smut fungi . They are plant parasites with about 1400 recognised species in 70 genera .

[ 2 ] They have a simple septum with a septal pore cap, this is different from Agaricomycotina which has a dolipore septum with parenthoesome.

[ 3 ] The group is monophyletic (has a common ancestor).
