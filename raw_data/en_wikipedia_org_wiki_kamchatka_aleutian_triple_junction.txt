The Kamchatka–Aleutian triple junction is a triple junction of tectonic plates , of the fault–fault–trench type, where the Pacific plate , the Okhotsk plate , and the North American plate meet.

[ 1 ] It is located east of the Kamchatka Mys peninsula and west of Bering Island .

Meiji Seamount is located to the southeast of the junction.

In the Kamchatka–Aleutian junction, the Kuril–Kamchatka Trench meets the Aleutian Trench . The former is a subduction zone while the latter is a transform fault in its western part.
