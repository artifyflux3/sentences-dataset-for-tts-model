Modern Greek [ edit ] In Modern Greek , it has two distinct pronunciations: In front of high or front vowels ( /e/ or /i/ ) it is pronounced as a voiceless palatal fricative [Ã§] , as in German ich or like some pronunciations of "h" in English words like hew and human .
