Place in Võru County, Estonia Rõuge Rõuge Coordinates: 57°43′40″N 26°54′35″E ﻿ / ﻿ 57.72778°N 26.90972°E ﻿ / 57.72778; 26.90972 Country Estonia County Võru County Parish Rõuge Parish Time zone UTC+2 ( EET ) Rõuge ( Võro : Rõugõ ; German : Rauge ) is a small borough ( alevik ) in Võru County in southeastern Estonia . It is the administrative centre of Rõuge Parish .
