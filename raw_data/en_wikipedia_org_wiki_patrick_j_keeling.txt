Palmer (postdoc) [ 5 ] Website www .botany .ubc .ca /people /patrick-keeling Patrick John Keeling is a biologist and professor in the Department of Botany at the University of British Columbia .

[ 6 ] [ 3 ] [ 7 ] His research investigates the phylogeny , genomics and molecular evolution of protists and his work has led to numerous advances in assembling the eukaryotic tree of life . He has also identified several cases of horizontal gene transfer .
