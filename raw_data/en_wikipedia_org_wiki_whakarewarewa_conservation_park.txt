[ 1 ] It lies close to SH 30 to the southwest of Rotorua .

It is managed by the Department of Conservation .

[ 1 ] The five kilometre Tuhoto Ariki Trail through the park is a dual mountain biking and walking track, which takes about 40 minutes by bike or 90 minutes on foot.
