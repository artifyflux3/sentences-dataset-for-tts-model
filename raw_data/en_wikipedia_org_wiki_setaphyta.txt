Anthocerotophytina (hornworts) are excluded.

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] A 2018 study found through molecular sequencing that liverworts are more closely related to mosses than hornworts, with the implication that liverworts were not among the first species to colonize land.

[ 3 ] Phylogeny [ edit ] There is strong phylogenetic evidence for Setaphyta.
