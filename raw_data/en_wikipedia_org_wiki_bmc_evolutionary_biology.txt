47657384 Links Journal homepage Online archive BMC Ecology and Evolution (since January 2021), previously BMC Evolutionary Biology (2001–2020), is a peer-reviewed open access scientific journal covering all fields of evolutionary biology , including phylogenetics and palaeontology . It was established in 2001 and is part of a series of BMC journals published by BioMed Central . It was an early journal to integrate with the Dryad Digital Repository to share supplementary data, [ 1 ] and it also organises a yearly image competition to help capture the diversity of the planet's flora and fauna.

[ 2 ] Abstracting and indexing [ edit ] The journal is abstracted and indexed in: Index Medicus / MEDLINE / PubMed [ 3 ] BIOSIS Previews [ 4 ] Chemical Abstracts Service EMBASE Scopus [ 5 ] The Zoological Record [ 4 ] CAB International Science Citation Index [ 4 ] Current Contents /Agriculture, Biology & Environmental Sciences [ 4 ] According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2020 impact factor of 3.260.
