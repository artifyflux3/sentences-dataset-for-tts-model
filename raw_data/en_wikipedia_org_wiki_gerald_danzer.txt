Danzer (born November 9, 1938) is professor emeritus of history at the University of Illinois at Chicago . He is a specialist in historical geography , world history , and the use of old maps as historical sources.

Early life [ edit ] Gerald Danzer was born on November 9, 1938. He received his Ph.D from Northwestern University in 1967 for a dissertation on the subject of "America's Roots in the Past: Historical Publication in America to 1860".

[ 1 ] Career [ edit ] Danzer is professor emeritus of history at the University of Illinois Chicago [ 2 ] where he has taught an MA program for teachers of history for over thirty years.

[ 3 ] He is a specialist in historical geography, world history, and the use of old maps as historical sources. He is a former director of the Chicago Neighborhood History Project .
