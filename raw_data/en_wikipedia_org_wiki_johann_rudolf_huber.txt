Johann Rudolf Huber (21 April 1668 – 28 February 1748) was a Swiss portrait artist.  Among his famous subjects were Charles III William, Margrave of Baden-Durlach , Joseph I, Holy Roman Emperor and Albrecht von Haller .

Biography [ edit ] Huber was born as the son of a landlord in Basel . There, in Bern , Venice and Rome he learned to paint. In 1699 he was appointed court painter at the court in Stuttgart . He did portraits of nearly 5,000 of his contemporaries. He died in Basel .
