36145205 Links Journal homepage Latest Articles Online issue archive Microbiology and Molecular Biology Reviews (published as MMBR) is a peer-reviewed scientific journal published by the American Society for Microbiology .

History [ edit ] The journal was established in 1937 as Bacteriological Reviews ( ISSN 0005-3678 ) and changed its name in 1978 to Microbiological Reviews ( ISSN 0146-0749 ). It obtained its current title in 1997.
