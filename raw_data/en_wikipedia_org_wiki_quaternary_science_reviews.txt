7580090 Links Journal homepage Online archive Quaternary Science Reviews is a peer-reviewed scientific journal covering quaternary science . It was established in 1982 by Pergamon Press and is currently published by Elsevier . The editor-in-chief is C.V. Murray Wallace ( University of Wollongong ). According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2013 impact factor of 4.571.
