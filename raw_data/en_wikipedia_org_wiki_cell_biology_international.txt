26684328 Links Journal homepage Online archive Online archive of Cell Biology International Reports Cell Biology International is a peer-reviewed scientific journal published by Portland Press for the International Federation for Cell Biology .

[ 1 ] The journal was established in 1977 as Cell Biology International Reports ( ISSN 0309-1651 ) and published by Elsevier , obtaining its current name in 1993. The journal was transferred to Portland Press in 2010. It covers all aspects of cell biology .

Abstracting and indexing [ edit ] The journal is abstracted and indexed in: [ 2 ] BIOBASE BIOSIS Chemical Abstracts Service Current Contents EMBASE MEDLINE / Index Medicus Science Citation Index According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2010 impact factor of 1.747.
