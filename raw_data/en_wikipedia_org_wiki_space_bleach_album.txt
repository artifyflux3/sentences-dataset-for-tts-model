_**Space**_ is the debut [album](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Album "Album") by [Bleach](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bleach_(American_band) "Bleach (American band)"). It was released in 1996 under [Forefront Records](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Forefront_Records "Forefront Records").

Personnel
---------

[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Space_(Bleach_album)&action=edit&section=2 "Edit section: Personnel")]

**Bleach**

*   David Baysinger – vocals
*   Brad Ford – [Wurlitzer electric piano](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wurlitzer_electric_piano "Wurlitzer electric piano"), guitars
*   Sam Barnhart – guitars, backing vocals
*   Todd Kirby – bass
*   Matt Gingerich – drums

**Additional musicians**

*   Todd Collins – percussion
*   Bob Mason – cello

### Production

[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Space_(Bleach_album)&action=edit&section=3 "Edit section: Production")]

*   Dan R. Brock – executive producer
*   [Eddie DeGarmo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eddie_DeGarmo "Eddie DeGarmo") – executive producer
*   Barry Blair – producer
*   Marcelo Pennell – recording, mixing
*   Ken Love – mastering at MasterMix (Nashville, Tennessee)
*   Jim Dantzler – art direction
*   Thunder Image Group – photography
*   Genie Greeman – make-up
*   Mike Kell & Associates – management
