Find sources: "Lilienfeld Prize" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( March 2017 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) The Julius Edgar Lilienfeld Prize of the American Physical Society , to remember Julius Edgar Lilienfeld , has been awarded annually, since 1989. (It was not awarded in 2002). The purpose of the Prize is to recognize outstanding contributions to physics.
