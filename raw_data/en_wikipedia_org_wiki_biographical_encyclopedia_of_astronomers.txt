The Biographical Encyclopedia of Astronomers ( BEA ) is a two-volume biographical dictionary , first published in 2007, [ 1 ] with a second edition released in 2014. The work covers astronomers from all geographies, born from antiquity to mid-1918. It includes more than 1500 [ 2 ] biographies of both well-known and more obscure astronomers , produced by 410 contributors.

[ 3 ] The encyclopedia has been published in both a print and online format by the publisher, Springer .
