He was a secretary of the National Library of France under Louis XIV . Carcavi was a French mathematician .

[ 1 ] Carcavi is known for his correspondence with Pierre de Fermat , Blaise Pascal , Christiaan Huygens , Galileo Galilei , Marin Mersenne , Evangelista Torricelli and René Descartes .
