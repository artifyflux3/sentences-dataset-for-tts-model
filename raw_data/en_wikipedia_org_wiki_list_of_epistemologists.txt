philosophy v t e This is a list of epistemologists , that is, people who theorize about the nature of knowledge , belief formation and the nature of justification . This list is by necessity incomplete, since countless other philosophers also deal with epistemological issues in their work.

This is a dynamic list and may never be able to satisfy particular standards for completeness. You can help by adding missing items with reliable sources .
