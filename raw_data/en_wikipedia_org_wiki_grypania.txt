Grypania spiralis fossil Scientific classification Domain: Incertae sedis Genus: † Grypania Walter, Oehler & Oehler, 1976 [ 1 ] Type species Grypania spiralis Walter, Oehler & Oehler, 1976 Grypania is an early, tube-shaped fossil from the Proterozoic eon. The organism, with a size over one centimeter and consistent form, could have been a giant bacterium , a bacterial colony, or a eukaryotic alga .

[ 2 ] The oldest probable Grypania fossils date to about 1870 million years ago (redated from the previous 2100 million) [ 2 ] [ 3 ] and the youngest extended into the Ediacaran period.

[ 4 ] This implies that the time range of this taxon extended for 1200 million years.
