[ 1 ] Early life and education [ edit ] He was educated at Trinity College, Dublin and ordained in 1882.

[ 2 ] Career [ edit ] Hayes began his ecclesiastical career as a curate at Derry Cathedral . He was Rector of Drumragh from 1893 to 1904; and of Templemore from 1904 until his elevation to the deanery.

[ 3 ] Death [ edit ] He died on 12 November 1938.
