[ 1 ] It is hypothesized to allow for widely distributed groups of neurons to achieve integrated and synchronized firing , [ 2 ] which is proposed to be a requirement for consciousness , as outlined by Gerald Edelman and Giulio Tononi in their book A Universe of Consciousness .
