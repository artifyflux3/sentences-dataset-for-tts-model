1099283354 Links Journal homepage Online archive Communications Chemistry is a peer-reviewed , open access , scientific journal in the field chemistry published by Nature Portfolio since 2018. The chief editor is Victoria Richards.

[ 1 ] Communications Chemistry was created as a sub-journal to Nature Communications along with Communications Biology and Communications Physics .

[ 2 ] Abstracting and indexing [ edit ] The journal is abstracted and indexed in: [ 3 ] Chemical Abstracts Service (CAS) Current Contents/Physical, Chemical and Earth Sciences Journal Citation Reports/Science Edition Science Citation Index Scopus According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2024 impact factor of 6.2, ranking it 50st out of 239 journals in the category "Chemistry, Multidisciplinary".
