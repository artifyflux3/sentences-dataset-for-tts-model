Through a range of initiatives developed in collaboration with social partners, the Department of Employment and Labour makes a substantial contribution to the decline in inequality, poverty, and unemployment. These programs aim to reduce workplace poverty, promote positive labor relations, increase economic efficiency and productivity, eliminate workplace discrimination and inequality.

As of 29 May 2019 [update] the Minister of Employment and Labour is Thembelani Thulas Nxesi . In the 2011/12 budget the department had a budget of R 1,981 million and a staff complement of 3,490 civil servants.
