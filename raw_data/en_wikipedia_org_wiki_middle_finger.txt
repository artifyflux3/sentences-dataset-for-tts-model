The **middle finger**, **long finger**, **second finger**,[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Middle_finger#cite_note-1)[[2]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Middle_finger#cite_note-2)**third finger**,[[3]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Middle_finger#cite_note-3)**tall finger** or **tall man**[_[citation needed](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation\_needed "Wikipedia:Citation needed")_] is the third digit of the [human](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Human "Human")[hand](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hand "Hand"), typically located between the [index finger](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Index_finger "Index finger") and the [ring finger](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ring_finger "Ring finger"). It is typically the longest digit. In [anatomy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anatomy "Anatomy"), it is also called _the third finger_, _digitus medius_, _digitus tertius_ or _digitus III_.

Overview
--------

[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Middle_finger&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Overview")]

In Western countries, [extending the middle finger](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_finger "The finger") (either by itself, or along with the [index finger](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Index_finger "Index finger") in the United Kingdom: see [V sign](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/V_sign "V sign")) is an offensive and obscene gesture, widely recognized as a form of insult, due to its resemblance of an [erect](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Erection "Erection")[penis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Penis "Penis").[[4]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Middle_finger#cite_note-4)[[5]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Middle_finger#cite_note-5) It is known, colloquially, as "flipping the bird",[[6]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Middle_finger#cite_note-6) "flipping (someone) off", or "giving (someone) the finger".

The middle finger is often used for [finger snapping](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Finger_snapping "Finger snapping") together with the thumb.[[7]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Middle_finger#cite_note-7)
