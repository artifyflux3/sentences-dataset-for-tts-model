[ 2 ] He is the author of a zij , the al-Zīj al-muḥaqqaq . From his name it is known he originated from Wābkana, Bukhara , but little else is known of his life. His zij is extant in four manuscripts . His book is written in Persian , even though its title is in Arabic . Another extant book by him, Kitāb-i maʿrifat-i usṭurlāb-i shamālī is also written in Persian.
