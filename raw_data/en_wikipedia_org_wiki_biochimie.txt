1039435995 Bulletin de la Société de Chimie Biologique: ISSN 0037-9042 Links Journal homepage Online archive Biochimie is a monthly peer-reviewed scientific journal covering the fields of biochemistry , biophysics , and molecular biology . It is published by Elsevier on behalf of the Société française de biochimie et de biologie moléculaire [ fr ] .

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] [ 3 ] The journal, started as a French language publication, is also open to English language articles since the late 2000s. 
As of March 2021 [update] , the editor-in-chief is Bertrand Friguet , succeeding Richard H. Buckingham .

[ 4 ] History [ edit ] The journal was established in 1914 under the title Bulletin de la Société de Chimie Biologique , obtaining its current title in 1971.

[ 3 ] [ 5 ] Abstracting and indexing [ edit ] The journal is abstracted and indexed in: BIOSIS Previews [ 6 ] Chemical Abstracts Service [ 7 ] Current Contents /Life Sciences [ 6 ] Embase [ 8 ] EMBiology Elsevier BIOBASE Index Medicus / MEDLINE / PubMed [ 3 ] PASCAL Science Citation Index Expanded [ 6 ] Scopus [ 9 ] According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2021 impact factor of 4.372.
