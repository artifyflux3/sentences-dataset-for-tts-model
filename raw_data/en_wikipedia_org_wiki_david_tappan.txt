David Tappan (1752–1803) was an American theologian . He occupied the Hollis Chair at Harvard Divinity School until his death in 1803.

[ 1 ] He was elected a Fellow of the American Academy of Arts and Sciences in 1796.

[ 2 ] He graduated from Harvard University in 1771.
