That is, for X = | Y | {\displaystyle X=|Y|\!} where Y is a logistic random variable, X is a half-logistic random variable.

Specification [ edit ] Cumulative distribution function [ edit ] The cumulative distribution function (cdf) of the half-logistic distribution is intimately related to the cdf of the logistic distribution. Formally, if F ( k ) is the cdf for the logistic distribution, then G ( k ) = 2 F ( k ) − 1 is the cdf of a half-logistic distribution. Specifically, G ( k ) = 1 − − e − − k 1 + e − − k for k ≥ ≥ 0.

{\displaystyle G(k)={\frac {1-e^{-k}}{1+e^{-k}}}{\text{ for }}k\geq 0.\!} Probability density function [ edit ] Similarly, the probability density function (pdf) of the half-logistic distribution is g ( k ) = 2 f ( k ) if f ( k ) is the pdf of the logistic distribution. Explicitly, g ( k ) = 2 e − − k ( 1 + e − − k ) 2 for k ≥ ≥ 0.
