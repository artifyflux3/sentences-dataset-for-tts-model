[ 2 ] History [ edit ] During the 19th century, secular subject matter in painting the Philippines increased extensively. With more tourists, ilustrados and foreigners demanding souvenirs and decorations from the country, Tipos del País developed in painting.

Damián Domingo was the most popular artist who worked in this style.
