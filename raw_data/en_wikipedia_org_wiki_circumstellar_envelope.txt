Usually circumstellar envelopes are formed from the dense stellar wind , or they are present before the formation of the star.

[ 1 ] Circumstellar envelopes of old stars ( Mira variables and OH/IR stars ) eventually evolve into protoplanetary nebulae , and circumstellar envelopes of young stellar objects evolve into circumstellar discs .
