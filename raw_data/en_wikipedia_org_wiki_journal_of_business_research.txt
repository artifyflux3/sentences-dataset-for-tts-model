173702027 Links Journal homepage The Journal of Business Research is a monthly peer-reviewed academic journal covering research on all aspects of business . It was established in 1973 [ 1 ] and is published by Elsevier . The editors-in-chief are Dipayan Biswas ( University of South Florida ) and Mirella Kleijnen ( Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam ).

Abstracting and indexing [ edit ] The journal is abstracted and indexed in Current Contents /Social & Behavioral Sciences, [ 2 ] PsycINFO / Psychological Abstracts , [ 3 ] RePEc , Scopus , [ 4 ] and the Social Sciences Citation Index .
