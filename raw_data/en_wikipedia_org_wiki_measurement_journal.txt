473227924 Links Journal homepage Online access Online archive Measurement is a peer-reviewed scientific journal covering all aspects of metrology . It was established in 1983 and is published 18 times per year. It is published by Elsevier on behalf of the International Measurement Confederation and the editor-in-chief is Paolo Carbone ( University of Perugia ). According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2021 impact factor of 5.131.
