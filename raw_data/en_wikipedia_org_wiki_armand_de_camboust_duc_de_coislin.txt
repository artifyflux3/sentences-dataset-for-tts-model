Find sources: "Armand du Cambout, 1st Duke of Coislin" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( July 2025 ) Armand du Cambout, 1st Duke of Coislin Armand du Cambout, 1st Duke of Coislin (1 September 1635, Paris – 16 September 1702) was a French lieutenant général des armées du roi, and a duke and peer of France . The son of a colonel in the Swiss Guards , he was elected a member of the Académie Française in 1652 aged 16 and a half. After his death his seat was then held by his two sons, Pierre and Henri-Charles .
