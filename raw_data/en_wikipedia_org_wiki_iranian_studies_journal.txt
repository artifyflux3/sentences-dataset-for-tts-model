1753915 Links Journal homepage Online access Online archive Iranian Studies is a peer-reviewed academic journal covering Iranian and Persianate history , literature , and society published quarterly by Cambridge University Press on behalf of the Association for Iranian Studies (formerly known as the International Society for Iranian Studies).

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] It was established in 1967 and up to 2021 was published 6 times a year by Routledge . The editor-in-chief is Nasrin Rahimieh .

Editors-in-chief [ edit ] The following persons are or have been editors-in-chief: Ali Banuazizi (1968–1982), founding editor [ 3 ] Ervand Abrahamian and Farhad Kazemi (1982–1986) Richard W. Bulliet (1987–1989) Abbas Amanat (1990–1997) Robert D. McChesney (1998–2003) [ 4 ] Homa Katouzian (2004–2016) [ 5 ] Ali Gheissari (2016–2021) [ 6 ] Sussan Siavoshi (2021–2023) [ 7 ] Nasrin Rahimieh (2023–present) [ 8 ] Abstracting and indexing [ edit ] The journal is abstracted and indexed in: Arts and Humanities Citation Index [ 9 ] Current Contents /Arts & Humanities [ 9 ] Current Contents/Social and Behavioral Sciences [ 9 ] Index Islamicus [ 10 ] International Bibliography of Periodical Literature [ 10 ] Linguistic Bibliography [ 10 ] Modern Language Association Database [ 10 ] ProQuest databases [ 10 ] Scopus [ 11 ] Social Sciences Citation Index [ 9 ] According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2023 impact factor of 0.7.
