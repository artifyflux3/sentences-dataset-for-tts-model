Scientific classification Kingdom: Fungi Division: Kickxellomycota Subdivision: Kickxellomycotina Benny 2007 Orders Ramicandelaberales Dimargaritales Barbatosporales Spiromycetales Orphellales Asellariales Kickxellales Harpellales Synonyms Harpellomycotina Doweld 2014 Kickxellomycotina is a fungus grouping [ 1 ] in the subkingdom of Zoopagomyceta Benny, 2007 .

[ 1 ] The name was changed from "Harpellomycotina", because "Kickxellomycotina" had an older stem.

[ 2 ] It came from the genus Kickxella , named after Jean Kickx .

[ 3 ] Orders include Asellariales , Kickxellales , Dimargaritales , and Harpellales .
