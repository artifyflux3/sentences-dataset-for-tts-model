24308833 Links Journal homepage Clinical Infectious Diseases is a peer-reviewed medical journal published by Oxford University Press covering research on the pathogenesis , clinical investigation, medical microbiology , diagnosis , immune mechanisms, and treatment of diseases caused by infectious agents. It includes articles on antimicrobial resistance , bioterrorism , emerging infections , food safety , hospital epidemiology , and HIV/AIDS . It also features highly focused brief reports, review articles, editorials, commentaries, and supplements. The journal is published on behalf of the Infectious Diseases Society of America . The editor-in-chief is infectious disease physician Paul Sax .

[ 1 ] According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal had a 2020 impact factor of 9.079, ranking it 18th out of 162 journals in the category "Immunology", [ 2 ] 3rd out of 92 journals in the category "Infectious Diseases" [ 3 ] and 12th out of 137 journals in the category "Microbiology".
