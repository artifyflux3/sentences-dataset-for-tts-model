Scientific classification Domain: Eukaryota Clade : Diaphoretickes Clade : CAM Yazaki et al.

, 2022 Subgroups Pancryptista Cryptista Microheliella Archaeplastida Rhodophyta Rhodelphidia Glaucophyta Viridiplantae Picozoa The CAM clade is a clade containing the Archaeplastida ( Plantae sensu lato ) and the clade Pancryptista (which, in turn, contains Cryptista and Microheliella maris ). This clade is supported by phylogenomic analyses from 2022 that mainly support the clade Pancryptista, but also mention that its closest lineage is Archaeplastida, making them sister taxa.

[ 1 ] Together, they form the clade CAM, which stands for the constituent clades ( Cryptista , Archaeplastida, and Microheliella ).
