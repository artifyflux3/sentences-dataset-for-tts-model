[ 1 ] History [ edit ] Godine was founded in 1970 by David R. Godine, who acted as publisher until his retirement in 2019. Leadership of the company was then assumed by Will Thorndike.

[ 2 ] In March 2020, Godine partnered with Two Rivers / Ingram to distribute their publications.
