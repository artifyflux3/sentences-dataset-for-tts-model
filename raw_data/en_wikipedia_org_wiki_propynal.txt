Chemical compound

**Propiolaldehyde** is an [organic compound](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Organic_compound "Organic compound") with [molecular formula](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Molecular_formula "Molecular formula") HC 2 CHO. It is the simplest chemical compound containing both [alkyne](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alkyne "Alkyne") and [aldehyde](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aldehyde "Aldehyde")[functional groups](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Functional_group "Functional group"). It is a colorless liquid with [explosive](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Explosive "Explosive") properties.[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Propynal#cite_note-eros-1)

Reactions
---------

[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Propiolaldehyde&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Reactions")]

The compound exhibits reactions expected for an electrophilic alkynyl aldehyde. It is a dienophile and a good [Michael acceptor](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Michael_acceptor "Michael acceptor"). Grignard reagents add to the carbonyl center.[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Propynal#cite_note-eros-1) Its explosive properties are attributed to the exothermicity of its polymerization.[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Propynal#cite_note-eros-1)

Preparation
-----------

[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Propiolaldehyde&action=edit&section=2 "Edit section: Preparation")]

Its acetal can be prepared from [acrolein](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Acrolein "Acrolein").[[2]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Propynal#cite_note-2)

Occurrence in interstellar medium
---------------------------------

[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Propiolaldehyde&action=edit&section=3 "Edit section: Occurrence in interstellar medium")]

Propynal has been observed in the [interstellar medium](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Interstellar_medium "Interstellar medium"). It is hypothesized to be formed from a [carbon monoxide](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carbon_monoxide "Carbon monoxide")-[acetylene](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Acetylene "Acetylene") complex.[[3]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Propynal#cite_note-3) Another possible pathway is through the reaction of [propynylidyne](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Propynylidyne "Propynylidyne") (C 3 H) with water.[[4]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Propynal#cite_note-4)

Hazards
-------

[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Propiolaldehyde&action=edit&section=4 "Edit section: Hazards")]

The compound is [explosive](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Explosive "Explosive"), possibly because it tends to polymerize.[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Propynal#cite_note-eros-1)
