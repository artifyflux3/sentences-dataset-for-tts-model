33890369 Links Journal homepage Online access Eighteenth-Century Studies is an academic journal established in 1966 and the official publication of the American Society for Eighteenth-Century Studies . It focuses on all aspects of 18th century history. It is related to the annual Studies in Eighteenth-Century Culture . The current editor-in-chief is Ramesh Mallipeddi ( University of British Columbia ). The journal is published quarterly in October, January, April, and July by the Johns Hopkins University Press .
