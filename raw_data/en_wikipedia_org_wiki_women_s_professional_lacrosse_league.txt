The league was formally composed of five teams: the Brave , Command , Fight , Fire , and Pride .

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] The league announced on February 27, 2020, that it would restructure and downsize the league to four teams, [ 3 ] which would ultimately be: the Brave , Command , Fight , and Pride .

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] League play started on Saturday, June 2, 2018.

[ 1 ] [ 4 ] After cancelling the 2020 season in April due to the COVID-19 pandemic , the league announced on August 6, 2020, that it would be closing down, and that some of its athletes would be given opportunities to play with Athletes Unlimited .

[ 5 ] The new Athletes Unlimited league began play in July 2021.

Media coverage [ edit ] On April 16, 2019, ESPN and the WPLL announced [ 7 ] that the 10-game 2019 [ 8 ] regular season would air on ESPN3 and the ESPN App. Meanwhile, the postseason semifinals would on ESPN3, with the championship game airing on ESPNU .
