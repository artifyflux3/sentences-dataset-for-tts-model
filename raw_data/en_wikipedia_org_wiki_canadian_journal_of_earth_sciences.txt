818994372 Links Journal homepage Online access Online archive The Canadian Journal of Earth Sciences is a monthly peer-reviewed scientific journal established in 1963, which reports current research on all aspects of the Earth sciences . It is published by NRC Research Press . The journal also publishes special issues that focus on information and studies limited in scope to a specific segment of the Earth sciences. The editors-in-chief are Dr. Brendan Murphy ( St. Francis Xavier University ) and Sally Pehrsson ( University of Saskatchewan ). According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2020 impact factor of 1.369.
