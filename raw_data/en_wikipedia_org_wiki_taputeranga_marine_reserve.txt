Location New Zealand Nearest city Wellington Coordinates 41°21′11″S 174°45′48″E ﻿ / ﻿ 41.353167°S 174.76337°E ﻿ / -41.353167; 174.76337 Area 854.8 ha Established 2008 Governing body Department of Conservation Taputeranga Marine Reserve is a legally protected area of sea and coast along the southern edge of Wellington , in New Zealand . It was officially opened in September 2008.

[ 1 ] The reserve covers 855 hectares (2,110 acres) [ 2 ] and includes all foreshore up to mean high water spring.
