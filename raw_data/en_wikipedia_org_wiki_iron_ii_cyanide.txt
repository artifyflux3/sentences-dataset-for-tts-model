Chemical compound

**Iron(II) cyanide** is a hypothetical [inorganic compound](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inorganic_compound "Inorganic compound") with the [empirical formula](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Empirical_formula "Empirical formula") Fe(CN)2. A chemical compound that has been produced from the decomposition of [ammonium ferrocyanide](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Ammonium_ferrocyanide&action=edit&redlink=1 "Ammonium ferrocyanide (page does not exist)") has been claimed to be Fe(CN)2, but no spectroscopic evidence has been presented.[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iron(II)_cyanide#cite_note-wj-1)[[2]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iron(II)_cyanide#cite_note-2)
