Infobox references Chemical compound Iron(II) cyanide is a hypothetical inorganic compound with the empirical formula Fe(CN) 2 . A chemical compound that has been produced from the decomposition of ammonium ferrocyanide has been claimed to be Fe(CN) 2 , but no spectroscopic evidence has been presented.
