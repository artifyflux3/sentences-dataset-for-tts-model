Night Wall I The sculpture in 2008 Artist Louise Nevelson Year 1972 ( 1972 ) Medium Steel sculpture Location Cambridge, Massachusetts , U.S.

Coordinates 42°22′42″N 71°07′04″W ﻿ / ﻿ 42.37842°N 71.11778°W ﻿ / 42.37842; -71.11778 Night Wall I is a sculpture by Louise Nevelson , installed outside Hauser Hall at Harvard Law School , [ 1 ] on the Harvard University campus in Cambridge, Massachusetts , United States. The 1972 painted steel sculpture was donated to Harvard University Art Museums by Mildred and Arnold Glimcher in 1985.

[ 2 ] The artwork was surveyed by the Smithsonian Institution 's " Save Outdoor Sculpture!

" program in 1995.

[ 3 ] It was restored and reinstalled in 2013; funding for the restoration was provided by the Harvard John A. Paulson School of Engineering and Applied Sciences and the Harvard Law School.
