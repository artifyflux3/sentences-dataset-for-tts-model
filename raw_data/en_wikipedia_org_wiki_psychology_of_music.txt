645313929 Links Journal homepage Online access Online archive Psychology of Music is a peer-reviewed academic journal that publishes papers in the field of music psychology . The editor-in-chief role is held jointly by Nikki Moran ( University of Edinburgh ) and Michelle Phillips ( Royal Northern College of Music ). It was established in 1973 and is published by SAGE Publications on behalf of the Society for Education, Music and Psychology Research .

Abstracting and indexing [ edit ] The journal is abstracted and indexed in Scopus and the Social Sciences Citation Index . According to the Journal Citation Reports , its 2012 impact factor is 1.553.
