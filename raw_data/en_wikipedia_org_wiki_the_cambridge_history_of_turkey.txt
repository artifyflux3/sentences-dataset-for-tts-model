[ 1 ] [ 2 ] [ 3 ] [ 4 ] [ 5 ] [ 6 ] [ 7 ] [ 8 ] The fourth volume was criticized for almost omitting the Armenian genocide , which it did not classify as a genocide, and never mentioning the Assyrian genocide or Greek genocide .
