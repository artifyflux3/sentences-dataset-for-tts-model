The Journal of Geography is an American academic journal published by the National Council for Geographic Education .

[ 1 ] The journal "publishes research, instructional approaches and book reviews on innovative approaches to geography research, teaching, and learning." [ 2 ] The editor in chief, As of 2021 [update] is Meredith Marsh  of  Lindenwood University, USA [ 3 ] It has an Impact factor of 1.262 for 2019 [ 4 ] References [ edit ] ^ "The Journal of Geography – National Council for Geographic Education" .
