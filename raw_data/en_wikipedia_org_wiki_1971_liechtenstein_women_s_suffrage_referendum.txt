women's suffrage was held in Liechtenstein on 28 February 1971.

[ 1 ] Voting was restricted to men, and resulted in a majority against its introduction. Voter turnout was 85.6%.

[ 2 ] Following the referendum, some women demonstrated in Vaduz and other towns, booing male pedestrians and carrying signs bearing the slogan "Men of Liechtenstein: Where's your virility".
