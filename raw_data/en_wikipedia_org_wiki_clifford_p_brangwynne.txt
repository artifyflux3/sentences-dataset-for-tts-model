( November 2022 ) Clifford P. Brangwynne Born Lexington, Massachusetts, USA Alma mater Carnegie Mellon University Harvard University Scientific career Fields Soft-matter physics, cell biology Institutions Princeton University Clifford P. Brangwynne is a professor of chemical and biological engineering at Princeton University , [ 1 ] the director of the Princeton Bioengineering Initiative, and the June K. Wu ’92 Professor in Engineering.

[ 2 ] He is also a researcher at the Howard Hughes Medical Institute .

[ 3 ] Education [ edit ] Brangwynne graduated from Carnegie Mellon University in 2001 with a degree in materials science and engineering, and got his Ph.D. in applied physics from Harvard University in 2007.

[ 2 ] He served as a postdoctoral fellow at the Max Planck Institute , working with Anthony Hyman .
