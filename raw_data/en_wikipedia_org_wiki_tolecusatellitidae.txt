The family contains two genera and 131 species.

[ 1 ] This family of viruses depend on the presence of another virus ( helper viruses ) to replicate their genomes, as such they have minimal genomes with very low genomic redundancy.

[ 2 ] Name [ edit ] The  name Tolecu satellitidae is a combination of Tolecu , from the first DNA satellite shown to be associated with To mato le af cu rl virus and satellite , the fact that it is a satellite.

[ 3 ] Taxonomy [ edit ] The species are ssDNA unless specified ssDNA(+). There are 62 ssDNA(+) and 69 ssDNA satellites.
