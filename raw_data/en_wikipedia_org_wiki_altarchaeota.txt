, 2018 " Candidatus Altarchaeum" is a monotypic genus of ultra-small archaea . It contains the sole species " Candidatus Altarchaeum hamiconexum" . It is in the monotypic family " Candidatus Altarchaeaceae" , monotypic order " Candidatus Altarchaeales" and monotypic phylum " Candidatus Altarchaeota" [ 4 ] .
