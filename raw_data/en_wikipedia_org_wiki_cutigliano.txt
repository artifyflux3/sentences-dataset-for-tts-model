10.750 Country Italy Region Tuscany Province Pistoia (PT) Comune Abetone Cutigliano Area • Total 43.8 km 2 (16.9 sq mi) Elevation 678 m (2,224 ft) Population (1 January 2007) [ 1 ] • Total 1,621 • Density 37/km 2 (96/sq mi) Demonym Cutiglianesi Time zone UTC+1 ( CET ) • Summer ( DST ) UTC+2 ( CEST ) Postal code 51024 Dialing code 0573 Website Official website Cutigliano was a comune (municipality) in the province of Pistoia in the Italian region Tuscany , located about 50 kilometres (31 mi) northwest of Florence and about 25 kilometres (16 mi) northwest of Pistoia . It has been a frazione of Abetone Cutigliano since 2017.
