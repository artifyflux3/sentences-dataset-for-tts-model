[ 3 ] Born in 1829, [ 4 ] he was educated at Portora Royal School and Trinity College, Dublin . He was ordained in 1852 and began his ecclesiastical career with a curacy at Kilglass . He was Rector of Lower Cumber [ 5 ] from  1867 [ 6 ] until his elevation to the Deanery .

[ 7 ] He died on 3 October 1911.
