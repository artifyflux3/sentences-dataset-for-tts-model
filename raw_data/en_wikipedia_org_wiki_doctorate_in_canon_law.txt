It can also be an honorary degree awarded by Anglican colleges. It may also be abbreviated ICD or dr.iur.can.

( Iuris Canonici Doctor ), ICDr , DCL , DCnl , DDC , or DCanL ( Doctor of Canon Law ). A doctor of both laws (i.e. canon and civil) is a JUD ( Juris Utriusque Doctor ) or UJD ( Utriusque Juris Doctor ).
