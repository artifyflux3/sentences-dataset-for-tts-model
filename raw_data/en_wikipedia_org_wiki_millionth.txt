It is the reciprocal of a million , and can be also written as 1 ⁄ 1,000,000 .

[ 1 ] Units using this fraction can be indicated using the prefix "micro-" from Greek , meaning "small".

[ 2 ] Numbers of this quantity are expressed in terms of μ (the Greek letter mu ).

[ 3 ] "Millionth" can also mean the ordinal number that comes after the nine hundred, ninety-nine thousand, nine hundred, ninety-ninth and before the million and first.
