If "Asian" is restricted  to refer to people from the continent of Asia , exclusive of Asian Russia and Turkey, who are not of predominantly European, African, or American ancestry, then the list is as follows: The first country listed is that of citizenship; the second, if any, is that of the Asian country of birth and/or ancestry, where different. Those of Asian ancestry but were born outside Asia are excluded.

The table lists astronauts whose origin or ancestry is in Central , East , South , Southeast , and West Asia .
