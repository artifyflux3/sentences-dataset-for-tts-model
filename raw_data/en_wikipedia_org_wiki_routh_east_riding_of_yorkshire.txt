It is situated is approximately 4 miles (6.4 km) north-east of Beverley , lying on the A1035 road .

All Saints Church at Routh According to the 2001 UK census , Routh parish had a population of 94.

[ 1 ] The parish church of All Saints is a Grade II* listed building .

[ 2 ] Governance [ edit ] Routh is represented locally by Tickton and Routh Parish Council a joint council with the adjacent parish of Tickton .

[ 3 ] while at county level is in the Beverley Rural ward of the East Riding of Yorkshire Council .

[ 4 ] At a parliamentary level it is part of the Beverley and Holderness constituency which is represented by Graham Stuart of the Conservative Party .
