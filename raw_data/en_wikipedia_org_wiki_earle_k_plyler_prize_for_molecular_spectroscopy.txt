Plyler Prize for Molecular Spectroscopy ) Prize awarded annually by the American Physical Society since 1977 The Earle K. Plyler Prize for Molecular Spectroscopy and Dynamics is a prize that has been awarded annually by the American Physical Society since 1977.

[ 1 ] The recipient is chosen for " notable contributions to the field of molecular spectroscopy and dynamics ".

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] [ 3 ] [ 4 ] The prize is named after Earle K. Plyler , who was a leading experimenter in the field of infrared spectroscopy ; as of 2024 it is valued at $10,000.  The prize is currently sponsored by the AIP Journal of Chemical Physics .
