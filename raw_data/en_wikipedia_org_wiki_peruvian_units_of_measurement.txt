[ 1 ] [ 2 ] System before metric system [ edit ] Units from Spanish Castillian were used.

[ 1 ] Length [ edit ] One vara was equal to 0.83598 m.

[ 1 ] One pie was equal to 0.27866 m/3 varas.

[ 2 ] Mass [ edit ] Several units were used to measure mass.  One libra was equal to 0.46009 kg.

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] Some other units are given below: 1 arroba = 25 libra 1 quintal = 100 libra 1 fanega = 140 libra.

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] [ 3 ] Area [ edit ] One topo was equal to 2706 m 2 .

[ 1 ] One fanegada was equal to 6459.6 m 2 .
