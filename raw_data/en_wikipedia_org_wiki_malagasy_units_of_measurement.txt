[ 1 ] System before metric system [ edit ] Several units were used.

Length [ edit ] One rahf was equal to 46.46 in (1.18 m) (the value was double in the northern part of Madagascar).

[ 2 ] Mass [ edit ] For gold and silver [ edit ] Units included: 1 vari = 3 nanki 1 sompi = 2 vari = 60 grain.

[ 2 ] Capacity [ edit ] One bambou was equal to 0.0568 bushel .
