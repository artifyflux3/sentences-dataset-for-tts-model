At each vertex of the tiling there is one triangle and one heptagon , alternating between two squares . The tiling has Schläfli symbol rr{7, 3}.  It can be seen as constructed as a rectified triheptagonal tiling , r{7,3}, as well as an expanded heptagonal tiling or expanded order-7 triangular tiling .

Dual tiling [ edit ] The dual tiling is called a deltoidal triheptagonal tiling , and consists of congruent kites . It is formed by overlaying an order-3 heptagonal tiling and an order-7 triangular tiling .

Related polyhedra and tilings [ edit ] From a Wythoff construction there are eight hyperbolic uniform tilings that can be based from the regular heptagonal tiling.

Drawing the tiles colored as red on the original faces, yellow at the original vertices, and blue along the original edges, there are 8 forms.
