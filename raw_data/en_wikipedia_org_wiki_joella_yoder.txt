She is an expert in the work of Christiaan Huygens .

Life [ edit ] Yoder gained her Ph.D. in 1985 from the University of Wisconsin . In 1986 she embarked on the project of cataloguing the manuscripts and letters of Huygens, many of them collected at the University of Leiden . It would take her over a quarter of a century to complete this cataloguing project.

[ 1 ] Yoder was married to Bill Yoder, in memory of whom she donated to Village Theatre .
