_**A Celebration of Endings**_ is the eighth[[note 1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/A_Celebration_of_Endings#cite_note-2) studio album by Scottish [alternative rock](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alternative_rock "Alternative rock") band [Biffy Clyro](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Biffy_Clyro "Biffy Clyro"). It was produced by [Rich Costey](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rich_Costey "Rich Costey") and was released on 14 August 2020. It debuted at number one on the [UK Albums Chart](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/UK_Albums_Chart "UK Albums Chart"), making it the band's third consecutive studio album to top the chart.

The band confirmed they were working on new material in an _[NME](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/NME "NME")_ interview in July 2019,[[2]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/A_Celebration_of_Endings#cite_note-3) having released their soundtrack album _[Balance, Not Symmetry](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Balance,\_Not\_Symmetry\_(album) "Balance, Not Symmetry (album)")_ a few months earlier. Another _NME_ interview in October 2019 confirmed the release of the album, with the working title _Opus 8_, for the 'first half of next year'.[[3]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/A_Celebration_of_Endings#cite_note-4)

The first single, "Instant History", was released on 20 February 2020, and first played on [Annie Mac](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Annie_Mac "Annie Mac")'s [BBC Radio 1](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/BBC_Radio_1 "BBC Radio 1") show.

The album was initially planned for release of 15 May 2020, but was pushed back to 14 August due to difficulties surrounding the [COVID-19 pandemic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/COVID-19_pandemic "COVID-19 pandemic").[[4]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/A_Celebration_of_Endings#cite_note-5)

The album received critical acclaim upon release. On review aggregate website [Metacritic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Metacritic "Metacritic"), the album has an average score of 84 out of 100, indicating "universal acclaim," based on 16 reviews.[[5]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/A_Celebration_of_Endings#cite_note-mc-6)_[Kerrang!](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kerrang! "Kerrang!")_ gave the album a perfect score, stating that "Biffy Clyro have delivered an album of restless invention, substance and style that arrives like a spray of water on the arid expanse of this saddest of summers."[[11]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/A_Celebration_of_Endings#cite_note-kerrang-12)_[The Independent](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The\_Independent "The Independent")_ also gave the album a positive review, writing that the album "soothes, shakes and surprises at every turn".[[10]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/A_Celebration_of_Endings#cite_note-independent-11)
