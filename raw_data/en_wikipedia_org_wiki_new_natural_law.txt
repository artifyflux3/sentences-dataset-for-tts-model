[ 1 ] The approach began in the 1960s with the work of Germain Grisez and has since been developed by John Finnis , Joseph Boyle , Olaf Tollefsen , Christopher Tollefsen and others.

[ 2 ] [ 3 ] [ 4 ] [ 5 ] [ 6 ] [ 7 ] [ 8 ] [ 9 ] NNL theory typically relies on a concept of 'basic goods'.
