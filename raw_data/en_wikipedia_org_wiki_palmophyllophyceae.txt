Synonyms Prasinophyceae Christensen 1962 The Palmophyllophyceae are a class of green algae consisting of the Palmophyllales and Prasinococcales .

[ 1 ] It belongs to Prasinodermophyta , a group that – according to Li et al.'s phylogenetic analyses – is basal to the rest of the Viridiplantae (that is, the group of all green algae, from which land plants evolved, also known as "green plants").
