Country Canada Based in Toronto Language English Website https://www.reorientmag.com Reorient was a Canadian publication focusing on the art and culture of Iran and the surrounding region [ 1 ] . It was founded in 2012 by Joobin Bekhrad who also edited and creatively directed the magazine.

[ 2 ] [ 3 ] [ 4 ] The areas covered primarily included visual art, music, literature, and film. The magazine ran until January 2018.

[ 5 ] Profile [ edit ] Reorient has been mentioned and cited in a number of publications and websites from around the world, including Newsweek , [ 6 ] , The Guardian [ 7 ] , Campaign Magazine , [ 8 ] The Globe and Mail, Art Dubai , [ 9 ] Canvas , [ 10 ] Harper’s Bazaar Art Arabia , [ 11 ] Oasis , [ 12 ] Art & Antiques , [ 13 ] the Cairo Review of Global Affairs , [ 14 ] and Ryerson Folio .
