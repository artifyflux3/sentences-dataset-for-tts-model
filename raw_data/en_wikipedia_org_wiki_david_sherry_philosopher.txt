Sherry (B.A., University of Montana , 1974; Ph.D., Claremont Graduate School , 1982) is a philosopher and professor at Northern Arizona University in Flagstaff, Arizona. He teaches History of Philosophy , History of Logic , as well as Philosophy of Mathematics .  He has published on Logic, Philosophy of Mathematics and Philosophy of Science.
