**World Scientific Publishing** is an [academic publisher](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Academic_publisher "Academic publisher") of scientific, technical, and medical books and [journals](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Academic_journal "Academic journal") headquartered in [Singapore](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Singapore "Singapore"). The company was founded in 1981. It publishes about 600 books annually, with more than 170 journals in various fields. In 1995, World Scientific co-founded the London-based [Imperial College Press](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Imperial_College_Press "Imperial College Press") together with the [Imperial College of Science, Technology and Medicine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Imperial_College_of_Science,_Technology_and_Medicine "Imperial College of Science, Technology and Medicine").

Company structure
-----------------

[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=World_Scientific&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Company structure")]

The company head office is in [Singapore](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Singapore "Singapore"). The Chairman and Editor-in-Chief is Dr [Phua Kok Khoo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phua_Kok_Khoo "Phua Kok Khoo"), while the Managing Director is [Doreen Liu](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Doreen_Liu "Doreen Liu"). The company was co-founded by them in 1981.[[4]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/World_Scientific_Publishing_Company#cite_note-4)

### Imperial College Press

[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=World_Scientific&action=edit&section=2 "Edit section: Imperial College Press")]

In 1995 the company co-founded [Imperial College Press](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Imperial_College_Press "Imperial College Press"), specializing in [engineering](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Engineering "Engineering"), [medicine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Medicine "Medicine") and [information technology](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Information_technology "Information technology"), with [Imperial College London](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Imperial_College_London "Imperial College London"). In 2006, World Scientific assumed full ownership of Imperial College Press, under a license granted by the university. Finally, in August 2016, ICP was fully incorporated into World Scientific under the new imprint, _World Scientific Europe_.
