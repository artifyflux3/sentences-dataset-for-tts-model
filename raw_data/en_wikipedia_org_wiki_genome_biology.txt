1076659408 Links Journal homepage Online archive Genome Biology is a peer-reviewed open access scientific journal covering research in genomics . It was established in 2000 and is published by BioMed Central . The chief editor is currently Andrew Cosgrove ( BioMed Central , New York).

Abstracting and indexing [ edit ] The journal is abstracted and indexed in: Biological Abstracts [ 1 ] BIOSIS Previews [ 2 ] CAB Abstracts [ 3 ] Current Contents /Life Sciences [ 2 ] Embase [ 4 ] EMBiology Index Medicus / MEDLINE / PubMed [ 5 ] Science Citation Index [ 2 ] Scopus [ 6 ] The Zoological Record [ 2 ] According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2024 impact factor of 9.1.
