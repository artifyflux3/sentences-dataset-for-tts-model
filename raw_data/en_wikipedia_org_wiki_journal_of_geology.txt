Indexing CODEN ( alt · alt2 ) · JSTOR ( alt ) · LCCN ( alt ) MIAR · NLM ( alt ) · Scopus · W&L ISSN 0022-1376 Links Journal homepage The Journal of Geology publishes research on geology , geophysics , geochemistry, sedimentology, geomorphology, petrology , plate tectonics , volcanology , structural geology , mineralogy , and planetary sciences. Its content ranges from planetary evolution to computer modeling of fossil development, making it relevant to geologists as well as other researchers working in the Earth or planetary sciences.

It was established in 1893 by Thomas Chrowder Chamberlin .
