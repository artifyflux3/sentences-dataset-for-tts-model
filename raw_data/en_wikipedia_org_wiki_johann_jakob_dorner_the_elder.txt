He was at first a pupil of Rösch at Freiburg and of Ignaz Bauer at Augsburg . He afterwards visited Italy, the Netherlands, and Paris. He was a professor and director of the Gallery at Munich in 1770, and died in that city in 1813. In the Darmstadt Gallery is a picture of Two Soldiers and a Maiden by him; and in the Pinakothek at Munich, a Linen Draper , which is a portrait of his wife, dated 1775.

The amateur Amalia von Schattenhofer was a pupil of Dorner.

[ 1 ] His son also became a painter.
