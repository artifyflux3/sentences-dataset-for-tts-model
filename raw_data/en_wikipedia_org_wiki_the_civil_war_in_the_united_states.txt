( June 2023 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) The Civil War in the United States is a collection of articles on the American Civil War by Karl Marx and Friedrich Engels , written between 1861 and 1862 for the New-York Tribune and Die Presse of Vienna , and correspondence between Marx and Engels between 1860 and 1866. It was published as a book in 1937, edited and with an introduction by Richard Enmale .

In 2016 International Publishers produced a completely revised second edition of The Civil War in the United States , edited and with an introduction by Andrew Zimmerman.

The articles promote the Union side of the war, arguing that the conflict was fundamentally about slavery .
