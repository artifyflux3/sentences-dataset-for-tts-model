Era names originated in 140 BCE in China, during the reign of the Emperor Wu of Han .

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] Since the middle of the 6th century CE, independent Vietnamese dynasties started to proclaim their own era names.

[ 2 ] [ 3 ] [ 4 ] During periods of direct Chinese rule , Chinese era names would gain official use in Vietnam, as was the case for other parts of China .

List of Vietnamese era names [ edit ] For a list of era names used in Vietnam during Chinese rule, see List of Chinese era names .

The following is a list of era names adopted by independent Vietnamese monarchs .

[ 5 ] Era names used in Vietnam during the four periods of direct Chinese rule are not included.
