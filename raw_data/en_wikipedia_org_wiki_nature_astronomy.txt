1165479718 Links Journal homepage Online access Online archive Nature Astronomy is a peer reviewed scientific journal published by Nature Portfolio .

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] It was first published in January 2017 (volume 1, issue 1), although the first content appeared online in December 2016. The editor-in-chief is Paul Woods, who is a full-time professional editor employed by the journal.

[ 3 ] Dr. Woods took over the leadership of the journal in April 2024 from May Chiao, [ 4 ] who launched the journal. The founding editors of this journal, in addition to May Chiao, were Paul Woods, Luca Maltagliati and Marios Karouzos.

[ 3 ] Abstracting and indexing [ edit ] The journal is abstracted and indexed in: Astrophysics Data System (ADS) [ 5 ] Science Citation Index Expanded [ 6 ] Scopus [ 7 ] According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2024 impact factor of 13.9, ranking it 5th out of 69 journals in the category "Astronomy & Astrophysics", behind four Review journals. Nature Astronomy is the leading primary research journal in astronomy & astrophysics, ranked by Journal Impact Factor .
