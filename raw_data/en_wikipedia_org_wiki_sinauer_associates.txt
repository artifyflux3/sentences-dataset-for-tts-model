is a publisher of college-level textbooks.

[ 1 ] It was started in 1969 by Andrew D. Sinauer and has since grown to be an internationally recognized publisher of seminal scientific works.

[ 2 ] [ 3 ] In 2017, the publisher continued as an imprint of Oxford University Press .
