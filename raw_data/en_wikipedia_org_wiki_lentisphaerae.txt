[ 2 ] [ 3 ] It includes two monotypic orders Lentisphaerales and Victivallales . Phylum members can be aerobic or anaerobic and fall under two distinct phenotypes. These phenotypes live within bodies of sea water and were particularly hard to isolate in a pure culture.

[ 4 ] One phenotype, L. marina , consists of terrestrial gut microbiota from mammals and birds. It was found in the Sea of Japan .

[ 4 ] The other phenotype ( L. araneosa ) includes marine microorganisms : sequences from fish and coral microbiomes and marine sediment .
