Abolfadl Harawi ( Persian : ابو الفضل هروى ) (also called Abu al-Fadl al-Harawi , Abufadlus Harwanensis ) [ 1 ] was a 10th-century astronomer who, along with al-Khujandi , [ 1 ] studied under the patronage of the Buyid dynasty in Rey , Persia .

[ 2 ] The nisba "Harawi" suggests that he was originally from Herat , in ancient times also known as Haraiva.
