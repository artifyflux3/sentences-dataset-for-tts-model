n − − i λ λ / κ κ ) ( n + i λ λ κ κ ) {\displaystyle {\frac {\lambda ^{2}e^{imn}}{\left(n-i\lambda /\kappa \right)\left(n+i\lambda \kappa \right)}}} In probability theory and directional statistics , a wrapped asymmetric Laplace distribution is a wrapped probability distribution that results from the "wrapping" of the asymmetric Laplace distribution around the unit circle . For the symmetric case (asymmetry parameter κ = 1), the distribution becomes a wrapped Laplace distribution. The distribution of the ratio of two circular variates ( Z ) from two different wrapped exponential distributions will have a wrapped asymmetric Laplace distribution. These distributions find application in stochastic modelling of financial data.
