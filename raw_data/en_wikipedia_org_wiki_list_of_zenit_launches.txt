All launches were conducted from either Site 45 of the Baikonur Cosmodrome or from Sea Launch 's Ocean Odyssey offshore launch platform.

Launch statistics [ edit ] Rockets from the Zenit family have accumulated 84 launches since 1985, 71 of which were successful, yielding a 84.5% success rate.
