The first cultured representative came from Utsteinen in the Sør Rondane Mountains ( East Antarctica ) and is a chemoheterotrophic , gram-negative , aerobic and oligotrophic bacterium. It has a limited number of carbon sources, optimized metabolism for survival in low nutrient habitats. Extreme resistance against antibiotics and toxic compounds was identified. Phylogenetically, it would be related to the phylum Armatimonadota according to molecular analysis.

[ 2 ] Member species [ edit ] Abditibacterium utsteinense , a gram-negative chemoheterotroph found in antarctic soil.
