896750387 Links Journal homepage Online access Online archive The Journal of Advanced Nursing (also known as JAN ) is a monthly peer-reviewed medical journal covering all aspects of nursing . It is published by John Wiley & Sons . JAN is a world-leading international peer-reviewed journal. JAN targets readers who are committed to advancing practice and professional development on the basis of new knowledge and evidence. .

History [ edit ] The journal was established in 1976 with James P. Smith as founding editor-in-chief . Subsequent editors-in-chief were Jane Robinson, Alison J. Tierney and Roger Watson . The current editor-in-chief is Debra Jackson ( University of Sydney ).

Abstracting and indexing [ edit ] The journal is abstracted and indexed in: CINAHL [ 1 ] Current Contents /Social & Behavioral Sciences [ 2 ] EBSCO databases Index Medicus / MEDLINE / PubMed [ 3 ] ProQuest databases PsycINFO [ 4 ] Science Citation Index Expanded [ 2 ] Scopus [ 5 ] Social Sciences Citation Index [ 2 ] According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2019 impact factor of 2.376.
