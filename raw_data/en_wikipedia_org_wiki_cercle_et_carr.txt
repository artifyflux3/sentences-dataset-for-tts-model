[ 1 ] The group published a journal with the same name. In 1930 they organised an exhibition in Paris showing 130 abstract works by various artists.  "Notre programme fût "Construction", fusse figuratif ou non-figuratif" ( Our formula was "Construccion" whether it was figurative or non-figurative).

[ 2 ] When the Abstraction-Création was founded in 1931 it absorbed the group.

[ 3 ] From 1936 Joaquín Torres García continued publication of the journal from Montevideo in Spanish and French with the title Círculo y Cuadrado "La seconde epoque de "Cercle et Carre" (Circle and Square, the second period) with the same logo. From abroad artists sent letters and articles to be published: Jean Hélion , Piet Mondrian , Umberto Boccioni , etc.
