The launch vehicles used the Douglas -built Thor first stage and the Lockheed -built Agena second stages. They are thus cousins of the more-famous Thor-Delta, which founded the Delta launch vehicle family. The first attempted launch of a Thor-Agena was on 28 February 1959. The first successful launch was on 13 April 1959, launching Discoverer 2 . It was the first two-stage rocket to place a satellite into orbit.
