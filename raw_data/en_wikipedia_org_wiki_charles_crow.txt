Charles Crow was Bishop of Cloyne [ 1 ] from 1702 until his death on 26 June 1726.

[ 2 ] Crow was born in Jurby [ 3 ] and educated at Trinity College, Dublin .

[ 4 ] He was a teacher then head master at St. Patrick's School in Dublin . He was afterwards Chaplain to Laurence, Earl of Rochester, Lord Lieutenant of Ireland . He was also Rector of Coolbanagher until his episcopal appointment.
