Find sources: "Meta-system" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( April 2025 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) A system about other systems A metasystem or meta-system is a "system about other systems", [ 1 ] such as describing, generalizing, modelling, or analyzing the other system(s). It links the concepts of a system and meta .
