3 Shenzhou 14 Chen Dong Liu Yang Cai Xuzhe 5 June 2022 [ 3 ] 4 December 2022 Delivered three taikonauts to the Tiangong space station; third crewed mission to Tiangong.

4 Shenzhou 15 Fei Junlong Deng Qingming Zhang Lu 29 November 2022 [ 4 ] 3 June 2023 Delivered three taikonauts to the Tiangong space station; fourth crewed mission to Tiangong.
