[ 1 ] [ 2 ] Unlike baryonic molecules , which form the nuclei of all elements in nature save hydrogen-1 , a mesonic molecule has yet to be definitively observed.

[ 3 ] The X(3872) discovered in 2003 and the Z(4430) discovered in 2007 by the Belle experiment are the best candidates for such an observation.
