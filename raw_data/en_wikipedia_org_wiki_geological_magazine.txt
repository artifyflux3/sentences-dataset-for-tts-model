Indexing CODEN ( alt · alt2 ) · JSTOR ( alt ) · LCCN ( alt ) MIAR · NLM ( alt ) · Scopus · W&L ISSN 0016-7568 Links Journal homepage Cambridge University Press page The Geological Magazine is a peer-reviewed scientific journal established in 1864, covering the earth sciences . It publishes original scientific research papers on geological topics. The journal is published bimonthly by Cambridge University Press .
