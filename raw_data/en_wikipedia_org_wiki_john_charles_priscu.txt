Priscu ( Romanian : Prișcu ; born 20 September 1952, Las Vegas, Nevada ), is a Romanian-American scientist who is the current Professor of Ecology in the Department of Land Resources and Environmental Sciences at Montana State University . He is a principal investigator in the McMurdo Dry Valleys Long Term Ecological Research (LTER) project.

[ 1 ] Filmography/TV appearances [ edit ] He appeared on documentary program Horizon in the episode "The Lost World of Lake Vostok".
