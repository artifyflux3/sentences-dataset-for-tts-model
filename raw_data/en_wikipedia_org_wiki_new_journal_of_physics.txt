Indexing CODEN ( alt · alt2 ) · JSTOR ( alt ) · LCCN ( alt ) MIAR · NLM ( alt ) · Scopus · W&L CODEN NJOPFM ISSN 1367-2630 LCCN 1193529983 Links Journal homepage New Journal of Physics is an online-only, open-access , peer-reviewed scientific journal covering research in all aspects of physics , as well as interdisciplinary topics where physics forms the central theme. The journal was established in 1998 and is a joint publication of the Institute of Physics and the Deutsche Physikalische Gesellschaft . It is published by IOP Publishing . The editor-in-chief is Andreas Buchleitner ( Albert Ludwigs University ).

New Journal of Physics is part of the SCOAP 3 initiative.

[ 2 ] In April 2023, on occasion of the World Quantum Day , IOP Publishing has launched a special collection of its most important articles published in the field of quantum research. The articles will be extracted from Materials for Quantum Technology , Quantum Science and Technology , New Journal of Physics and Reports on Progress in Physics .

[ 3 ] Abstracting and indexing [ edit ] The journal is abstracted and indexed in: Astrophysics Data System [ 4 ] Chemical Abstracts Service [ 5 ] Current Contents /Physical, Chemical & Earth Sciences [ 6 ] Directory of Open Access Journals [ 7 ] EBSCO databases [ 7 ] Ei Compendex [ 8 ] INIS Atomindex [ 9 ] Inspec [ 10 ] Science Citation Index Expanded [ 6 ] Scopus [ 11 ] zbMATH Open [ 7 ] According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2023 impact factor of 2.8.
