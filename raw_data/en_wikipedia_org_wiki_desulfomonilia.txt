D. palmitatoxidans" D. tiedjei Desulfomonile is a Gram negative , strictly anaerobic , and non-motile bacterial genus from the phylum Desulfobacterota .

[ 1 ] [ 3 ] [ 4 ] Desulfomonile bacteria can reduce sulfur oxyanions to H 2 S .

[ 4 ] It is the only known member of the family Desulfomonilaceae, order Desulfomonilales, and class Desulfomonilia.
