[ 1 ] He was born in West Clandon ; educated at Westminster School and Trinity College, Cambridge ; and ordained in 1766.

[ 2 ] He held livings at Chesham , Wilden , Motcombe and West Lavington .

[ 2 ] He was Dean of Derry from 1781 until 1783.
