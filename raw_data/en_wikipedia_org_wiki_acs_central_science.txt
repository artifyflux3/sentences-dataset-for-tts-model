890595211 Links Journal homepage Online access Online archive ACS Central Science is a monthly peer-reviewed open access scientific journal covering chemistry and related fields. Its title refers to the phrase " central science ", which has long been used to describe the role played by chemistry in connecting the physical and life sciences .

Established in 2015, it is the first fully open-access journal published by ACS Publications , the American Chemical Society 's publishing arm. The editor-in-chief is Carolyn R. Bertozzi ( Stanford University ).

[ 1 ] According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2023 impact factor of 12.7.
