(1853) Genera Sarcosagium Thelocarpon Thelocarpaceae is the sole family of lichen -forming fungi in the order Thelocarpales . The family contains two genera, Sarcosagium and Thelocarpon .

[ 1 ] The family was circumscribed by lichenologist Hugo Zukal in 1893, [ 2 ] while the order was proposed by Robert Lücking and H. Thorsten Lumbsch in 2016.
