987587574 Links Journal homepage The Cornell Policy Review is an online academic journal published by the Cornell Institute for Public Affairs . It is verified by the Network of Schools of Public Policy, Affairs, and Administration and edited and run by the program's students.

[ 1 ] It was originally published biannually, but switched to a rolling online publication in the 2015–16 academic year. Formerly known as The Current , the journal publishes articles, commentaries, and interviews relating to public policy .
