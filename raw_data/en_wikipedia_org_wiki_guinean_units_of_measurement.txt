[ 1 ] Units used before the metric system [ edit ] These units were mainly Portugal, England and local.

[ 1 ] Length [ edit ] Several units were used to measure length.  Some of units are provided below: [ 1 ] [ 2 ] 1 pik = 0.578 m 1 jacktan = 3.658 m.

Mass [ edit ] A number of units were used to measure mass.  One benda was equal to 0.0642 kg.

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] One kantar was equal to 0.977 kg.

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] One gammell was 1/5 kantar.

[ 1 ] [ 2 ] Some other units are given below: [ 1 ] [ 2 ] 1 akey = 1 ⁄ 48 benda 1 mediatabla = 1 ⁄ 32 benda 1 aguirage = 1 ⁄ 16 benda 1 quinto = 3 ⁄ 32 benda 1 piso = 1 uzan = 1 ⁄ 8 benda 1 seron = 3 ⁄ 16 benda 1 eggaba = 1 ⁄ 3 benda [ 3 ] 1 benda (offa) = 1 ⁄ 2 benda p One rotl was equal to 0.9538 lb avoirdupois.
