Men's only sports include baseball and football. Women's only sports include field hockey, golf, softball, and volleyball.

History [ edit ] Prior to 2023, the college's teams were known as the "Dutchmen" and "Dutchwomen." [ 2 ] The nickname was changed to "Garnet Chargers" as part of a branding update.

[ 2 ] Garnet has been the school's official color for 150 years, and the name "chargers" is a reference to "Schenectady’s legacy as a leader in electrical technologies." [ 2 ] Affiliation [ edit ] The Garnet Chargers compete in the NCAA Division III and are members of the Liberty League for all sports except ice hockey, which competes in NCAA Division I as a member of ECAC Hockey .
