Find sources: "Abu ul-Ala Shirazi" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( February 2025 ) Abu ul-Ala Shirazi Died 1001 Nationality Persia Known for treatment of malaria Scientific career Fields medicine Abu ul-Ala Shirazi (died 1001) lived around the 10th century at the court of the Buyid emir 'Adud al-Dawla . He found that arsenic could cure malaria .
