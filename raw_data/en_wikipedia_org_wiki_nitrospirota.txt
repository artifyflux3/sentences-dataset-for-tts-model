Phylum of bacteria Nitrospirota Scientific classification Domain: Bacteria Kingdom: Pseudomonadati Phylum: Nitrospirota Garrity & Holt 2021 [ 1 ] Classes & Orders Nitrospiria Thermodesulfovibrionia Synonyms "Nitrospirae" Garrity and Holt 2001 "Nitrospiraeota" Oren et al. 2015 "Nitrospirota" Whitman et al. 2018 The Nitrospirota are a phylum of bacteria . They include multiple genera such as Nitrospira , the largest.

History of knowledge [ edit ] The first member of this phylum, Nitrospira marina , was discovered in 1985.

[ 2 ] The second member, Nitrospira moscoviensis , was discovered in 1995.

[ 3 ] [ 4 ] Nitrospira inopinata was discovered in 2015 [ 5 ] [ 6 ] and cultivated in 2017.

[ 7 ] Metabolism [ edit ] Nitrospirota contains nitrifying taxa which oxidize nitrite to nitrate (nitrite-oxidizing bacteria, NOB [ 8 ] ) and commamox bacteria: Nitrospira inopinata .

[ 5 ] [ 6 ] Dissulfurispira thermophila ( Thermodesulfovibrionia ) grows by disproportionation of thiosulfate or elemental sulfur.
