_**Space**_ is an album by American jazz group the [Modern Jazz Quartet](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Modern_Jazz_Quartet "Modern Jazz Quartet") featuring performances recorded in 1969 and released on the [Apple](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apple_Records "Apple Records") label.[[5]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_(Modern_Jazz_Quartet_album)#cite_note-Modern_Jazz_Quartet_discography-5)

Reception
---------

[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Space_(Modern_Jazz_Quartet_album)&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Reception")]

The [Allmusic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Allmusic "Allmusic") review by [Scott Yanow](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scott_Yanow "Scott Yanow") stated: "Overall this is an average but worthy outing from a group whose excellence could always be taken for granted".[[2]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_(Modern_Jazz_Quartet_album)#cite_note-Allmusic-2)
