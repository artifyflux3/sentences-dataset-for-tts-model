The range is located in the northern hemisphere of Titan, between 56–7° north and 61–3° west.

[ 1 ] The Misty Montes are named after the Misty Mountains , a range of mountains in J. R. R. Tolkien 's fictional world of Middle Earth which appears most prominently in The Hobbit .

[ 1 ] The name follows a convention that Titanean mountains are named after mountains in Tolkien's work.

[ 2 ] It was formally announced on November 13, 2012.
