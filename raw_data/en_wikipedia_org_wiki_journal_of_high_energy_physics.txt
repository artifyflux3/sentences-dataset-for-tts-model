637672365 Links Journal homepage Online archive Access to content before 2010 The Journal of High Energy Physics is a monthly peer-reviewed open access scientific journal covering the field of high energy physics . It is published by Springer Science+Business Media on behalf of the International School for Advanced Studies . The journal is part of the SCOAP 3 initiative.

[ 1 ] According to the Journal Citation Reports , the journal has a 2020 impact factor of 5.810.
