Included in the list below are all Presidents of Cornell University from Andrew Dickson White , the university's co-founder and first president from 1865 to 1888, through the 15th and current president, Michael Kotlikoff , who was appointed president of the university in 2025. Since the university's founding in 1865, there have been 15 Presidents of Cornell University, excluding four interregnum presidents who served during university presidential transitions.

New York's only land-grant university , Cornell University was founded in 1865 by Ezra Cornell and Andrew Dickson White. Its main campus is in Ithaca, New York. Its medical school , Weill Cornell Medicine , is located in Manhattan , New York City, and it also maintains a facility in the Education City section Qatar . In 1954, Cornell joined the newly formed Ivy League, and the league's only land-grant institution.
