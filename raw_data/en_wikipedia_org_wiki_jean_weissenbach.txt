Find sources: "Jean Weissenbach" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR ( April 2010 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this message ) Jean Weissenbach Born 13 February 1946 ( 1946-02-13 ) (age 79) Strasbourg , France Nationality French Known for Human genome Awards Gairdner Award (2002) Scientific career Fields Genetics Institutions Genoscope Jean Weissenbach (born 13 February 1946) is a French biologist. He is the current director of the Genoscope . He is one of the pioneers of sequencing and genome analysis.
