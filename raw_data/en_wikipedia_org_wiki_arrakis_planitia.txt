It is located in Titan's southern hemisphere , between 74 and 80° south and 113–134° east, [ 1 ] within the Mezzoramia region.

[ 2 ] Arrakis Planitia is named after Arrakis , a fictional desert planet that is featured prominently in Frank Herbert 's Dune novels.

[ 1 ] The name follows a convention that Titanean plains are named after planets in Herbert's work.
