_**Space**_ is a 1990 [ambient house](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ambient_house "Ambient house")[concept album](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Concept_album "Concept album") by [Jimmy Cauty](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jimmy_Cauty "Jimmy Cauty") under the alias **Space**. Originally intended to be [The Orb](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Orb "The Orb")'s debut album, _Space_ was refactored for release as a solo album following Cauty's departure from that group. _Space_ was independently released on [KLF Communications](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/KLF_Communications "KLF Communications"), the record label formed to distribute the work of Cauty's other project, [The KLF](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_KLF "The KLF").

Origins
-------

[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Space_(Jimmy_Cauty_album)&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Origins")]

_Space_ was originally a collaboration between [Alex Paterson](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alex_Paterson "Alex Paterson") and [Jimmy Cauty](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jimmy_Cauty "Jimmy Cauty"), the original line-up of The Orb. Cauty's record label [KLF Communications](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/KLF_Communications "KLF Communications") planned to release it as The Orb's debut album,[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_(Jimmy_Cauty_album)#cite_note-1)[[2]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_(Jimmy_Cauty_album)#cite_note-2) but when Cauty left The Orb in April 1990[[3]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_(Jimmy_Cauty_album)#cite_note-3) to concentrate on producing music as [The KLF](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_KLF "The KLF") with [Bill Drummond](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bill_Drummond "Bill Drummond"), he took the recordings with him. Reworked to remove Paterson's contributions, the album was released by KLF Communications in July 1990 as an eponymous album,[[4]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_(Jimmy_Cauty_album)#cite_note-discog-4) with Cauty alone receiving credit.[[5]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_(Jimmy_Cauty_album)#cite_note-5)

[Youth](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Youth_(musician) "Youth (musician)"), a friend and colleague of both men, recalled: "Jimmy and Alex were making an album called Space when they had a big argument. Jimmy stormed off, took all of Alex’s bits off the record and released it under his own name. Alex was mortified. I told him not to worry – we’d make a record that was even better".[[6]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_(Jimmy_Cauty_album)#cite_note-made-clouds-6)

According to Cauty, _Space_ "was a jam, all done on [Oberheim](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oberheim "Oberheim")[keyboards](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Synthesizer "Synthesizer"). Loads of [samples](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sampling_(music) "Sampling (music)")... were chucked in there as well. I started on Monday morning and by Friday it was all done".[[7]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_(Jimmy_Cauty_album)#cite_note-enig-7) He said: "Nothing was prepared, I started on a Monday morning and just jammed the whole thing."[[8]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_(Jimmy_Cauty_album)#cite_note-Prendergast-8)

Composition
-----------

[[edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Space_(Jimmy_Cauty_album)&action=edit&section=2 "Edit section: Composition")]

_Space_ takes the listener on a voyage through the [Solar System](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Solar_System "Solar System") from [Mercury](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mercury_(planet) "Mercury (planet)") outwards, with vast distances of empty space between worlds represented by periods of [minimalist](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minimalism "Minimalism") ambience and near-silence. [Synthesisers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Synthesisers "Synthesisers"), excerpts from classical compositions and nursery rhymes (including [Twinkle Twinkle Little Star](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Twinkle_Twinkle_Little_Star "Twinkle Twinkle Little Star")), [sinusoidal loops](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sine_wave "Sine wave"), and communications from space [flight controllers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Flight_controller "Flight controller") are among the sounds used to describe the voyage. This musical interpretation of a physical journey is also a characteristic of the early [ambient house](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ambient_house "Ambient house") recordings _[The Orb's Adventures Beyond the Ultraworld](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The\_Orb%27s\_Adventures\_Beyond\_the\_Ultraworld "The Orb's Adventures Beyond the Ultraworld")_ and The KLF's _[Chill Out](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chill\_Out\_(KLF\_album) "Chill Out (KLF album)")_.

Cauty has called _Space_ "a record for 14-year-old space cadets to go and take [acid](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/LSD "LSD") [to] for the first time".[[7]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_(Jimmy_Cauty_album)#cite_note-enig-7)

The album is "more important for who's on it than what's in it", he concluded.[[9]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Space_(Jimmy_Cauty_album)#cite_note-AM-9)
