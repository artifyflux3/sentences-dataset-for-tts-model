Austria) measures were still used during time the state, Czechoslovakia, was established.) [ 1 ] [ 2 ] Old local measures during the first half of the 20th century [ edit ] A number of units were used to measure quantities like length and capacity.

Length [ edit ] A number of units were used in Czechoslovakia to measure length.  One latro was equal to 1.917 m.

[ 1 ] Capacity [ edit ] In Czechoslovakia , several units were used to measure capacity.  One korec (or one strych) was equal to 93.592 L.
