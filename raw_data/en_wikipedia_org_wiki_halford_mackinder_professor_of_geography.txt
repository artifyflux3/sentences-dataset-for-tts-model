[ 1 ] In February 2025 the chair title was renamed to 1971 Professor of Geography.

[ 1 ] Originally, the post was named after Sir Halford Mackinder , the first Reader in the Department of Geography in Oxford, and an important figure in the early years of Geography as an academic subject in the United Kingdom.
