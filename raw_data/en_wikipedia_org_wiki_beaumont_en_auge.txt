Beaumont-en-Auge ( French pronunciation: [bomɔ̃ ɑ̃.n‿oʒ] ⓘ , literally Beaumont in Auge ) is a commune in the Calvados department in the Normandy region in northwestern France . The town hosts one of the last kaleidoscope manufacturers in France.
