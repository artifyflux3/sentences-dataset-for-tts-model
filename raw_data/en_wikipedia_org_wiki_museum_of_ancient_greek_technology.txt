[ 1 ] [ 2 ] It has four permanent annexes in Athens , [ 3 ] Ancient Olympia , [ 4 ] Katakolo [ 5 ] and Heraklion [ 6 ] and it is famous for its travelling exhibitions, domestically [ 7 ] [ 8 ] [ 9 ] and abroad.

[ 10 ] [ 11 ] [ 12 ] [ 13 ] In 2019 it has been nominated as “European Museum of the Year” by the European Museum Forum . It is also a member of International Council of Museums (ICOM).
