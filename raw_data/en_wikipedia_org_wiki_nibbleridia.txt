2022 Genera Ubysseya Nibbleromonas Nibbleridia is a phylum of predatory microbial eukaryotes in the supergroup Provora . Known as nibblerids , the group contains the class Nibbleridea , order Nibbleridida , family Nibbleridae , and genera Ubysseya and Nibbleromonas .
